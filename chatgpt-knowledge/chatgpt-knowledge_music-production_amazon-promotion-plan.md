## Answer

This pitch for Tim White's "Never Say A Word" effectively highlights its potential as a hit through various strategic promotions. It emphasizes the track’s appeal, notable collaborations, and digital marketing strategies, positioning it well for summer success. Here are a few tips to enhance the pitch:

1. **Clear Objective**: Start with a clear statement of what you're seeking from Amazon – support for streaming promotions, inclusion in playlists, etc.

2. **Key Metrics**: Include more specific data on past successes to bolster your case – e.g., numbers from previous hits.

3. **Engagement Strategies**: Expand on how you plan to utilize the influencer campaign and direct engagement tools to convert listeners into long-term fans.

4. **Partnerships**: Mention any potential or confirmed partnerships that could amplify the reach of the campaign.

By refining these areas, the pitch could become even more compelling to Amazon.
