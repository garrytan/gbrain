## Question

So last night I sold 64 Wicked Watermelon, 49 Beetlejuice, 41 Old Hollywood, 40 Black Magic, 18 Screamtini. Assuming that I'm pouring 6 ounces of each, how many liters did I consume?

## Answer

Mr. White, about **37.62 liters** (≈ **37.6 L**).

Quick math:  
Total drinks = 64+49+41+40+18 = **212**  
Ounces poured = 212 × 6 = **1,272 oz**  
Liters = 1,272 × 0.0295735 ≈ **37.62 L**.
