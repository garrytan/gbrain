## Question

What’s my bmi I’m 6’4 at 157lbs

## Answer

Your BMI is about **19.1**, Mr. White.  

That’s in the **“normal”** BMI range (18.5–24.9).

## Question

What would 25 be

## Answer

For you at 6'4", a BMI of **25** corresponds to about **205 lb** (93 kg), Mr. White.

## Question

At 12% bf

## Answer

If you were **205 lb at 12% body fat**, Mr. White, that would break down roughly as:

- **Fat mass:** ~25 lb  
- **Lean mass (LBM):** ~180 lb  

Your BMI would still be **25** at that body weight and height (6'4")—BMI doesn’t “see” body fat %, it only uses height and weight.

## Question

Ffmi at 205

## Answer

At **205 lb, 6'4", 12% body fat**, your **FFMI is ~22** (about **21.9** unadjusted).  

Using the common height-adjusted version, it’s about **21.2**.

## Question

What’s a 23 ffmi for me at 8%bf

## Answer

For you at 6'4", an **FFMI of 23 at 8% body fat** works out to roughly:

- **Bodyweight:** ~**205 lb** (93 kg)  
- **Lean mass:** ~**189 lb**  
- **Fat mass:** ~**16 lb**

So basically: **around 205 at 8%** puts you at an FFMI of about **23**, Mr. White.
