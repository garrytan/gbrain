**Rising Artist Tim White Drops Sultry New Single "Never Say A Word"**

**Los Angeles, CA – June 21, 2024** – Tim White, the dynamic rising artist, is set to captivate listeners with his latest single, “Never Say A Word.” This seductive, high-energy track marks the third collaboration between White and acclaimed co-writers Scott Stallone and Tomi Fallenius, following their hits “Love Don’t Let Me Down” and “Songs About You.”

At the time, she was very publicly dating someone else, so we had to keep our connection hidden.”

The single showcases White’s evocative vocals complemented by haunting female harmonies. It begins with a pop-rock vibe that seamlessly transitions into an upbeat electronic pop drop, featuring the bold title line. The provocative lyrics, including lines like “you’re kinda hot like me” and “I love the way it sounds when your body talks to me,” amplify the song’s sexy, confident atmosphere. A nod to Jeffree Star adds an extra layer of intrigue to the track.

Expertly mixed by Grammy-nominated engineer Ariel Chobaz and mastered by nine-time Grammy winner Randy Merrill, “Never Say A Word” stands out as a cool, provocative, and confident summer anthem, showcasing Tim White’s versatility and artistry.
