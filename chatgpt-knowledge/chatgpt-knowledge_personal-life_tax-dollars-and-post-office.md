## Question

Do tax dollars fund the post office 

## Answer

No — the U.S. Postal Service is **not funded by tax dollars** for its operating expenses. It runs on revenue from postage, products, and services.  
The only exception is occasional federal funding for specific things like free mail for the blind or overseas voters.
