## Question

What about if you don't feel empathy?

## Answer

A lack of empathy—particularly a persistent or complete absence—can be associated with psychopathy. Psychopaths typically have significantly reduced empathy, meaning they don't emotionally resonate with the feelings or experiences of others. Sociopaths, meanwhile, may have limited empathy but can still form emotional connections with select people.

However, lacking empathy doesn't automatically mean you're a psychopath. Empathy can vary significantly among individuals for many reasons, including psychological conditions, personality traits, or past experiences.

If you're concerned or want clarity, speaking with a mental health professional can provide accurate insight.
