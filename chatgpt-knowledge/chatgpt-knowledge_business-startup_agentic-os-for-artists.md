## Answer

Mr. White — **“Agentic OS for artists & labels” is YC-plausible, but I’d tighten it.**

YC descriptions tend to be blunt category statements: for example, Koyal uses **“Agentic AI Filmmaking Platform”** as its short YC descriptor, while other YC entertainment companies use direct “what it is / who it helps” framing like **“Lovable for Minecraft Mods”** or “automated marketplace for raves and electronic dance music.” citeturn812790view2turn167456view2

### YC 50-char description

Your current line:

**Agentic OS for artists & labels**

**AI catalog OS for artists & labels**

### Hero H1

Between:

**Monetize your music catalog**  
and  
**Release more music with less work**

I’d choose **“Monetize your music catalog”** if the buyer is labels, managers, or artists with existing songs. It speaks to money, which is sharper.

But it may imply only licensing/royalties/sync. If your product also helps release, promote, and grow catalog revenue, the best H1 is probably:

**Release and monetize music with less work**

That combines both pain points and avoids sounding like a passive catalog-finance tool.
