# Lindsay's sister-in-law

> **Source:** ChatGPT conversation
> **Date:** 2024-03-20T02:31:19Z
> **Messages:** 12
> **Topic:** research-learning

## Question

Okay, so we have a female named Lindsay. She's getting married to a male named Austin. Lindsay has a sister named Mackenzie. What is Mackenzie's relationship to Austin?

## Answer

Mackenzie is Austin's sister-in-law.

## Question

Does that make Austin McKenzie's brother-in-law?

## Answer

Yes, that makes Austin Mackenzie's brother-in-law.

## Question

Are you sure or are you just saying yes?

## Answer

Yes, I'm sure. Once Lindsay and Austin are married, Austin becomes Mackenzie's brother-in-law, and Mackenzie becomes Austin's sister-in-law.

## Question

Austin has three brothers. What are their relationships to McKenzie?

## Answer

Austin's three brothers would be Mackenzie's brothers-in-law.

## Question

If Mackenzie gets married to Tim, what is Tim's relationship to Austin?

## Answer

If Mackenzie marries Tim, Tim becomes Austin's brother-in-law.

## Question

What about Austin's brothers in relation to Tim?

## Answer

Austin's brothers would also be Tim's brothers-in-law.
