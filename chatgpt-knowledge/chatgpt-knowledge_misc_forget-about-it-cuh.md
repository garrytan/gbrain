## Answer

Mr. White, when you say “forget about it, cuh,” you’re essentially telling someone “never mind, don’t worry about it, bro.” “Cuh” is street-slang short for “cousin,” used like “homie” or “friend” — so the phrase is just a casual way to drop the subject and move on.
