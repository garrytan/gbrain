To calculate the Fat-Free Mass Index (FFMI), you need to know the total lean mass and the height of the individual. The formula is:

\[ \text{FFMI} = \frac{\text{Lean body mass in kg}}{\text{height in meters}^2} \]

From the provided information:
- Lean Tissue (lbs) is 145.4 (from the 10/26/2023 measurement).
- Height is 76.0 in (which is 6.33 feet or 1.93 meters).

First, convert lean body mass from pounds to kilograms:
\[ 145.4 \text{ lbs} \times 0.453592 = 65.96 \text{ kg} \]

Next, plug the values into the FFMI formula:

\[ \text{FFMI} = \frac{65.96}{1.93^2} = \frac{65.96}{3.7249} = 17.71 \]

So, Mr. White, your FFMI is approximately 17.71.
