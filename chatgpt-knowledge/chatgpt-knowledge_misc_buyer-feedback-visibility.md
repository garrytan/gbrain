# Buyer feedback visibility

> **Source:** ChatGPT conversation

## Question

Can buyers see feedback i leave them on facebook marketpalce

## Answer

No, buyers cannot see the feedback you leave them on Facebook Marketplace. Only you and Facebook can see it. Buyers can only see the ratings and reviews sellers receive from other buyers, not the feedback you’ve given.
