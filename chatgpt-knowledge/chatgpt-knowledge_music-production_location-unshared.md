## Question

Is it possible she just blanket stopped sharing location globally for a temporary period of time?

## Answer

Mr. White, yes—if she flips off “Share My Location” it stops sharing to everyone. In that case you’d see “Location Not Available” under her name. Seeing “Can see your location” means she still has sharing enabled for you, so she didn’t globally disable it.

## Question

Wouldn’t that mean she turned off sharing for me but I still have it on for her

## Answer

Mr. White, “Can see your location” simply means your share-to-her is still ON. It does not tell you her share-to-you status. In other words:

- You are sharing to Sophie (hence “Can see your location”).  
- Sophie’s share-to-you is off (so you see no map or “Location Not Available”).  

These toggles are independent—her having you on her list doesn’t force her to share back. If she wanted to share with you, you’d see her location or “Location Available” instead.
