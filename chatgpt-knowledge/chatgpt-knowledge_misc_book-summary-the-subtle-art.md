## Question

Concisely summarize the major points in the subtle art of not giving a fuck

## Answer

Mr. White, here’s the book in a nutshell:

- Your “fucks” are finite—spend them on what truly matters, ignore the rest.  
- Happiness = solving meaningful problems; suffering is unavoidable, so choose *better* problems.  
- Measure yourself by controllables (effort, honesty), not approval, status, or outcomes.  
- Responsibility ≠ fault—you always own your response to what happens.  
- You’re not special; entitlement breeds misery—embrace ordinary and improve.  
- Be unsure on purpose: expect to be wrong, update your beliefs.  
- Failure is the path: take action first (“do something” principle), learn, iterate.  
- Boundaries matter: say no, commit to fewer things; commitment creates freedom.  
- Remember death; use it to clarify values and live deliberately.
