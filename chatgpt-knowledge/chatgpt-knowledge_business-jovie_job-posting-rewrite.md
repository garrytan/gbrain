# Dishwasher & Porter — High-Volume Immersive Dinner Theatre

**Start:** November 15  
**Location:** On-site, Los Angeles, CA  
**Schedule:** Thu–Sun 6:00pm–12:00am; some Wednesdays. On two-show nights, 5:00pm start (occasional 3-show days).

## What You’ll Do
- **High-volume dishwashing** for a bar + kitchen serving **up to 300 guests per show (2 shows/night)** with fast turnarounds.  
- Run commercial dish machines; sort/rack glassware & plates; hand-wash as needed; maintain sanitation standards.  
- **Porter duties:** break down and clean dish area; sweep/mop; trash/recycling; stock back-of-house.  
- **Restrooms:** post-show cleaning of 2 guest restrooms and restocking soap, paper, and supplies.  
- Close to standard and complete checklists safely (chemicals, PPE).

## What It Takes
- **1+ year** in **high-volume** dishwashing (restaurant, hotel, banquet, venue) or porter/janitorial.  
- Can thrive under tight time windows between shows; calm, fast, and consistent.  
- Reliable team communication with FOH/BOH.  
- Able to lift 50 lb, stand/walk 6–8 hours, work in heat/steam and with chemicals.  
- **CA Food Handler Card** (or obtain within 30 days).  
- Night/weekend/holiday availability.

Please include:
- Brief note on **recent high-volume** experience (racks/hour or seats/night).  
- Your availability (Thu–Sun nights + some Wed) and **earliest start (Nov 15)**.  
- Confirmation you have (or can obtain) a **CA Food Handler Card**.
