"Tortured Poets Society" appears to be a misinterpretation or variation of the actual album title, which is "The Tortured Poets Department." This is Taylor Swift's eleventh studio album, announced on February 4, 2024, and released on April 19, 2024. The album consists of 16 standard tracks and includes collaborations with Post Malone and Florence + The Machine.

The album has received mixed reviews, with some praising its emotional depth and others critiquing its length. It features personal and introspective songwriting, exploring themes of heartbreak, public perception, and emotional vulnerability.
