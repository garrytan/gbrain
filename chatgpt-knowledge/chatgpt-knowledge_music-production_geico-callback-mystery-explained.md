## What is probably happening

When you dial GEICO:

1. Their system identifies your caller ID.
2. It matches you to an open claim, quote, policy, or callback queue.
3. A GEICO/claims vendor outbound dialer immediately calls you from a non-800 number.
4. Your phone shows it as an incoming Dallas call while you are also trying to call the main GEICO number.

The fact that it happened **twice, immediately, only when dialing that GEICO number** makes random spam less likely.

## Still treat it as unverified

Do **not** give SSN, bank info, full DOB, or payment info to the 945 number unless GEICO confirms it.
