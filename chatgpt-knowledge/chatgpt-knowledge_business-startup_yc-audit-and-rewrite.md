## Quick YC audit (what to fix)
- **Lead is clever, not clear.** “Stop sending fans to a page of links” is fine, but you need *who it’s for* in the first line.
- **Promise is vague.** “Guides every fan…” → say what improves: *email/SMS captures, streams, tips, CTR*.
- **Missing proof / specifics.** Add 2–4 concrete bullets: what it personalizes on (new vs returning, device, referrer, geo, past clicks).
- **Feature list is long + a bit “SaaS-y.”** YC prefers: simple product, clear wedge, sharp differentiation.
- **CTA should match MVP reality.** “Request early access” is good—add “Private alpha, onboarding manually” near the top, and what they get (free setup, priority, etc.).
- **Copy cleanup:** fix typos (“aduit”, “ts”), remove repeated “Request Early Access” if it feels spammy.
