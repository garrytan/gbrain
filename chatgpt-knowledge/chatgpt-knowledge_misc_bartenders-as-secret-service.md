## Question

mock up a costume for our bartenders at our show. they will be playing secret service agents while bartending

## Answer

For the bartenders at your show, the costume should be stylish, practical, and reflective of a Secret Service agent. Here’s a mockup idea:

### Costume Components:
1. **Black Suit:**
   - Tailored black suit jacket and trousers.
   - A crisp white dress shirt underneath.
   - Black tie or bow tie for a formal touch.

2. **Accessories:**
   - Sunglasses (classic aviators or black wraparound shades).
   - An earpiece with a coiled cord (fake or real) to mimic communication devices.
   - Black leather belt.

3. **Footwear:**
   - Black polished dress shoes.

4. **Props:**
   - A small lapel pin (e.g., an American flag or a star) to add authenticity.
   - Optional black gloves for a more tactical look.

5. **Name Tag:**
   - A small, professional name tag with “Agent [Last Name]” or simply “Agent.”

### Optional Additions:
- **Badge:** A mock badge on the belt or inside the suit jacket.
- **Utility Belt:** With prop items like a flashlight, small notebook, and pen.

### Female Version:
- A fitted black skirt suit or pantsuit with similar accessories.
- Sensible black heels or flats.

### Practical Considerations:
- Ensure the costume allows for easy movement and comfort, especially for bartending activities.
- Fabrics should be breathable and not too heavy to avoid overheating.

This costume will not only make the bartenders look the part but also add to the immersive experience of your show.

## Question

will fingerless gloves pass health code

## Answer

Fingerless gloves typically do not meet health code requirements for food and beverage service because they do not fully cover the hands and fingers, which are critical areas for maintaining hygiene. Here are a few alternatives that maintain the sexy look while adhering to health code requirements:

### Alternatives to Fingerless Gloves:

1. **Stylish Full Gloves:**
   - Opt for tight-fitting, stylish black full gloves that cover the entire hand. Look for gloves made of breathable material to ensure comfort during service.

2. **Black Latex or Nitrile Gloves:**
