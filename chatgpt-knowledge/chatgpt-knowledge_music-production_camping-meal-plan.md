\*Quest’s “net” carbs; full carbs are mostly fiber/polyols.

**Dial it in**

- Need to top up ~6 g fat & ~7 g carbs (∼25 kcal)?  
  - 2 Tbsp queso (3 F/3 C) **or**  
  - 1 oz tortilla chips (7 F/19 C—halve the serving) **or**  
  - One 12-oz hard seltzer (0 F/2 C/100 kcal) and drop half a Quest bar.

That lands you within ≈ ±5 g of each macro and ±50 kcal of your 1 444-kcal cut—close enough for a five-day trip.

### Ramen & chips reality check  
One brick instant ramen **= 380 kcal, 14 F, 52 C, 8 P**. If you crush a pack, drop a Quest + Barebells and count it as your carb/fat bomb for the day.

---

### Game plan

- **Morning:** Shake + Quest bar.  
- **Mid-day:** Shake.  
- **Dinner around the fire:** Hot-dog, queso/chips or seltzer.  
- **Pre-sleep:** Shake + (if needed) ½ Quest to finish macros.  

Log it in MacroFactor each night; you’ll stay on target without Genius Shots. Enjoy the trip and keep those abs photo-ready when you get back.
