## Question

How to make simple syrup

## Answer

To make simple syrup, mix equal parts sugar and water in a saucepan. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring until the sugar is completely dissolved. Cool it down before using. Store it in the refrigerator.

## Question

How much sugar for 8 oz of water

## Answer

For 8 oz of water, use 8 oz of sugar to make simple syrup.

## Question

A 4lb bag of sugar?

## Answer

A 4lb bag of sugar is equivalent to 64 oz. So, it would make 64 oz of simple syrup when mixed with an equal amount of water.
