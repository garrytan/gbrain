## Question

Moscow mule recipe

## Answer

A Moscow Mule is a refreshing and simple cocktail. Here's how to make one:

### Ingredients:
- 2 oz Vodka
- 4 to 6 oz Ginger beer
- 1/2 oz Fresh lime juice
- Ice cubes
- Lime wedge for garnish
- Mint sprig for garnish (optional)
- Copper mug or highball glass

### Instructions:
1. **Prepare the Mug/Glass**: Fill a copper mug or a highball glass with ice cubes.
2. **Mix**: Pour 2 oz of vodka and 1/2 oz of fresh lime juice over the ice.
3. **Top Off**: Add 4 to 6 oz of ginger beer to fill the mug or glass.
4. **Garnish**: Stir gently to mix. Garnish with a lime wedge and, if you like, a sprig of mint.
5. **Serve**: Enjoy your Moscow Mule!

Note: The copper mug is traditional and believed to enhance the flavor and experience, but it's not mandatory. The essential part is the balance of the sharp ginger beer with the vodka and the freshness of the lime.

## Answer

The French 75 is a classic cocktail with a refreshing and bubbly taste. Here’s how to make it:

### Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 ounces gin
- 3/4 ounce fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 ounce simple syrup (or to taste)
- 3 ounces Champagne (or a similar sparkling wine)
- Ice cubes
- Lemon twist, for garnish

### Instructions:
1. **Combine Gin, Lemon Juice, and Syrup**: In a cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine the gin, fresh lemon juice, and simple syrup.
2. **Shake**: Shake well until the mixture is well chilled, about 15 seconds.
3. **Strain**: Strain into a chilled champagne flute or a coupe glass.
4. **Top with Champagne**: Carefully top the drink with Champagne.
5. **Garnish**: Twist a strip of lemon peel over the drink to release the oils, and drop it into the glass as garnish.
6. **Serve**: Enjoy immediately.

The French 75 is a light and effervescent cocktail that’s perfect for celebrations or as an aperitif.
