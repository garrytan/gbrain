## Question

what does the new meta manus do

## Answer

Here’s what **Meta’s “Manus” AI** does (as of early 2026):

**Core purpose**  
Manus is an **autonomous AI agent platform** Meta acquired and is integrating across its products. It’s not just a chatbot; it is designed to *carry out complex tasks from start to finish* without constant human guidance. citeturn0search34

**Key capabilities**
- **Research & synthesis:** gather information across the web, synthesize into structured reports, summaries, and documents. citeturn0search34  
- **Automation & workflow execution:** plan and perform multi-step tasks (e.g., code writing, data analysis, report building). citeturn0search34  
- **Web interaction:** navigate websites, extract data, fill forms, and complete online procedures. citeturn0search34  
- **Content & file generation:** create slides, websites, dashboards, visualizations, and other outputs. citeturn0search34  
- **Asynchronous execution:** tasks can continue even if you disconnect, with results delivered when done. citeturn0search34

**Integration into Meta products**
- **Meta Ads Manager:** advertisers can use Manus inside Ads Manager to *generate natural language campaign insights*, detect anomalies, build reports, and answer questions about metrics. It doesn’t yet make automatic bid changes — it informs decisions. citeturn0search11  
- **Messaging/Agents:** Meta is rolling out **Manus Agents** that let you interact with your AI agent in apps like Telegram (and soon WhatsApp) to start tasks via chat. citeturn0search26

**What it isn’t**
- Not merely a conversational chatbot. Manus is structured to *execute work autonomously* and generate finished results, not just text responses. citeturn0search34

**Business context**
Meta views this tech as part of a shift from reactive AI models to *agentic systems* that perform meaningful work across platforms — research, business workflows, and future commerce/assistant features. citeturn0news4

This summary refers to **the AI agent Manus integrated by Meta**, not the unrelated **Manus VR gloves** used for hand tracking in XR/robotics. citeturn0news4
