## Question

## Answer

Mr. White, lymphatic drainage can help with **fluid-related** puffiness—by moving excess interstitial fluid out of tissues it may give you a “de-bloating” feeling—but it **doesn’t** touch gas-related bloating:  

- **Fluid retention:** Professional lymphatic drainage massage has been shown to ease swelling and puffiness (including mild abdominal fluid build-up), so you might feel less bloated afterward citeturn0news22.  
- **Gas-related bloating:** That’s driven by trapped air or impaired gut motility. Lymphatic work doesn’t address gas—abdominal massage that stimulates your bowels or dietary tweaks (e.g., reducing FODMAPs, eating slower) are what actually relieve gas bloating citeturn0search12.
