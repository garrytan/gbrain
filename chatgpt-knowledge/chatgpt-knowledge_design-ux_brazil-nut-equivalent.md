# Brazil Nut Equivalent

## Question

My diet currently allows for 15g of olive oil. I want to remove that and eat nuts instead. Find me the macro equivalent in nuts

## Answer

To replace 15g of olive oil with nuts, you need to match the macronutrient profile, particularly the fat content. Olive oil is almost entirely fat.

- 15g of olive oil has approximately 135 calories and 15g of fat.

Almonds, for instance:
- 1g of almonds has about 6 calories and 0.5g of fat.

So, to match 15g of olive oil, you would need about 22.5g of almonds (providing roughly 135 calories and 11.25g of fat). This isn't an exact match in fat content, but it's close, and nuts provide additional nutrients like protein and fiber.

## Question

What about Brazil nuts

## Answer

For Brazil nuts:

- 1 gram of Brazil nuts has approximately 7 calories and 0.7 grams of fat.

To match the 135 calories and 15 grams of fat in 15g of olive oil:

- You would need about 19 grams of Brazil nuts, which would provide around 133 calories and 13.3 grams of fat.

This is a close match, although Brazil nuts also offer other nutrients like selenium, protein, and fiber.

## Question

How many Brazil nuts is that

## Answer

Brazil nuts vary in size, but on average, one Brazil nut weighs about 5 grams. So, 19 grams of Brazil nuts would be approximately 3 to 4 nuts. Keep in mind that this is an estimate, and actual weights can vary. It's always best to use a scale for precise measurement.
