# Streams Needed for RIAA Certifications

## Question

How many streams do you need for Ria’s certifications

## Answer

For RIAA certifications in the United States, the number of streams needed varies based on the type of certification. Here's the breakdown:

- Gold certification: 500,000 units
- Platinum certification: 1,000,000 units
- Multi-Platinum certification: 2,000,000 units and more in increments of 1,000,000

For digital streaming, 1,500 audio or video streams are equivalent to 10 track sales, which counts as 1 unit. Therefore, for Gold certification, you would need 75 million streams, for Platinum, 150 million streams, and for Multi-Platinum, it would require at least 300 million streams, and so on, increasing in increments of 150 million streams for each additional platinum level.

## Question

Does YouTube count 

## Answer

Yes, YouTube streams do count towards RIAA certification. Both video and audio streams from YouTube are included in the calculations for digital streaming units needed to achieve gold, platinum, and multi-platinum certifications.

## Question

What about UGC 

## Answer

Yes, User Generated Content (UGC) on platforms like YouTube also counts towards RIAA certifications. This includes videos created by users that feature music, such as covers, remixes, or other derivative works, provided they meet certain criteria set by the platform and the music is properly licensed. These streams are factored into the overall streaming count needed for certifications.
