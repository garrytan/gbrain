## Answer

Mr. White, here’s the clear answer:

**In a Facebook group like the one shown, your post is visible to:**
- **Everyone who is a member of that group**
- **No one outside the group**, unless:
  - A group member **shares or screenshots it**, or
  - The group is set to **public** (many "ask for help/money" groups actually are public)

So the visibility depends on **the group’s privacy setting**:

### **If it’s a PRIVATE group**
- Only members can see your post
- Non-members cannot
- Posts still *can* be screenshot by members

### **If it’s a PUBLIC group**
- Anyone on Facebook can view it
- Posts might appear on search, feeds, or profiles depending on Facebook behavior

---

## **How to check the group privacy quickly**
On the group’s page (top area), it will say either:
- **Public Group**
- **Private Group**

That one word tells you everything you need.
