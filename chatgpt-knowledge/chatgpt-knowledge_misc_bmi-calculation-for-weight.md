# BMI Calculation for Weight

## Question

325lbs at 6'1 is what bmi

## Answer

The BMI for a person who is 6'1" and weighs 325 lbs is approximately **42.88**, which falls in the **Class 3 (Severe) Obesity** category.

## Question

for 30 bmi

## Answer

To have a BMI of 30 at 6'1", the target weight would be approximately **227.4 lbs**.

## Question

6'4 male

## Answer

To have a BMI of 30 at 6'4", the target weight would be approximately **246.5 lbs**.
