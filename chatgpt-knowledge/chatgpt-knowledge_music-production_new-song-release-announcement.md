## Question

what does breakthrough artist mean

## Answer

A "breakthrough artist" refers to a musician or performer who has recently achieved significant recognition and success, often moving from relative obscurity to a higher level of fame and influence. This term implies that the artist has made a notable impact with their music, gaining a larger audience and critical acclaim. Breakthrough artists often experience a surge in popularity due to a hit song, album, or performance that propels them into the spotlight.
