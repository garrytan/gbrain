## 🛠 Tips to Get It *More* Haunted Mansion–Like

Even with a “haunted voice” generator, you’ll likely need to process it further to nail the tone. Here are sound design tweaks:

- **Add reverberation / echo** – long decays, subtle slap, pre-delay to make the voice float in space.  
- **Layer in ambient whisper / low drones** behind the voice (just below its volume) for depth.  
- **Pitch modulation / subtle warble** – slight detuning or vibrato in places gives it an uncanny quality.  
- **EQ / filter** – scoop mids or boost low-mid for warmth, cut highs subtly to make it darker.  
- **Delay / slap echo on some words** – especially at phrase ends (“…now…”, “farewell…”)  
- **Dynamic pauses / randomness** – insert extra pauses or stutters to simulate ghostly hesitation.  
- **Distortion / saturation (very lightly)** on select vocal peaks to add grit.
