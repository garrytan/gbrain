## Answer

Mr. White — here’s a cleaned up, clearer set of app notes, grouped and action-oriented:

**Metric Cards / Metrics Stack**
- Improve drag-and-drop visuals: the drag state for metric cards should look cleaner and more polished.  
- Clean up the color palette on the metric-card stack for consistency and readability.  
- Fix `% lean` calculation/labeling: current result seems incorrect (example: at 14.7% BF it shows “53” despite implying top ~1% leanness). Recheck formula, units, and percentile mapping.  
- Body score “starting point” card: text is currently illegible — increase contrast/size and adjust layout.

**Onboarding**
- Make onboarding quiz available pre-auth, but persist answers and attach them after account creation.  
- Redesign welcome screen with stronger visual hierarchy.  
- Improve design hierarchy across onboarding screens so “Continue” is the obvious primary action.  
- Auto-advance onboarding steps when a settings section is completed (wherever it’s safe/clear to do so).

**Apple Health Sync**
- Add missing weight icon on the Apple Health synced screen.

**Sharing**
- Add score sharing via two triggers:
  - Automatically prompt/share flow when a screenshot is detected.
  - Manual share button on the score screen.
