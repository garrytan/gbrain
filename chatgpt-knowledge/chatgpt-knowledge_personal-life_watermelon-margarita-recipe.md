## Question

Watermelon margarita.

## Answer

Here's a recipe for a Watermelon Margarita:

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups fresh watermelon, cubed and seeded
- 2 oz tequila
- 1 oz lime juice (freshly squeezed)
- 1 oz triple sec
- 1 tsp agave syrup (optional, for added sweetness)
- Ice cubes
- Lime wedge and salt for rimming the glass (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Blend the watermelon cubes until smooth. Strain the juice to remove any pulp.
2. Rim the glass with a lime wedge and dip it into salt if desired.
3. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add the watermelon juice, tequila, lime juice, triple sec, and agave syrup.
4. Shake well until thoroughly mixed and chilled.
5. Fill the rimmed glass with ice and pour the margarita mixture over the ice.
6. Garnish with a lime wedge or a small watermelon slice.

Enjoy your refreshing Watermelon Margarita!

## Question

Pineapple margaritas with mezcal and jaelpino

## Answer

Here's a recipe for a Pineapple Margarita with Mezcal and Jalapeño:

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup (8 oz) pineapple juice
- 1.5 oz mezcal
- 0.5 oz lime juice (freshly squeezed)
- 0.5 oz triple sec
- 0.5 oz simple syrup
- 2-3 slices of fresh jalapeño (adjust to taste)
- Ice cubes
- Lime wedge and salt for rimming the glass (optional)
- Pineapple slice or jalapeño slice for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Rim the glass with a lime wedge and dip it into salt if desired.
2. Muddle the jalapeño slices in the bottom of a cocktail shaker to release their flavor.
3. Fill the cocktail shaker with ice. Add the pineapple juice, mezcal, lime juice, triple sec, and simple syrup.
4. Shake well until thoroughly mixed and chilled.
5. Fill the rimmed glass with ice and pour the margarita mixture over the ice, straining out the jalapeño slices if preferred.
6. Garnish with a lime wedge, pineapple slice, or a fresh jalapeño slice.

Enjoy your Pineapple Margarita with Mezcal and Jalapeño!

## Question

Mojito with chambord

## Answer

Here's a recipe for a Mojito with Chambord:

**Ingredients:**
- 10 fresh mint leaves
- 1/2 lime, cut into 4 wedges
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1 oz white rum
- 1 oz Chambord
- Club soda
- Ice cubes
- Mint sprig and lime wedge for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Place the mint leaves and 1 lime wedge into a sturdy glass. Use a muddler to crush the mint and lime to release the mint oils and lime juice.
2. Add 2 more lime wedges and the sugar, and muddle again to release the lime juice.
3. Fill the glass almost to the top with ice cubes.
4. Pour the rum and Chambord over the ice.
5. Fill the glass with club soda and stir well.
6. Garnish with the remaining lime wedge and a sprig of mint.

Enjoy your refreshing Mojito with Chambord!
