## Question

are eggs dairy

## Answer

No — eggs are **not dairy**.

**Why:**  
- **Dairy** comes from the *milk* of mammals (cows, goats, etc.).  
- **Eggs** come from birds (like chickens), not milk-producing mammals.

So eggs are **protein foods**, not dairy products.

Yes, **eggs are vegetarian** for most definitions.

**Facts:**
- **Lacto-ovo vegetarians** eat eggs ✔️
- **Ovo-vegetarians** eat eggs ✔️
- **Vegans** do **not** eat eggs ❌

Reason: eggs are unfertilized and do not involve killing an animal.

## Question

so what is the differen between vegan and vegetarian

## Answer

**Difference between vegetarian and vegan (clear and factual):**

**Vegetarian**
- Eats **plants**
- May eat **animal by-products**:
  - Dairy ✔️
  - Eggs ✔️
- Does **not** eat meat, poultry, or fish

**Vegan**
- Eats **plants only**
- Eats **no animal products at all**:
  - Meat ❌
  - Fish ❌
  - Dairy ❌
  - Eggs ❌
  - Honey ❌

**One-line summary:**  
Vegetarians avoid killing animals; vegans avoid **using** animals.
