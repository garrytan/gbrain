## Question

All 5 of my moes smart switches have suddenly gone offline. I have no internet issues or problems with other smart devices. How can I resolve this?

## Answer

Troubleshooting your MOES smart switches could involve a variety of steps:

1. **Check Network Settings:**
   - Ensure that your router is functioning properly and that its firmware is updated.
   - Make sure your smart switches are within the range of your Wi-Fi network.

2. **Restart Devices:**
   - Restart your router and smart switches to see if this resolves the connectivity issue.

3. **Reset Smart Switches:**
   - If restarting doesn't help, you may need to reset your smart switches to factory settings and set them up again. Consult the manual for instructions on how to do this.

4. **Check for Software Updates:**
   - Check if there are any firmware or software updates available for your smart switches.

5. **Inspect Smart Home Hub:**
   - If you have a smart home hub, ensure it's working properly and is updated.

6. **Check Manufacturer's Website or Contact Support:**
   - Check the MOES website or contact their customer support for specific troubleshooting steps or known issues.

7. **Check for Interference:**
   - Other wireless devices or appliances could cause interference. If possible, eliminate potential sources of interference and see if the connectivity improves.

8. **Check App Settings:**
   - Ensure that the settings within your smart device app are correct and that the switches are still registered.

Following these steps methodically should help in identifying or fixing the issue with your MOES smart switches going offline.
