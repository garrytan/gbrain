Mr. White, here’s a four-step plan:

---

**1. Hygiene-task audit**  
Make sure you cover every zone and function:  
- **Face:** daily shave, cleanse, exfoliate (once/week), post-shave balm  
- **Body:** chest, stomach shave/trims, underarms, groin (if desired), shower soap  
- **Nose/ears:** nasal-hair trimming, ear-canal hair if needed  
- **Eyebrows/sideburns:** trim/shape  
- **Hair:** shampoo, conditioner, mousse, gel/paste  
- **Oral care:** brush, floss, mouthwash, whitening strips  
- **Skin care:** moisturizer, barrier ointment (Aquaphor)  
- **Lips:** chapstick  
- **Nails:** clip, file, nail-brush under-nail cleaning  

---

**2. Right tools & products**

| Barrier ointment   | Aquaphor Healing Ointment  
| Chapstick          | Nivea Men Active Care  
| Nails              | Tweezerman clipper + nail-brush + medium file  

---

**3. Optimization & tool-upgrades**  
- Swap disposable razors for a long-life cartridge handle (fewer replacements).  
- Upgrade body groomer to a waterproof, cordless multi-groomer (all attachments: chest, groin, trimmer).  
- Consolidate nose/ear and detail trimming on one multi-groomer platform.  
- Use electric toothbrush with timer and pressure sensor—more consistent brushing.  
- Keep whitening strips in a slim case; apply while shampooing (automates usage).  
- Pre-fill travel-size bottles—cut rinse time.  

---

**4. Modular bag system**  
- **Main dopp kit:** water-resistant, hangable (e.g. Sea to Summit Hanging Toiletry Bag)  
- **Inner pouches:**  
  - **Shower pouch:** razor, shaving gel, body groomer, nose trimmer  
  - **Hair pouch:** shampoo, conditioner, mousse, gel  
  - **Sink pouch:** toothbrush, floss, mouthwash, moisturizer, Aquaphor, chapstick  
  - **Detail pouch:** nail clipper/brush, scissors, file  
- Label each pouch; drop only the one you need into the steam/shower area.  
- After use, wipe dry tools, return to their pouch—keeps wet items away from dry.  

---

With this you’ll hit every hygiene point, have optimal gear, shave minutes off your routine and keep everything organized and dry.
