## Question

Possibly a hot pink background with the bold text or something. Or something else creative that requires nothing to be shot. Either b-roll, or generated backgrounds. 

Come up with an idea I can build out in davinci resolve relatively quickly (im a skilled video editor)

## Answer

For your lyric video for "Never Say A Word," here's a creative and visually appealing idea you can build out in DaVinci Resolve quickly:

### Concept: Neon Night Vibes

**Background:**
1. **Hot Pink Gradient:** Use a hot pink gradient background that transitions between various shades of pink. This will give the video a dynamic and lively feel.
2. **Neon Elements:** Add subtle, animated neon elements like lines, shapes, or abstract patterns that move slowly in the background. This will enhance the visual interest without overwhelming the text.

**Text:**
1. **Bold Text:** Use a bold, sans-serif font like "Montserrat" or "Futura" in white or a contrasting bright color (like neon blue) to stand out against the hot pink background.
2. **Animation:** Animate the text to appear with smooth transitions like fades, slides, or zooms. Sync these transitions with the beat of the song for added impact.
3. **Text Glow:** Add a slight neon glow effect to the text to complement the neon elements in the background. This can be done using the Glow effect in DaVinci Resolve.

**Additional Elements:**
1. **B-Roll Footage:** Incorporate some stock footage of cityscapes at night, neon signs, or abstract light patterns. Overlay these clips with a low opacity to create a layered, dreamy effect.
2. **Particle Effects:** Use particle effects like slow-moving sparkles or light flares to add a touch of magic to the video.

### Step-by-Step Execution:

1. **Create Background:**
   - Add a hot pink gradient background using the Color Generator in DaVinci Resolve.
   - Overlay animated neon shapes or lines using Fusion. These can be simple shapes like circles, lines, or waves that move subtly.

2. **Text Animation:**
   - Add your text using the Text+ tool.
   - Animate the text to appear on screen in sync with the beat of the song. Use keyframes to create smooth transitions like fades, slides, or zooms.
   - Apply a Glow effect to the text to create a neon glow.

3. **Incorporate B-Roll:**
   - Select stock footage clips of cityscapes, neon signs, or abstract lights.
   - Overlay these clips on top of the gradient background with a low opacity to add depth without distracting from the text.

4. **Add Particle Effects:**
   - Use the Fusion page to create or add pre-made particle effects like sparkles or light flares.
   - Position these effects to complement the movement of the text and background elements.

5. **Final Adjustments:**
   - Adjust the color grading to ensure the hot pink background and neon elements are vibrant and cohesive.
   - Sync the transitions and effects with the music to ensure a smooth and engaging flow.

This concept will give your lyric video a vibrant, modern look that matches the energetic and catchy vibe of "Never Say A Word." It's simple enough to execute quickly but visually striking to keep viewers engaged.
