## Question

What is SDR?

## Answer

**SDR = Sales Development Representative**, the role that books qualified meetings for Account Executives.

**What they do**
- Prospect: build lead lists, research, light personalization.
- Outreach: cold email, LinkedIn, calls, DMs.
- Qualify: budget, authority, need, timeline (BANT/CHAMP).
- Set demos, hand off to AE; no heavy closing.

**Comp**
- Base: ~$45–65k.
- OTE (base + commission hitting quota): **$80–150k** common.
- Quota examples: 10–25 qualified meetings/month or $ pipeline created.

**KPIs**
- Meetings booked, show rate, qualified rate, opportunities created, replies %, calls/day.

**Why it fits**
- You’re persuasive, resilient, and comfortable high-volume. Easy to automate.

**Automation stack**
- **Lead gen:** Apollo/Clay.
- **Enrichment/verify:** Clearbit, NeverBounce.
- **Sequencing:** Instantly/Lemlist.
- **Personalization:** Claude/GPT generates 1–2 bespoke lines from site/LinkedIn.
- **Routing:** Calendly + Aircall; HubSpot/Pipedrive CRM.
- **VA help:** list cleanup, inbox triage, data entry.
