### Why no PayPal fee
eBay Managed Payments folds payment processing into the final value fee, so you **don’t pay a separate PayPal %** anymore (buyers may pay with PayPal, but you’re charged only eBay’s combined fee + the $0.40 per order). citeturn2search4turn2search3

### Fine print / sensitivities
- **Fee rate:** DJ Equipment / Pro Audio is **9.35%** (since Feb 14, 2025) + **$0.40** per order; fee is charged on the **total amount of the sale, including item price, shipping, and sales tax**. citeturn1view0turn4search1
