## Question

For our halloween show, I will playing the joker. I will be in a dr office and giving people "shots" they will be alcoholic shots with syringes. I will give the guests shots in character as the joker. Before they come in they will pick the "treatment" they want "Voka or tequila etc" and pay for the experience with our cashier who is a waitress in character as Harley quin and acting as the nurse. 

I need to welcome the guest, ask them some comedic questions like reading an eye chart or asking them about their sexual history etc. then I will give them the shot and then give them some one liner to get them out of the room and welcome the next patient.  

Give me some ideas for a script I can use. I want to be in character as the joker but also in the provocative and sexy theme of the show.

## Answer

Here's a script you can use while staying in character as the Joker, blending humor, provocation, and a touch of dark charm:

---

**[As the guest enters]**

**Joker:** *[With a wide grin]* "Ah, welcome to my little house of horrors, darling! The doctor is in, and it’s time for your... treatment. Let’s see, what’s your poison today? Vodka to numb the pain or tequila to burn it all away?"

*[Wait for their response]*

**Joker:** "Excellent choice! Now, before we begin, I need to ask you a few routine questions... You know, just to ensure you’re *sane* enough for this procedure."

---

**[Comedic Questions]**

**Joker:** *[Points to an eye chart with chaotic, Joker-themed symbols]* "Can you read the top line for me? Oh wait, I forgot—I'm the one who's supposed to be insane here!"

**Joker:** *[Leans in close]* "How’s your sexual history? Because after this shot, I might just be your wildest encounter!"

**Joker:** *[In a teasing tone]* "Any allergies? Just kidding! I don't care. It’s not like you’ll remember this night, right?"

---

**[Administering the Shot]**

**Joker:** *[As he prepares the syringe]* "Now, this won’t hurt... much. But if it does, well, that’s just part of the fun, isn’t it?"

*[Gives the shot]*

---

**[One-Liner to Exit]**

**Joker:** *[With a wink]* "There you go, my little lunatic. Don’t be a stranger—remember, I always love repeat patients."

**Joker:** "Now, off you go! Harley’s waiting outside with the *bill*... but remember, the insanity is on the house!"

**Joker:** *[Laughing maniacally]* "Next patient, please! Let’s see who else needs a dose of madness."

---

This script should help you create a memorable and engaging experience for your guests, fully embodying the Joker while keeping things provocative and in line with the theme of the show.
