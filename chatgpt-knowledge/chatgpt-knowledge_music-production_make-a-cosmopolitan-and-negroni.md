## Question

I want to clean my humidifier and I have hydrogen peroxide. Can I put hydrogen peroxide in the humidifier with water and then run it? Will that help clean it? Will that do anything good? Will that be harmful in any way?

## Answer

Using hydrogen peroxide in your humidifier is not recommended. While hydrogen peroxide is a powerful disinfectant, when used in a humidifier, it can be dispersed into the air and potentially inhaled. This could irritate your lungs and mucous membranes. Instead, use hydrogen peroxide to clean the humidifier parts by soaking or wiping them, then rinse thoroughly with water before using the humidifier. Always follow the manufacturer's instructions for cleaning and maintenance.

## Question

A Cosmo as a bartender makes it

## Answer

To make a Cosmopolitan, a bartender typically follows these steps:

1. **Ingredients**: You'll need vodka, triple sec (or any orange-flavored liqueur), cranberry juice, and fresh lime juice.

2. **Mixing**: Combine the ingredients in a cocktail shaker. A common ratio is 1.5 oz vodka, 1 oz triple sec, 0.5 oz cranberry juice, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice.

3. **Shaking**: Add ice to the shaker, then shake well to chill the mixture.

4. **Straining and Serving**: Strain the cocktail into a chilled martini glass.

5. **Garnish**: Often, a Cosmopolitan is garnished with a twist of lime or a small slice of lime on the rim of the glass.

Each establishment or bartender might have their own slight variations, but this is the general method for making a Cosmopolitan.

## Question

Negroni

## Answer

To make a Negroni, the process is straightforward:

1. **Ingredients**: Equal parts of gin, sweet vermouth, and Campari.

2. **Mixing**: Combine the gin, sweet vermouth, and Campari in a mixing glass.

3. **Stirring**: Add ice and stir the mixture until well-chilled.

4. **Straining and Serving**: Strain the cocktail into an old-fashioned glass filled with ice.

5. **Garnish**: Garnish with an orange peel or slice.

This classic cocktail is known for its balanced, bitter, and sweet flavor profile.
