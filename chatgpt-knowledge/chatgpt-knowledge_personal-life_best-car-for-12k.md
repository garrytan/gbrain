## Question

So of i buy this cash, do i wire the money or use a cardnor what

## Answer

Cash deal ≈ 3 common ways—ask the dealer which they’ll take before you show up:

| Option | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| **Cashier’s check** (get at your bank) | Widely accepted, no extra fees | Bank trip, dealer will verify; bring ID |
| **Wire transfer** | Works for high $$, no paper to carry | Must initiate during bank hours; car/keys released only after funds hit their account (could be next day) |
| **Debit card for a small deposit + cashier’s check for the balance** | Locks the car while you grab the check | Many banks cap daily POS at $5k–$10k |

Avoid:

* **Credit card for the full amount** – dealers rarely allow it > $3k because they eat ~3% fees.  
* **Actual stacks of cash** – AML paperwork, counting delays, safety risk.

**Do this:**

1. Call the finance manager, ask “cash purchase, what form of payment do you want?”  
2. Have the bank print the cashier’s check **payable to the dealership’s legal name**; note the exact purchase price incl. tax/fees.  
3. Bring a second form of ID + proof of insurance to drive away same day.
