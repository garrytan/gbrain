1. **Low Visual Hierarchy on Key Actions**  
   - “Complete Your Setup” items blend into the background.  
   - The “Add →” link looks small and secondary, when it should feel like a CTA.  

2. **Empty-State Problem**  
   - Dashboard content is almost blank aside from the checklist.  
   - Returning users (like your test account here) see little incentive to engage.  

3. **Redundant Wording**  
   - Example: “Social links added” under “Add social links” is confusing — reads as if the step is already done.  

4. **Feedback Loop Missing**  
   - No visual progress indicator (e.g., 1/3, 2/3, 3/3 complete).  
   - No reinforcement (confetti, badge, highlight) when a task is completed.  

5. **Navigation Density**  
   - Sidebar has six items but most won’t be used early.  
   - “Overview” and “Dashboard” are essentially duplicates.  

---

## 🎯 Recommendations
1. **Boost CTA Prominence**
