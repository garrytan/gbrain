The cleanest pronunciation is:  

**Zoo-LAY-nuh** (like “Helena”).  

Some people might also say:  
- **Zoo-LEE-nuh** (softer middle syllable, like “Selena”), or  
- **Zoo-LEN-uh** (shorter “e” sound).  

But if you want it elegant and smooth, **Zoo-LAY-nuh** is the standout.
