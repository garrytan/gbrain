## Question

I don’t wanna accept inbound phone calls anymore. I wanna force petty text or email and I’ll make an outbound call of it warrants my time. Helps keep set this up

## Answer

Here’s how to set up a system where calls go to voicemail or are screened, and people are funneled into text or email unless you decide otherwise:

---

### 📱 On Your iPhone (assumed):
1. **Silence Unknown Callers**  
   **Settings > Phone > Silence Unknown Callers** → *ON*  
   → Sends calls not in your contacts straight to voicemail.

2. **Custom Voicemail Greeting (Set the Tone)**  
   > “Hey, I don’t take inbound calls. Please text or email me what this is about. If it’s important, I’ll get back to you.”

3. **Do Not Disturb with Allow List**  
   **Settings > Focus > Do Not Disturb**  
   - Allow calls only from Favorites (if anyone).
   - Silence others.

4. **Auto-Reply with Text**  
   **Settings > Focus > Do Not Disturb > Customize Screens**  
   - Add **Auto-Reply** message like:  
     > “I don’t answer calls. Text or email me what this is about.”

---

### 📩 Force Text/Email (Everywhere Else):
- **Email Signature:**
  > *“I don’t take unscheduled calls. Please email or text first. I’ll respond if warranted.”*

- **Text Auto-Reply (Optional via Shortcut Automations):**  
   Use **Shortcuts > Automation** to auto-reply to unknown texts.

---

### 🔒 Bonus: Use a Call Screening App
- **Apps like Hiya, Truecaller, or Google Voice**  
   → Screen calls, send unknowns to voicemail, or block spam entirely.
