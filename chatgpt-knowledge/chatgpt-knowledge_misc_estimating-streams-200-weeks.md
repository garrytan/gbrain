## Question

5,000 streams a week. 1 million when

## Answer

To find out when 1 million streams will be reached at a rate of 5,000 streams per week:

\[ \text{Weeks} = \frac{1,000,000 \text{ streams}}{5,000 \text{ streams per week}} \]

\[ \text{Weeks} = 200 \]

It will take 200 weeks to reach 1 million streams.

## Question

## Answer

To convert 200 weeks into years:

\[ \text{Years} = \frac{200 \text{ weeks}}{52 \text{ weeks per year}} \]

\[ \text{Years} \approx 3.85 \]

It will take approximately 3.85 years to reach 1 million streams at a rate of 5,000 streams per week.

\[ \text{Weeks per year} = 52 \]

Next, we divide the total number of streams by the number of weeks in a year:

\[ \text{Streams per week} = \frac{1,000,000 \text{ streams}}{52 \text{ weeks}} \]

\[ \text{Streams per week} \approx 19,231 \]

You would need approximately 19,231 streams per week to reach 1 million streams in 1 year.
