## The core idea

Artists fail because the release loop is too slow.

They make a song, wait weeks or months, manually coordinate artwork, metadata, distributor uploads, pre-saves, smart links, social posts, fan notifications, videos, analytics, and follow-up. By the time they learn anything, they are already exhausted or onto the next song.

Jovie compresses that loop.

> **Song → release plan → assets → links → fan activation → analytics → next release.**

That is the flywheel.
