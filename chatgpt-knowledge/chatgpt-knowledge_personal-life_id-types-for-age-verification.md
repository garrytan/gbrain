## Question

Espresso martini bartender recipe

## Answer

For a classic Espresso Martini, you'll need:

1. 2 oz Vodka
2. 1/2 oz Coffee Liqueur (like Kahlúa)
3. 1 oz Espresso (freshly brewed and cooled)
4. Ice
5. 1/2 oz Simple Syrup (optional, for added sweetness)

Instructions:
1. Fill a shaker with ice.
2. Add vodka, coffee liqueur, espresso, and simple syrup (if using).
3. Shake well until the outside of the shaker feels cold.
4. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
5. Garnish with a few coffee beans.

This recipe strikes a balance between strong coffee flavor and the smoothness of vodka, with a touch of sweetness from the liqueur. Enjoy responsibly!

## Question

Margaritta

## Answer

For a classic Margarita, you'll need:

1. 2 oz Tequila
2. 1 oz Lime Juice (freshly squeezed)
3. 1/2 oz Orange Liqueur (like Cointreau or Triple Sec)
4. Salt (for rimming the glass)
5. Ice
6. Lime wheel (for garnish)

Instructions:
1. Rim the edge of a glass with lime juice and dip in salt.
2. Fill the glass with ice.
3. In a shaker, combine tequila, lime juice, and orange liqueur with ice.
4. Shake well.
5. Strain into the prepared glass.
6. Garnish with a lime wheel.

This Margarita recipe offers a perfect blend of tequila's robustness with the freshness of lime and the subtle sweetness of orange liqueur. Enjoy responsibly!
