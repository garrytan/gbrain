## Answer

Mr. White, I think you mean **the “Shining”-style table read / rehearsal scene in _The Lost City_** — the scene where Daniel Radcliffe’s villain setup has that polished, slightly absurd “rich guy expedition briefing” energy.

If you mean **“table radar”** literally, there *isn’t* a real radar table driving the plot. It’s more like a **prop / command-table visual** used to sell the billionaire-villain “I have a whole operation” vibe.

For the **island / jungle stuff** in _The Lost City_: it was **mostly real location work**, not just LED volume. A lot was shot in the **Dominican Republic**, with practical jungle/beach locations plus studio/VFX cleanup. The movie uses greenscreen and VFX where needed, but the island look is largely location-based.

Likely read:

- **Island/jungle:** real Dominican Republic locations + VFX
