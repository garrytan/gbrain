How does eating my post workout carbs prior to my workout effect muscle growth

## Answer

Consuming carbs before your workout instead of post-workout could have a few effects, influenced by factors like timing, type of exercise, and your overall nutrition.

1. Energy Availability: Eating carbs pre-workout can provide energy, potentially allowing for more intensity and possibly benefiting muscle growth indirectly.

2. Glycogen Replenishment: Consuming carbs post-workout is usually recommended for glycogen replenishment, which is beneficial for muscle recovery and growth. Eating them before might not be as effective for this purpose.

3. Insulin Response: Carbs trigger an insulin response, which can be anabolic, but timing might be less critical than overall daily intake.

4. Nutrient Timing: Dr. Mike Isratel and RP Strength suggest that nutrient timing is less crucial than total daily intake for muscle growth but can still be a factor.

Overall, the impact might not be significantly negative if your total caloric and macronutrient targets for the day are met.
