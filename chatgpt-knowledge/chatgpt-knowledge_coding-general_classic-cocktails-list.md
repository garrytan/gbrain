---

### 🥃 Whiskey / Bourbon Cocktails
- **Old Fashioned** – whiskey, sugar, bitters, orange peel  
- **Whiskey Sour** – whiskey, lemon, sugar (optionally egg white)  
- **Mint Julep** – bourbon, mint, sugar, crushed ice  
- **Boulevardier** – bourbon, Campari, sweet vermouth *(not possible without vermouth, so skip)*  
- **Hot Toddy** – whiskey, hot water, honey, lemon  

✅ Usable: *Old Fashioned, Whiskey Sour, Mint Julep, Hot Toddy*

---

### 🍸 Vodka Cocktails
- **Cosmopolitan** – vodka, triple sec, cranberry, lime  
- **Moscow Mule** – vodka, lime, ginger beer  
- **Bloody Mary** – vodka, tomato juice, lemon, Worcestershire, Tabasco, salt/pepper  
- **Screwdriver** – vodka, orange juice  
- **Vodka Collins** – vodka, lemon, sugar, soda  

✅ Usable: *All above (no vermouth needed)*

---

### 🍹 Rum Cocktails
- **Daiquiri** – rum, lime, sugar  
- **Mojito** – rum, mint, lime, sugar, soda  
- **Piña Colada** – rum, pineapple, coconut cream  
- **Cuba Libre** – rum, Coke, lime  
- **Rum Punch / Planter’s Punch** – rum, citrus, sugar, bitters, optional grenadine  

✅ Usable: *All above*

---

### 🥂 Tequila Cocktails
- **Margarita** – tequila, triple sec, lime  
- **Paloma** – tequila, grapefruit soda (or juice + soda), lime  
- **Tequila Sunrise** – tequila, orange juice, grenadine  

✅ Usable: *All above*

---

### 🍋 Gin Cocktails
- **Tom Collins** – gin, lemon, sugar, soda  
- **Gimlet** – gin, lime, sugar or lime cordial  
- **Gin Fizz** – gin, lemon, sugar, soda  
- **Negroni** – gin, Campari, sweet vermouth *(skip)*  
- **Bee’s Knees** – gin, lemon, honey  

✅ Usable: *Tom Collins, Gimlet, Gin Fizz, Bee’s Knees*

---

### 🧊 Other / Mixed Classics
- **Dark ’n’ Stormy** – dark rum, ginger beer, lime  
- **Whiskey Highball** – whiskey, soda  
- **Gin and Tonic** – gin, tonic, lime  
- **Vodka Soda** – vodka, soda, lime  
- **Tequila Soda** – tequila, soda, lime  
- **Cuba Libre** – rum, Coke, lime
