## Question

So if I have, like, say, three Coke Zeros in a day, and then a caffeine-free Diet Coke, and then an espresso shot, and then a black ice coffee, what's my total estimated caffeine content for the day?

## Answer

To estimate your total caffeine intake for the day based on the beverages you've mentioned:

- **Three Coke Zeros:** 3 x 34 mg = 102 mg
- **Caffeine-free Diet Coke:** 0 mg
- **Espresso shot (from Starbucks):** 75 mg
- **Black iced coffee:** Typically around 165 mg per 12 oz serving

Adding these up, your total caffeine content for the day would be:
102 mg + 0 mg + 75 mg + 165 mg = **342 mg**.
