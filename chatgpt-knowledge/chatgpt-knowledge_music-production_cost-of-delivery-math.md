## Question

What would I have to value my time at to be profitable 

## Answer

Let’s work it backward.  

Profit = Sale price – (Car cost + Time cost)  

Sale price = **$15**  
Car cost (IRS $0.67/mi) for a 5-mile round trip = **$3.35**  
Time in minutes = **20 min** (0.333 hr)  

We want Profit ≥ 0:  
\[
15 - (3.35 + 0.333T) \ge 0
\]  
\[
0.333T \le 11.65
\]  
\[
T \le 34.95 \ \text{\$/hr}
\]  

So for a **5-mile trip, 20 min total**, you’d have to value your time at **$35/hr or less** to break even.  

If you value your time higher than that, you’re losing money.
