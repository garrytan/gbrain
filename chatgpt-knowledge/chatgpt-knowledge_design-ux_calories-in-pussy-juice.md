How do I stop people from calling me on Snapchat?

## Answer

To stop people from calling you on Snapchat, you can manage your privacy settings to limit who can contact you. Here's how to adjust those settings:

1. **Open Snapchat**: Launch the Snapchat app on your device.
2. **Go to Your Profile**: Tap on your profile icon, usually located in the top-left corner of the screen.
3. **Access Settings**: Tap on the gear icon in the top-right corner to enter the settings menu.
4. **Scroll to 'Who Can...':** In the settings menu, scroll down until you see the 'Who Can...' section.
5. **Select 'Contact Me'**: Tap on 'Contact Me'.
6. **Adjust Settings**: Here, you have a few options. You can select 'My Friends' if you only want people you've added as friends to be able to call you. There is no option to completely disable calls without also blocking messages, but limiting this to 'My Friends' can significantly reduce unwanted calls.

If you're receiving calls from someone you've added but no longer wish to receive calls from, you may need to remove them from your friends list or block them. Blocking someone on Snapchat prevents them from contacting you in any form, including calls, messages, and snaps. 

To block someone:
1. Go to your friends list.
2. Find and hold down on the person's name.
3. Select 'More'.
4. Tap 'Block'.

Remember, changes in privacy settings and blocking someone can affect how you interact with others on Snapchat, so use these features as you see fit.
