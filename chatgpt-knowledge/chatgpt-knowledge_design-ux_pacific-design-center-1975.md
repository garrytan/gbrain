## Question

Hey, when was the Pacific Design Center built?

## Answer

The Pacific Design Center in West Hollywood, California, was built in phases. The first phase, the Blue Building (known as the "Blue Whale"), was completed in 1975. The Green Building was completed in 1988, and the Red Building was finished in 2012.

## Question

Was that the plan from the beginning or did that evolve over time?

## Answer

The phased construction of the Pacific Design Center was part of the original plan. The architect, Cesar Pelli, designed it with the intention of expanding over time. This approach allowed for the complex to grow and adapt to the changing needs of the design community.

## Question

So is it fully complete now?

## Answer

Yes, the Pacific Design Center is considered fully complete with the addition of the Red Building in 2012. This marked the final phase of the original design plan by Cesar Pelli.
