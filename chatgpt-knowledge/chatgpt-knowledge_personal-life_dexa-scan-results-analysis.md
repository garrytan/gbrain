## Question

What is my FFMI?

## Answer

The Fat-Free Mass Index (FFMI) is calculated using the formula:

\[ FFMI = \frac{FFM}{height^2} \]

Where:
- FFM is the fat-free mass in kilograms
- height is in meters

From the information you provided:
Your weight is 183 lbs (83.01 kg) and 10% body fat.

So, your fat mass = \( 0.10 \times 83.01 \) = 8.301 kg
Your fat-free mass (FFM) = Total mass - Fat mass = 83.01 kg - 8.301 kg = 74.709 kg

You are 6'4" which is approximately 1.93 meters.

Inserting these values into the formula:

\[ FFMI = \frac{74.709}{1.93^2} \]
\[ FFMI = \frac{74.709}{3.7249} \]
\[ FFMI = 20.04 \]

So, Mr. White, your FFMI is approximately 20.04.
