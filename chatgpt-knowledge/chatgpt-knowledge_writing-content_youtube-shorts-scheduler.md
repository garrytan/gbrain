## Question

I need to hire someone to handle uploading & scheduling youtube shorts to my youtube channel.

I will provide the video files already edited and they need to upload them to the channel and set up all the tags and descriptions.  It should all be SEO friendly and engineered to do well. 

## Answer



There are a few ways you can find someone to handle uploading and scheduling YouTube shorts for your channel. Here are some options:

1. Use Freelance Platforms: Freelance platforms such as Upwork, Freelancer, and Fiverr are great places to find qualified freelancers who can handle uploading and scheduling YouTube shorts. You can post a job listing on these platforms, outlining the job requirements, and freelancers can bid on the job.

2. Reach out to Virtual Assistants: Virtual assistants are another option to consider when looking for someone to handle uploading and scheduling YouTube shorts. You can find virtual assistants on freelance platforms or by reaching out to virtual assistant agencies. Virtual assistants can handle a variety of tasks, including social media management, content creation, and scheduling.

3. Hire a YouTube Manager: If you have a larger budget, you may want to consider hiring a YouTube manager to handle all aspects of your YouTube channel, including uploading and scheduling shorts. YouTube managers typically have experience with video editing, SEO, and social media marketing, and can help grow your channel and reach a larger audience.

When hiring someone to handle uploading and scheduling YouTube shorts, be sure to provide clear instructions and guidelines for the job. You should also provide the video files and any other necessary assets to ensure a smooth and efficient process.

## Answer

Sure, here's an example job listing that you can use as a starting point for hiring someone to handle uploading and scheduling YouTube shorts on Upwork:

Title: YouTube Shorts Uploader and Scheduler

Description:
I'm looking for a reliable and experienced individual to handle uploading and scheduling YouTube shorts for my channel. The ideal candidate should have a good understanding of YouTube SEO and be able to optimize each video for maximum visibility and engagement. 

Responsibilities:
- Upload pre-edited YouTube short videos to my channel
- Write and optimize titles, descriptions, tags, and thumbnails for each video
- Schedule each video for optimal viewing times
- Keep track of video performance and make adjustments as needed
- Ensure all videos are properly formatted and comply with YouTube's community guidelines

Requirements:
- Proven experience uploading and scheduling YouTube shorts
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Strong attention to detail and ability to multitask
- Knowledge of YouTube SEO best practices
- Ability to work independently and meet deadlines

To apply, please provide:
- Your resume or CV
- A portfolio or samples of previous YouTube short uploads you've handled
- Your availability and expected hourly rate

Thank you for your interest in this position!
