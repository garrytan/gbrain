## Answer

"Ted Lasso" originated from a series of promotional videos for NBC Sports' coverage of the Premier League in 2013. The character Ted Lasso, played by Jason Sudeikis, was depicted as an American football coach hired to coach a Premier League soccer team, despite having no experience with the sport. The character's cheerful ignorance and unflagging optimism struck a chord with viewers.

The popularity of these sketches led to the development of the "Ted Lasso" television series, which premiered on Apple TV+ in August 2020. The show, co-created by Sudeikis, Bill Lawrence, Joe Kelly, and Brendan Hunt, follows a similar premise but dives deeper into the characters and their stories, blending comedy with heartwarming and motivational themes. Sudeikis' portrayal of Lasso earned critical acclaim, and the show quickly gained a strong fanbase for its positive and uplifting message.
