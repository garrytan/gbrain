Mr. White, **“AI GTM agent for music artists”** is strong for a VC audience.

I’d make one refinement:

> **The AI go-to-market agent for music artists**

Use **“go-to-market”** on the hero slide instead of **“GTM”** unless the deck is purely for SaaS/VC insiders.

Best version:

> **The AI go-to-market agent for music artists**  
> Helping artists launch, grow, and monetize every release.

Why it works: it is specific, active, and tied to revenue/growth. It also avoids the vague “AI manager” framing.

Slightly sharper options:

> **The AI growth agent for music artists**

> **AI-powered go-to-market for music artists**

> **The AI revenue and growth agent for artists**

My pick:

> **The AI go-to-market agent for music artists**

With this subline:

> **Jovie helps artists turn every release into a coordinated growth and revenue campaign.**

## Answer

Mr. White, yes — **“AI marketing agent for music creators”** is probably the cleanest version if your market includes Suno/Udio-style creators, indie artists, producers, and non-traditional musicians.

Best hero line:

> **The AI marketing agent for music creators**

Best subline:

> **Jovie helps anyone turning ideas into songs launch, promote, and monetize their music.**

I would avoid **“music artists”** if you want to include prompt-native creators. “Artist” implies someone with an existing identity, fanbase, and career. **“Music creator”** feels broader and more internet-native.

I would avoid **“AI manager of artists”** for the pitch deck. It sounds like career management, booking, legal, brand deals, PR, and human representation.

I would avoid **“AI rev ops for music catalogs”** unless you are selling into labels, publishers, distributors, catalog owners, or rights holders. It sounds more B2B enterprise and less creator-first.

My strongest version:

> **Jovie is the AI marketing agent for music creators.**  
> **Create a song. Launch a campaign. Grow an audience. Monetize the catalog.**

Slightly more VC-coded:

> **The go-to-market layer for AI-native music creation**

Slightly more consumer/creator-coded:

> **The AI agent that turns songs into campaigns**

My pick for the deck:

> **The AI marketing agent for music creators**

Then define the category underneath:

> **As AI makes music creation instant, Jovie gives every creator the marketing infrastructure to launch, grow, and monetize.**
