## Answer

Here’s a cleaned-up duties checklist for your dishwashers:

### **Dishwasher Duties Checklist**

---

#### **Start of Shift**
1. **Stock Kitchen Items:** 
   - Ensure all plates, pans, and kitchen items are fully stocked on the line.
   
2. **Bathroom Maintenance:** 
   - Check that bathrooms are clean and fully stocked with necessary supplies.
   
3. **Trash Removal:** 
   - Take out any boxes or trash accumulated from the start of the day.

---

#### **End of Day**
1. **Dishes:**
   - Wash, dry, and put away all dishes in their designated areas.
   
2. **Floor Cleaning:**
   - Scrub and hose down all floors in the dish pit, line, and back kitchen area.
   
3. **Trash Management:**
   - Take out the trash and replace it with a new liner bag.
   
4. **Drain Maintenance:**
   - Clean and empty all drains.
   
5. **3-Compartment Sink:**
   - Scrub the 3-compartment sink thoroughly.
   
6. **Dishwasher Cleaning:**
   - Clean and wipe down the inside and outside of the dishwasher machine.
   
7. **Box Removal:**
   - Ensure all boxes are taken out.
   
8. **Rag Collection:**
   - Return all used rags to the designated area for washing.

---

#### **Weekly Tasks**
1. **Oven Cleaning:**
   - Perform a deep clean of the oven.
   
2. **Trash Bins:**
   - Wash all trash bins thoroughly.
   
3. **Wall Maintenance:**
   - Wipe down and keep walls clean.

---

This checklist should help keep the dishwashing area clean and organized, ensuring a smooth operation throughout the day.

Here’s the updated bartender duties checklist incorporating your detailed instructions:

### **Bartender Duties Checklist**

---

#### **Pre-Show**
1. **Update Inventory:**
   - Update the inventory sheet for specialty cocktails with the total liters in all three fridges.
   - If any cocktail has less than 6 liters available, immediately pour out into bottles. Notify the manager if more needs to be batched.

2. **Check Mixer & Liquor Inventory:**
   - Perform a quick check of mixer and liquor inventory.
   - Stock all mixers at all four bars and wells.
   - Replenish the back bar with additional liquor as needed.

3. **Bar Setup:**
   - Place bar mats at each bar.
   - Set up each bar with 2 matched shaker cup sets, 1 strainer, 1 jigger, 1 wine key, 1 speed opener, and 1 large ice scoop.
   - Bring out bus bins and dump buckets with strainers.
   - Stock clean bar rags in the towel drawer at FOH, lobby bars, and no man’s land.
   
4. **Glassware & Water:**
   - Stock glassware on the back bar.
   - Restock bottled water in the fridges.

5. **Ice Preparation:**
   - Just before the show, fill the large and small ice bins and bring them out.

6. **Pre-Shift (7:20 PM):**
   - Fire test prints to ensure Clover systems are working properly.
   - Check printer paper levels.

7. **Final Preparation:**
   - Get last looks on your outfit.
   - Be in place by 7:25 PM, ready for a 7:30 PM doors open.

---

#### **Post-Show**
1. Deep clean bars and fridges by wiping down all surfaces with hot, clean water; repeat as needed.

3. Join servers in polishing silverware, glasses, and plates until complete.
4. Notify the manager if running low on any pertinent supplies.
