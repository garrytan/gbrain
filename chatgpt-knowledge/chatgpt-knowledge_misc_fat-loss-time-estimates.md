2. **Goal Fat Mass**:
   - Goal body fat percentage: 12.4%

3. **Fat to Lose**:
   - Fat to lose = Current fat mass - Goal fat mass = 24.42 - 22.26 = 2.16 pounds

### If Losing 1 Pound per Week:

- Fat loss per week: 1 pound (assuming all weight loss is fat, which is an ideal scenario)
- Time to lose 2.16 pounds: 2.16 weeks

### If Losing 2 Pounds per Week:

Therefore, it will take approximately 2.16 weeks (around 2 weeks and 1-2 days) if you lose 1 pound per week, and approximately 1.08 weeks (around 1 week and 1 day) if you lose 2 pounds per week.
