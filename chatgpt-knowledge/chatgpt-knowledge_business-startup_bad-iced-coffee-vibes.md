### **What You Need**

### **Sunday Iced Coffee Setup**
**Step 1: Brew a strong pot (2x normal strength)**  
Use 1.5–2x the amount of grounds you normally would. You’ll dilute with ice later.

**Step 2: Let it cool for 15–30 min**  
Just leave the pot off the heat so it’s not piping hot going into glass.

**Step 3: Pour into your jar and refrigerate**  
Seal it. Keeps fresh in the fridge for up to 4–5 days.

---

### **Daily Routine**
- Fill a glass with ice
- Pour your cold coffee over it
- Optional: Add water if it’s too strong, or milk, or a splash of a flavored creamer

---

### **Once a Week**
Rinse your jar. Repeat on Sundays. Done.
