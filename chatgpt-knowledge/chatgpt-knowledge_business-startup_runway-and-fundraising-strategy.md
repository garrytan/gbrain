## 1) Retargeting: **not yet** (lowest priority this week)
Retargeting only pays when you have:
- meaningful traffic volume
- clear funnel events (visit → preview → claim → publish → upgrade)
- a stable onboarding flow

Until you’ve got consistent inbound/outbound conversion, retargeting is mostly paying to re-show ads to people you haven’t learned how to convert yet.

**Do instead (free “retargeting”):**
- Email/SMS follow-ups triggered by behavior (previewed but didn’t claim, claimed but didn’t publish, published but didn’t share, shared but didn’t upgrade).
That closes the leaky bucket without ad spend.

## 2) Referral program: **yes, but ultra-lightweight**
A referral program is valuable *because your product is itself a link surface*. But don’t build a complex system now.

### The offer you proposed (50% for 2 years) is too rich early
It can work, but it has downsides:
- anchors your unit economics early
- attracts “deal hunters” before PMF
- adds accounting + edge cases

### Better early version (simple + still compelling)
- **30% recurring for 12 months** *or* **$20 credit per paying referral**
- Pay only on **paid conversions** (not signups)

### Implementation that takes hours, not days
- Generate a unique referral code/URL per creator
