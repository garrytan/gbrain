### 1) **Protein & Recovery**

### 2) **Green + Energy**

- **Greens:** 1 handful spinach  
- **Fruit:** ½ green apple + ½ frozen pineapple  
- **Extras:** 1 tsp honey, ice

### 3) **Keto / Low-Sugar**
- **Base:** 1 cup unsweetened almond milk  
- **Fat:** 2 tbsp chia or flax  
- **Fruit:** ¼ cup frozen berries  
- **Extras:** ½ avocado, ice

### 4) **Creamy Oat Breakfast**
- **Base:** ¾ cup milk of choice  
- **Oats:** ¼–⅓ cup quick oats  
- **Fruit:** ½ banana + cinnamon  
- **Protein:** Optional scoop whey

### 5) **Post-Workout Carbs + Electrolytes**
- **Base:** 1 cup coconut water  
- **Fruit:** 1 banana + ½ cup frozen mango  
- **Extras:** ½ cup Greek yogurt, ice

- For consistency: more liquid = thinner; more ice/frozen fruit = thicker.
