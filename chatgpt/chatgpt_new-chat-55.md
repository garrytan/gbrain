Is it legal to cold call someone on the do not call list for sales

---

## Assistant

In Los Angeles, California, as in the rest of the United States, it is generally illegal to make sales calls to numbers listed on the National Do Not Call Registry. This applies to cold calls where the recipient has no existing relationship with the caller. There are exceptions, such as calls from charitable organizations, political groups, and other non-commercial calls. Violating these regulations can result in significant fines.

Where would I report this

---

## Assistant

In Los Angeles, California, or anywhere in the United States, you can report unwanted sales calls to numbers on the National Do Not Call Registry to the Federal Trade Commission (FTC). You can do this online through the FTC's complaint assistant at [donotcall.gov](https://www.donotcall.gov) or by calling 1-888-382-1222. It's helpful to provide as much information as possible, such as the date of the call, the company's name or phone number, and the content of the call.
