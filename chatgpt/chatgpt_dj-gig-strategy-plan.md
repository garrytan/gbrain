## Assistant

Mr. White, here’s a concise action plan to secure high-paying DJ gigs:

1. **Refine Your Brand & Materials**  
   - **Update EPK**: Create a polished Electronic Press Kit (EPK) highlighting your biggest milestones (Pacha NYC, 70M streams, Armada Music releases, etc.).  
   - **Include Recent Work**: Incorporate clips of newer tracks like "Never Say A Word" to demonstrate you’re still active and evolving.  
   - **Visual Identity**: Maintain consistent, professional artwork, logos, and branding across all platforms.

2. **Leverage Your Track Record**  
   - **Highlight High-Profile Collabs**: Emphasize your work with Cosmic Gate, Armada signings, and the support from heavyweight DJs.  
   - **Mention Industry Ties**: Showcase your roles with Netflix, Google, Hulu, and collaborations with Randy Jackson/Lauryn Hill—this sets you apart from typical DJs.

3. **Set Clear Pricing & Targets**  
   - **Local Gigs**: Minimum $1,000. Be upfront with potential clients or agencies; this weeds out lowball offers.  
   - **Travel Gigs**: Minimum $3,500 plus expenses. Negotiate travel, lodging, and a hospitality rider in your contract.

4. **Strategic Outreach & Networking**  
   - **Agents & Promoters**: Target specialty booking agencies that handle boutique events, festivals, and upscale venues with budgets to match.  
   - **Industry Events**: Attend or perform at smaller industry showcases (ADE, IMS, Miami Music Week) to reintroduce yourself to key players.  
   - **Build Relationships**: Reconnect with old contacts (Disco Biscuits’ circle, ex-Pacha promoters, label A&Rs) to find lucrative bookings through referrals.

5. **Digital Presence & Consistency**  
   - **Regular Release Schedule**: Even short edits, remixes, or mixtapes posted on social media to prove you’re active and relevant.  
   - **Social Proof**: Highlight well-known DJs who’ve supported your music; show photos or short quotes from them endorsing your work.  
   - **Targeted Ads**: Use well-crafted Facebook/Instagram ads to reach club owners, promoters, and agencies in your chosen markets.

6. **Showcase High Production Value**  
   - **Performance Footage**: Record at least one gig with professional audio and multi-angle video to exhibit your stage presence and crowd engagement.  
   - **Production Offerings**: If feasible, bundle special effects, custom visuals, or unique stage setups to justify higher rates.

7. **Maintain Momentum & Exclusivity**  
   - **Limit Availability**: Promote a sense of exclusivity by not saturating your local market with too many shows.  
   - **Follow-Up & Feedback**: After each event, secure testimonials from promoters and attendees to bolster your EPK and social proof.

By reinforcing your established reputation, showcasing fresh content, and aligning with promoters who regularly book top-tier talent, you’ll better position yourself to secure consistent, high-paying gigs.
