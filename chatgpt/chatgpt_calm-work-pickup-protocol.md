# 60-Minute “Calm Pickup” Protocol

## T-60 to T-50: Set the frame (3 mins) + logistics (7 mins)
- Text/email: “Arriving at __:__ to collect personal items only. I’ll be quick.”  
- Write your purpose line (on phone notes): **“I’m just here to grab my belongings and return any company property.”** Read it before you walk in.
- Pack: small tote/backpack, item list, charger/ID/keys, any company items to return, water. Wear neutral clothes; pockets clear.

## T-50 to T-40: Downshift your nervous system (10 mins)
- Do a 10-minute NSDR (deep-rest) reset. It reliably lowers arousal and steadies mood. citeturn0search1turn0search6turn0search16

## T-40 to T-35: Scripts (5 mins)
- Pick 1 line from each row and practice aloud once:
  - **Greeting:** “Hi. I’m here to collect my personal items.”
  - **Boundary (repeat & redirect):** “I’m just here to grab my things. Where can I do that?”
  - **If poked:** “I’m keeping this simple today—just picking up my stuff.”
  - **End:** “All set. Thanks for the help. Have a good one.”

## T-35 to T-30: Breathing buffer (5 mins)
- Do guided **box breathing 4-4-4-4** (inhale-hold-exhale-hold). citeturn0search2turn0search7

## T-30 to T-20: Mental walkthrough (10 mins)
- Picture the route, who you might see, and you staying neutral. Use **if-then plans**:
  - *If* someone asks why you quit, *then* “I’m not discussing that today—just here for my belongings.”
  - *If* someone is snide, *then* half-smile + nod + boundary line above.
- Set a **time box**: target <10 minutes inside.

## T-20 to T-10: Pre-drive/body tune (10 mins)
- Sip water. Skip caffeine.
- Quick **cold splash** to face or wrists, 20–30 sec (fast way to take the edge off).
- Put a calm track on cue (instrumental/lo-fi). One earbud only if you’ll keep it discreet.

## T-10 to T-0: Car arrival ritual (2 mins)
- Do **5 physiological sighs** (two quick inhales through nose, long slow exhale through mouth). It drops stress fast; use this any time during the pickup. If you want a 60–120 sec walkthrough, play one of these super-short clips. citeturn0search0turn0search5turn0search10turn0search20

# Inside: the 5-step playbook (aim: <10 minutes)
1) **Posture & pace:** Shoulders down, slow walk, low voice. Half-smile.
2) **Open & ask:** “Hi—here to collect my personal items. Where can I do that?”
3) **Pack methodically:** Phone on Do Not Disturb. Check drawers, coat hook, break area, shared storage. Quick photo of desk before/after if helpful.
4) **Boundary & redirect loop (if needed):**  
   - Them: “So… why’d you really quit?”  
   - You: “I’m not discussing that today. Just grabbing my things. Where should I leave my badge?”
   - If repeated: same line + “I’m on a tight clock.”
5) **Close cleanly:** Return any company property, confirm you’re done, “Thanks—have a good one,” and leave. Don’t linger.

# Tiny contingency plan
- If anyone escalates: stop, repeat boundary once, and **pause** (sigh breath). If still escalated: “I’ll come back at a better time,” and leave.
- If asked to sign anything unexpected: “I’ll review this later” and do **not** sign on the spot.
