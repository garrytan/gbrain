### 🔐 Admin Console (for domain admins)
1. Go to **https://admin.google.com** in your browser.  
2. Enter your **admin email address** (your domain email, *not* @gmail.com) and password.  
3. Complete any two-factor authentication prompts.  
This signs you into the **Google Workspace Admin Console** for managing users and domain settings. citeturn0search0turn0search1

### 📧 General Workspace login (email/apps)
If you just need to access your domain email or apps (Gmail, Drive, Calendar, etc.):
1. Go to **https://accounts.google.com/signin**.  
2. Enter your **full Workspace email** (e.g., you@yourdomain.com) and password.  
3. Complete 2FA if enabled.  
This gives access to your Workspace inbox and apps linked to that account. citeturn0search13

- “Google Apps” is now called **Google Workspace**; the core login process uses standard Google account sign-in. citeturn0search31
