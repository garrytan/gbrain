## User

Is there an age cap on how old you can be to drive Uber?

---

## Assistant

Uber doesn't have a specific age cap for drivers. However, you need to meet the minimum age requirement to drive in your city, have a valid driver's license, and at least one year of driving experience (or three years if you're under 23 years old). The car you intend to drive must also meet Uber's vehicle requirements, which vary by region.

## Assistant

Yes, technically, someone who is 103 years old could drive for Uber if they meet all the necessary requirements, including holding a valid driver's license and passing any required vehicle inspections. There are no upper age limits set by Uber as long as the driver can legally operate a vehicle and fulfills Uber’s other criteria.

## Assistant

That's a valid concern. While Uber requires drivers to have a valid driver's license and pass a vehicle inspection, there are no specific Uber policies that address the natural decline in abilities that can occur with aging. However, all drivers are still subject to the regular licensing requirements and renewal processes set by their state, which for California includes vision tests and potential reevaluation of driving skills during license renewals, especially for older adults. Additionally, if a driver’s ability to drive safely is called into question, further assessments can be mandated by the DMV.
