## Assistant

The **Razz Me Up** cocktail at The Vault is a renamed version of the Lewinsky's Lemonade. Here's the recipe:

### **Razz Me Up**
- **1.5 oz Vodka**
- **0.5 oz Raspberry Liqueur** (like Chambord)
- **2 oz Lemonade**
- **0.25 oz Fresh Lemon Juice** (optional, for extra tartness)
- **Top with Club Soda** (splash, for fizz)

**Garnish:** Lemon wheel or fresh raspberries  
**Glass:** Collins or highball  
**Method:** Shake vodka, raspberry liqueur, and lemonade with ice. Strain into ice-filled glass. Top with soda and garnish.
