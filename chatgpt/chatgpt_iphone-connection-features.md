## Assistant

Mr. White, that “glululup” is Apple’s **“Bringing Devices Together”** handshake. It just primes AirDrop / NameDrop—nothing moves until you pick something. Here’s what you can trigger the moment that “Connected” pops up:

- **NameDrop** – swap a brand‑new contact card or just receive theirs. (If you already have each other saved, it won’t overwrite anything.) citeturn0search1  
- **Quick‑AirDrop** – tap a photo, video, file, link, or passkey, hit Share → AirDrop, and it shoots over instantly without re‑finding the device. citeturn0search7  
- **SharePlay jump‑start** – open Apple Music, TV, Fitness+, a supported game, etc., start playing, bring the phones together again, and it launches a synced SharePlay session so you both watch/listen in real time with shared controls. citeturn4search0  
- **Tap to Cash (iOS 18+)** – in Wallet > Apple Cash, enter an amount, hold phones together, authenticate, and the money moves—no phone numbers exchanged. citeturn3search0  

That’s it. Once you pull the phones apart, the link ends; you’ll need to bring them back together to start another drop, SharePlay, or cash transfer.
