## User

what is a rusty nail and a salty dog

---

## Assistant

A Rusty Nail and a Salty Dog are both classic cocktails, each with distinct ingredients and flavor profiles.

1. **Rusty Nail**:
   - **Ingredients**:
      - 1 1/2 ounces Scotch whisky
      - 1/2 ounce Drambuie (a sweet, gold-colored liqueur with a Scotch whisky base, flavored with herbs, spices, and honey)
   - **Preparation**:
      - Fill a mixing glass with ice.
      - Pour the Scotch whisky and Drambuie over the ice.
      - Stir well until chilled.
      - Strain into an old-fashioned glass filled with ice.
   - **Garnish**: Traditionally, a Rusty Nail is garnished with a lemon twist, though this is optional.

2. **Salty Dog**:
   - **Ingredients**:
      - 1 1/2 ounces vodka or gin
      - 4 ounces grapefruit juice
      - Salt for rimming the glass
   - **Preparation**:
      - Rim an old-fashioned glass with salt (wet the rim of the glass, then dip it in salt).
      - Fill the glass with ice.
      - In a shaker filled with ice, combine the vodka or gin and grapefruit juice.
      - Shake well, then strain into the prepared glass.
   - **Garnish**: A Salty Dog can be garnished with a grapefruit slice or lime wedge, though this is optional.

Each of these cocktails offers a unique taste experience; the Rusty Nail is sweet and smooth with a hint of herbal complexity, while the Salty Dog is tart, refreshing, and has a salty kick from the rimmed glass.
