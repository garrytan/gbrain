## User

Same thing, watermelon juice. I had it shipped, and it was left out for 24 hours. Is it still good?

---

## Assistant

Mr. White—probably not safe.

- If it’s the kind that’s sold **refrigerated** or says **“Keep Refrigerated”** (most fresh/cold-pressed watermelon juices), 24 hours at room temp exceeds the **2-hour rule** → **discard**. citeturn0search0  
- Watermelon juice is relatively **low-acid and high-sugar**, so bacteria grow fast at room temp. citeturn0search3  
- **Only exception:** an **unopened, shelf-stable** (aseptic/UHT) carton that’s specifically labeled for **room-temperature storage**; those are fine at room temp until opened. Once opened, refrigerate. citeturn0search8turn0search1

Don’t taste-test; if there’s any doubt (swelling, fizzing, off-odors—or no signs at all), **throw it out**. citeturn0search19

Mr. White—no. **Discard it.**
