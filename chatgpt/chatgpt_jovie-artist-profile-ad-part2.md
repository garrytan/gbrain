Core structure:

## The website idea

**Jovie is the invisible layer behind the moments that build an artist.**

Each section should show one moment:

1. A fan hears the track.
2. A fan scans.
3. A fan supports.
4. A fan pre-saves.
5. A fan buys a ticket.
6. Jovie turns all of it into audience intelligence.
7. The artist sees the city light up.

That lets you target DJs and LA late-night artists without explaining everything like a dashboard.
