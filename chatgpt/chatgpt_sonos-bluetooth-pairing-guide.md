Hey homie, how can I pair a Sonos, uh, speaker over Bluetooth? What's the, what's the Bluetooth pairing combo on the Sonos, like, controls? I've got a play button and then up and down.

## Assistant

Short answer: on *most* Sonos speakers, including anything that only has **play/pause + volume up/down**, there is **no Bluetooth pairing combo** at all. They simply don’t do Bluetooth audio.

Let’s break it down for you, Mr. White:

### 1. What you’re describing
You said you have:
- A **play button**
- **Volume up and volume down**
- No other obvious buttons (like a Bluetooth icon, infinity symbol, or dedicated mode button)

That’s typical of:
- **Play:1 / Play:3 / Play:5**
- **Older Sonos One / One SL**
- Some soundbars, etc.

These are **Wi-Fi only** speakers. They:
- Connect via the **Sonos app** (over Wi-Fi)
- Do **not** accept Bluetooth audio from your phone, laptop, etc.
- Have **no secret button combo** to enter Bluetooth pairing mode

So if that’s your unit:  
> You **cannot** pair that Sonos directly over Bluetooth. Full stop.

### 2. When Sonos *does* have Bluetooth
Only the **portable** / newer lines support Bluetooth:
- **Sonos Roam / Roam SL**
- **Sonos Move / Move 2**
- **Sonos Era 100 / Era 300** (Bluetooth plus line-in on some models)

Those have:
- A **dedicated Bluetooth button** (often on the back or top)
- The normal process is something like:
  - Hold the Bluetooth button until the LED flashes a certain way
  - Then pick it from your phone’s Bluetooth list

If your speaker doesn’t have that Bluetooth button, you’re out of luck for direct BT pairing.

### 3. Workarounds if you *must* use Bluetooth
If you really want to feed Bluetooth audio into Sonos, your options are basically hacks:

1. **External Bluetooth receiver into a Sonos line-in**
   - Only works if you have a Sonos with **line-in** (e.g., old **Play:5**, **Five**, some soundbars, or an **Amp/Port**).
   - You’d connect a cheap BT receiver → line-in → group that speaker with the rest.

2. **Just use Wi-Fi (recommended)**
   - Install the **Sonos app**
   - Add the speaker to your Wi-Fi network
   - Use:
     - Sonos app directly
     - AirPlay 2 (if your Sonos model supports it + you’re on Apple)
     - Spotify Connect, etc.
