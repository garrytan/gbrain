## Assistant

**Boston Harbor Punch**

*Ingredients:*
- 1.5 oz Earl Grey-infused gin
- 0.75 oz honey syrup
- 0.5 oz fresh lemon juice
- 0.5 oz fresh grapefruit juice
- 1 dash Angostura bitters
- Splash of club soda
- Lemon twist for garnish

*Instructions:*
1. To make the Earl Grey-infused gin, steep 2 Earl Grey tea bags in 8 oz of gin for about 2 hours. Remove the tea bags and strain the gin.
2. Combine the Earl Grey-infused gin, honey syrup, lemon juice, grapefruit juice, and Angostura bitters in a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
3. Shake well and strain into a chilled glass filled with ice.
4. Top with a splash of club soda.
5. Garnish with a lemon twist.

This cocktail pays homage to the historic event with its use of tea-infused gin, a nod to the tea thrown into Boston Harbor. The honey syrup and citrus juices add a refreshing and balanced flavor, perfect for modern celebrations.

## Assistant

**The Tea Party Special**

*Ingredients:*
- 1 oz Earl Grey-infused bourbon
- 0.5 oz elderflower liqueur
- 0.5 oz fresh lemon juice
- 0.5 oz honey syrup
- 2 dashes of aromatic bitters
- Splash of sparkling water
- Lemon wheel and edible flower for garnish

*Instructions:*
1. To make the Earl Grey-infused bourbon, steep 2 Earl Grey tea bags in 8 oz of bourbon for about 2 hours. Remove the tea bags and strain the bourbon.
2. In a shaker filled with ice, combine the Earl Grey-infused bourbon, elderflower liqueur, lemon juice, honey syrup, and aromatic bitters.
3. Shake well and strain into a chilled rocks glass filled with ice.
4. Top with a splash of sparkling water.
5. Garnish with a lemon wheel and an edible flower.

The Tea Party Special is a sophisticated and floral cocktail, marrying the rich flavors of Earl Grey-infused bourbon with the delicate notes of elderflower. It’s a perfect drink for commemorating the historic Boston Tea Party with a modern twist.
