What is cpi

---

## Assistant

CPI stands for Consumer Price Index. It's a measure that examines the weighted average of prices of a basket of consumer goods and services, such as transportation, food, and medical care. It's commonly used as an indicator of inflation or deflation in an economy. When the CPI increases, it suggests that the average cost of this basket of goods and services has risen, indicating inflation. Adjusting wages, rent, or other financial obligations based on CPI changes is a way to align them with the current cost of living.
