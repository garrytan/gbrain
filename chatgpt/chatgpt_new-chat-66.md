A classic Cosmopolitan cocktail recipe typically includes the following ingredients:

- 1 1/2 oz vodka
- 1 oz cranberry juice
- 1/2 oz Cointreau (or any triple sec)
- 1/2 oz fresh lime juice

Instructions:

1. Fill a shaker with ice.
2. Add the vodka, cranberry juice, Cointreau, and lime juice.
3. Shake well until the outside of the shaker feels cold.
4. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
5. Garnish with a lime wheel or a twist of orange peel.

Enjoy responsibly!

For a refreshing gin-based cocktail, you might enjoy a classic **Gin and Tonic**. Here's a simple recipe:

### Ingredients:

- 2 oz Gin
- 4-6 oz Tonic Water
- Lime wedge for garnish
- Ice cubes

### Instructions:

1. Fill a highball glass with ice cubes.
2. Pour the gin over the ice.
3. Top with tonic water.
4. Gently stir to combine.
5. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Squeeze the lime wedge into the drink for an extra zesty flavor. Enjoy your refreshing Gin and Tonic!
