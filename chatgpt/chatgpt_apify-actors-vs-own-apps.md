## Assistant

Mr. White — here’s the straight, money‑speed comparison to hit **$10k MRR**.

## Bottom line
- **Fastest path:** Ship a **portfolio of Apify actors** priced on subscriptions + usage. Lower build scope, built‑in distribution, faster iteration.  
- **Your own apps:** Bigger upside/brand, but slower to first dollars (marketing, infra, support, SEO, payments, churn tooling).

## Speed-to-MRR math
**Apify actors**
- Typical sub price targets: **$29 / $49 / $99+** per month + usage.
- Path to $10k MRR:  
  - 120 subs @ $49 + 80 subs @ $29 ≈ **$9.9k** (plus usage often pushes you over).  
  - Or 150–220 total subs across 6–10 actors.
- Realistic ramp (if you ship weekly):  
  - **Month 1:** 3–5 actors live → $500–$1.5k MRR  
  - **Month 2:** 6–10 actors + iterations → $2k–$5k MRR  
  - **Month 3–4:** 10–15 actors, compounding reviews → **$7k–$12k MRR**
- Why it’s fast: marketplace demand + search, smaller surface area per product, easy upsell to bundles.

**Your own apps**
- Even with an MVP:  
  - **Cycle:** validate → build → market → iterate (SEO/ads/partnerships).  
  - **Time:** typically **3–6+ months** to $5k MRR unless you already have distribution.  
  - **Team/ops:** auth, billing, support, hosting, analytics, growth stack.

## Effort, risk, and levers
| Dimension | Apify actors | Your apps |
|---|---|---|
| Build scope | Tiny, single‑job tools | Full product surface |
| Distribution | Apify marketplace + search | You must create demand |
| Pricing power | Moderate, usage add‑ons | Higher ceiling if you win |
| Churn risk | Per‑actor but diversified across many | Concentrated in one product |
| Platform risk | Dependency on Apify | You own stack, slower start |

## What to build (fastest niches)
Go for **recurring, business‑critical scrapes/automations** with immediate ROI:
1. **B2B lead gen**: local venues, events, brand partnerships; enrich with emails/socials.  
2. **Price/stock monitoring**: liquor, CPG, apparel, tickets; alerting + CSV/webhook.  
3. **Social trend trackers**: TikTok/IG/YT mentions for a term (e.g., “Never Say A Word”), export + webhook to Slack/Sheets.  
4. **Local SEO packs**: scrape Google/Maps/Yelp/Resy/OpenTable for prospects; dedupe + enrichment.  
5. **E‑com competitive intel**: Amazon/Walmart/Target category deltas, buy‑box changes.  
6. **Events + venues**: scrape calendars in bulk for LA/NY; perfect for hospitality outreach.

Bundle several into **vertical packs** (e.g., *Hospitality Prospecting Suite*), and sell higher‑tier subs.

## Pricing playbook (actors)
- **Tiers:** $29 (hobby), $49 (pro), $99+ (agency) + per‑run usage.  
- **Value hooks:** free trial runs, 1‑click export to Google Sheets, webhooks to Zapier/Make.  
- **Retention:** saved configs, schedules, email/slack alerts, simple dashboards.

## 90‑day plan to $10k MRR
**Weeks 1–2**
- Ship **4 actors** (lead gen, price monitor, social mentions, events).  
- Each gets: clear README, example outputs, Sheets template, webhook sample, logo, keywords.  
- Start a “playbook” page linking them as a vertical pack.

**Weeks 3–6**
- Add **4–6 more** actors (variants + adjacent sites).  
- Instrument: error handling, rate‑limit resilience, retries, proxy pooling.  
- Collect reviews, iterate on DX (inputs, presets).

**Weeks 7–12**
- Hit **10–15 actors**; launch **2 bundles** with discounted annual plans.  
- Light outbound: short Looms to agencies/freelancers who use Sheets/Make.  
- Add usage‑based alerts and scheduled reports (email + Slack).

## When your own app wins instead
- You already have warm distribution or a partner channel.  
- A single product can command **$50–$200+ ARPU** with low churn (e.g., your **Jovie** smart‑link idea if you secure creators/labels early).  
- You need custom UX impossible inside an actor.

---

**Recommendation:** Focus the next 90 days on **Apify actor portfolios** aimed at **agencies and scrappy operators**. Ship weekly, bundle vertically, and layer usage‑based pricing. Reassess at ~$7k MRR to decide whether to double down or rotate the cash flow into your highest‑conviction app.
