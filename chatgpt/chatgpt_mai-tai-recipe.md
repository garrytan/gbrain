Here's a classic **Mai Tai** recipe (original Trader Vic–style):

### 🍹 **Mai Tai Recipe**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 oz aged Jamaican rum (like Appleton Estate)
- ¾ oz fresh lime juice
- ½ oz orange curaçao
- ¼ oz orgeat syrup
- ¼ oz simple syrup

**Optional float:**
- ½ oz dark rum (e.g., Myers’s) floated on top

**Garnish:**
- Mint sprig
- Spent lime shell
- Optional: cherry or pineapple wedge

**Instructions:**
1. Add all ingredients (except float) to a shaker with crushed ice.
2. Shake briefly (just to mix and chill).
3. Pour into a double old-fashioned glass (no straining).
4. Float dark rum on top if desired.
5. Garnish with mint and lime shell.
