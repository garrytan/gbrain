### 3. **“Behind the Song” Auto-Content Generator**
- **Concept**: An AI-powered tool that creates short-form, TikTok/Instagram-ready “Behind the Song” clips: visual storytelling of production, inspiration, and lyrics overlay. Auto-formats for Stories, Reels, and Notes.

### 4. **Smart Playlist & Story Link Aggregator**
- **Concept**: A mini web app that produces a dynamic landing page linking snippets shared via Instagram/TikTok to full tracks/playlists across platforms, with auto-social preview cards (for IG Stories, TikTok bios).
- **Why it matters**: Enhances convertibility from snippet to full play. Smart links already aid music marketing (e.g., Soundplate Clicks). This optimizes discovery from social posts. citeturn0search24

### 6. **Micro-Influencer Matchmaking Platform**
- **Concept**: A matchmaking service for indie musicians to connect with micro TikTok and Instagram creators. It simplifies commissioning UGC, monitors performance, and ensures synergy (trend, genre).

### 7. **Songwriter Spotlight Toolkit**
