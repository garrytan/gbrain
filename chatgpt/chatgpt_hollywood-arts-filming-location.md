## Assistant

No — NeueHouse (formerly "Newhouse") isn't on the same lot as the former Nickelodeon on Sunset, where the *Victorious* outdoor eating scenes (the “Asphalt Café”) were filmed.

- The **Asphalt Café** scenes were shot in the **parking lot behind Nickelodeon on Sunset**, located at **6230 Sunset Boulevard & Argyle** in Hollywood citeturn0search0turn0search4.
- **NeueHouse Hollywood** now occupies the historic **CBS Radio/Studio** complex at **6121 Sunset Boulevard**, several blocks west of Argyle citeturn0search3turn0search1.

In short: *Asphalt Café* = Sunset + Argyle parking lot (now demolished), whereas NeueHouse = Sunset + normative CBS studio building—not the same location.

No — NeueHouse Hollywood is **not** located at The Hollywood Palladium (aka “The Palladium”).

- **The Hollywood Palladium** sits at **6215 Sunset Blvd** at the corner of Sunset & Argyle and is a historic concert venue built in 1940 citeturn0search1turn0search9.
- **NeueHouse Hollywood**, on the other hand, occupies the former CBS Radio/Studio building at **6121 Sunset Blvd**, part of the Columbia Square complex, about four blocks west of the Palladium citeturn0search0turn0search2turn0search24.

So, while both are on Sunset Boulevard in Hollywood, they are distinct buildings, roughly a 5-minute walk—or one short drive—apart.
