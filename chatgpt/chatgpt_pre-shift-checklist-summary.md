### Lemon Drop Shot Recipe

**Ingredients:**
- 1.5 oz Vodka (preferably chilled)
- 0.5 oz Triple Sec (optional)
- 1 oz Fresh Lemon Juice
- 0.5 oz Simple Syrup
- Sugar for rimming the glass
- Lemon wedge for garnish

**Instructions:**

1. **Prepare the Glass:** Rim a shot glass with sugar. Do this by running a lemon wedge around the rim of the glass, then dipping it into sugar.

2. **Mix the Ingredients:** In a cocktail shaker, combine the vodka, triple sec (if using), fresh lemon juice, and simple syrup. Add ice to the shaker.

3. **Shake Well:** Shake vigorously for about 10-15 seconds until the mixture is well chilled.

4. **Strain and Serve:** Strain the mixture into the sugar-rimmed shot glass.

5. **Garnish:** Garnish with a small lemon wedge if desired.

Enjoy your Lemon Drop Shot!
