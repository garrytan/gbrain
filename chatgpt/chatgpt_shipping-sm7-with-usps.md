## Fastest reliable path

**Go to USPS directly.**  
Use the **West Hollywood Post Office** with the self-service kiosk / package drop.

USPS Ground Advantage requires **your own packaging**, so do **not** use free Priority Mail boxes unless your label is Priority. citeturn477701search0

**Why USPS:**  
You already have a USPS Ground label. USPS is the safest place to get the first scan and avoid carrier handoff bullshit. The West Hollywood USPS listing shows a **Self-Service Kiosk** available today, Wednesday, until **5 PM**. citeturn527240search0

## What to do

1. Go to USPS.
2. Buy a **ReadyPost box** or plain shipping box there.
3. Pack it with padding.
4. Tape the label flat.
5. Drop it at the counter/package chute, or ask for an acceptance scan if the line is tolerable.

## Avoid

- **FedEx Office:** wrong carrier.
- **UPS Store:** they may pack it, but USPS acceptance can be inconsistent and you may not get a real USPS scan right away.
- **Staples:** good for buying a box/tape, but the Hollywood Staples result is UPS shipping/drop-off, not clearly USPS. citeturn527240search12

## Packing the SM7 + Cloudlifter

Use a box around **12×9×6** or slightly bigger. Wrap the SM7 well; it is heavy enough to smash around inside a loose box. Put the Cloudlifter in its own padded layer. Shake test: nothing should move.
