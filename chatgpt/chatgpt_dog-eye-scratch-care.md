## Assistant

If your dog scratched your eye, it's important to take action right away. Here's what you should do:

1. **Wash your hands** thoroughly to avoid introducing bacteria to the eye.
2. **Rinse the eye** with sterile saline solution or clean water. Do not use tap water if possible, as it may contain impurities.
3. **Do not rub your eye** as it may worsen the damage.
4. **Use a cold compress** to reduce swelling if necessary.
5. **Avoid contact lenses** if you wear them, and use glasses instead for now.
6. **Seek medical attention** immediately if you experience any of the following:
   - Blurry vision or vision changes
   - Severe pain
   - Sensitivity to light
   - Redness or swelling that doesn’t improve
   - Persistent discomfort
   - Any discharge from the eye

A doctor or ophthalmologist can assess if there’s any corneal damage and provide appropriate treatment, such as antibiotic eye drops to prevent infection.
