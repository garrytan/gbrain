## Assistant

Mr. White,

Urban Outfitters isn’t your best bet for a true, budget black dress shirt—you’ll find mostly trend-driven pieces (their BDG/UO Label button-downs) priced around \$50–70 and styled more casually than a work uniform. For a classic black dress shirt under \$40, check:

- **Uniqlo** (often \$30–35)  
- **Target**’s Goodfellow & Co. line (about \$25–30)  
- **Amazon Basics** (around \$20)  

If you’re set on UO, hunt the sale rack—you might score something closer to H&M prices, but reliability and fit won’t match those other options.
