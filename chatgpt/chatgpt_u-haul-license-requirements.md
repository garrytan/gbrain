To rent a U-Haul, you need a valid driver's license. Here's what you need to know:

1. **Driver's License Requirements**: You must have a valid driver’s license issued by a government authority.
2. **Age Requirements**: You need to be at least 18 years old to rent a U-Haul truck and 16 years old to rent a trailer.
3. **International Licenses**: U-Haul accepts international driver’s licenses if you meet the age requirements and the license is valid.

Always check with U-Haul directly or on their official website for any updates or specific requirements related to your location or rental situation.
