## Assistant

If you kill the hotspot while the update is still in the “Update Requested” or “Estimating time remaining…” phase, **it won’t cancel the update**—but it will pause until a new network is available. Here’s what will happen:

1. **iPhone will stay on the same screen**.
2. You’ll see a **pop-up asking to join a new Wi-Fi network**, or you can manually go to Wi-Fi settings and connect.
3. Once on a new network, the update will **resume from where it left off**—you won’t need to start over.

So yes, you can safely turn off the iPad hotspot and switch to your main Wi-Fi. Just reconnect as soon as the iPhone prompts you or manually through Settings.
