### Common beginner requirements at CA ranges  
- **ID & age**: 18 + for rifles, 21 + for handguns; government-issued ID required.  
- **Waiver & safety**: Sign a liability waiver and complete a safety video or briefing on-site.  
- **Gear**: Closed-toe shoes; eye and ear protection (almost always provided free).  
- **Ammo & targets**: Must buy on-site (no outside ammo); targets sold by the piece. citeturn0search1  

All three ranges welcome first-timers and will walk you through safety basics before you ever pick up a firearm.
