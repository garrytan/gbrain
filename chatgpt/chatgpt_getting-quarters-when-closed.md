## User

Where can you get quarters of the bank is closed

---

## Assistant

You can get quarters when the bank is closed by using a change machine at a laundromat, arcade, or car wash. Some grocery stores and customer service desks at retail stores also provide quarters, often in exchange for bills. Additionally, self-checkout areas in supermarkets might give quarters as change when you pay with cash.

## User

Where else

---

## Assistant

Other places to get quarters when banks are closed include:

1. Vending machines: Insert a bill and press the change return button.
2. Gas stations: They often have a supply for customers.
3. Convenience stores: Similar to gas stations, they may provide change.
4. Fast food restaurants: Some might be willing to give quarters in exchange for bills.
5. Transit stations: If they have ticket machines that accept cash, they often give quarters as change.
6. Pharmacies: Like grocery stores, they may provide change at the register. 

Remember, it's always polite to make a small purchase when asking for change.
