Yes, you should shave before using the Nood hair removal system. This step is important for a few reasons:

1. **Efficiency**: Shaving removes the surface hair, allowing the IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) technology used by Nood to target the hair follicles more effectively. The light energy is absorbed by the pigment in the hair follicle, not the hair above the skin, making the treatment more efficient.

2. **Comfort**: Shaving helps reduce the discomfort that might be caused by the IPL treatment. If the hair is too long, the light can burn it above the skin's surface, which could be painful and less effective.

3. **Safety**: Shaving reduces the risk of burning the hair on the skin's surface, which can not only cause discomfort but also potentially harm the skin.

It's generally recommended to shave 12-24 hours before using an IPL device like Nood. This timing allows any irritation from shaving to subside before you use the IPL treatment.
