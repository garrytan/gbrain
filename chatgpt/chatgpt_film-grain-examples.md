**Characteristics**
- Very fine grain  
- Soft, creamy texture  
- Grain is subtle even at ISO 400  
- Designed for portraits and skin tones  
- Low contrast, smooth highlight rolloff  

**Visual feel**
- Clean film look  
- Slight pastel colors  
- Grain appears almost invisible unless zoomed.

### CineStill Film Grain

**Characteristics**
- Medium-visible grain  
- Prominent in shadows and night scenes  
- Strong halation around bright lights (red glow)  
- Tungsten-balanced (especially 800T)

**Visual feel**
- Cinematic  
- Textured night photography  
- Grain contributes heavily to atmosphere.

### Fujifilm (Fuji Pro / Fuji Superia)

**Characteristics**
- Slightly more visible grain than Portra  
- Grain structure slightly sharper  
- Cooler color palette (greens/blues)  
- Good dynamic range

**Visual feel**
- Natural but slightly crisp  
- Grain has a tighter pattern than CineStill  
- Popular for fashion/editorial looks.
