A 4x4 grid of 16 head-and-shoulders portraits in a perfect 4-row by 4-column layout with thin white margins between cells. Each cell contains one portrait. All 16 cells are VARIATIONS of the SAME person — an aesthetic anchored to YOUNG ALICE EVE at her She's Out of My League / Star Trek Into Darkness peak (2010-2013, ~age 28), pushed slightly younger to 24-26.

The archetype across all 16 cells (consistent identity):
- Bright striking ICY-BLUE eyes, large and slightly almond, perfectly symmetrical, the eyes that anchor the face
- English-rose porcelain skin with subtle warm undertone, no-makeup-makeup, faint pink cheeks
- NATURAL BLONDE — somewhere between platinum-blonde and light honey, often with slightly warmer roots — soft and luminous, not bleached
- Oval face with defined high cheekbones (less gaunt than Knightley, more rounded-elegant than Werbowy)
- NATURALLY FULL lips with a strong cupid's bow — fuller than Knightley, less pouty than Foy, classically beautiful
- Refined elegant nose, straight bridge, naturally proportioned
- Soft naturally arched darker-blonde brows
- Defined feminine jawline that tapers gracefully — not angular-gaunt
- Long slender neck, visible delicate collarbones
- Body: lean naturally curvy classic British model frame — hourglass-leaning lean (NOT the BBL-Instagram aesthetic, NOT skeletal Knightley), natural unaltered proportions, small-to-medium natural breasts
- Age: 24 to 26
- Overall vibe: classic Hollywood-British elegance meets modern editorial — Grace Kelly bone clarity with contemporary supermodel poise

Vary across the 16 cells (styling only — same person):
- Hair length and styling: long with body (4 cells) · shoulder-length lived-in wave (3 cells) · chin-length sleek bob (2 cells) · classic blowout (2 cells) · low chignon (2 cells) · high pony (2 cells) · half-up with face-framing pieces (1 cell)
- Hair register: platinum to honey-blonde to soft warm-blonde across the spread — slight root variation OK
- Expression range: closed-mouth elegant smile (4 cells) · open warm smile with eyes crinkling (3 cells) · composed gentle gaze (3 cells) · soft knowing smirk (3 cells) · slightly parted-lips contemplative (3 cells)
- Angle: three-quarter left (5 cells) · three-quarter right (5 cells) · straight-on (4 cells) · slight chin-down with eyes up (2 cells)
- Lighting mood: warm golden-hour window light · clean midday natural · soft overcast — all directional enough to show cheekbone shadow

Shared (every cell): plain white seamless cyclorama background, simple white tank top OR fitted white tee (low enough to show collarbones), thin gold chain or small gold studs OK, natural skin texture with visible pores, light tinted lip balm only, photographed on Hasselblad medium format with shallow depth of field, slightly warm clean color grade with deep blacks — 2000s editorial color science. Photorealistic, magazine quality, not AI-looking, no uncanny valley. Catchlights in eyes.

Hard rules: same person across all 16 cells, identity unmistakable — only styling/expression/angle/lighting vary. NO modern Instagram filler aesthetic, NO BBL silhouette, NO overdrawn lips, NO buccal-fat-removal hollows. Exactly 16 cells. No text, labels, numbers, or watermarks.
