# Minimal Luxe (recommended)
**Goal:** elevate, legible at a glance, premium comedy vibe.

**Layout (feed 1080×1350 / story 1080×1920)**
1. Top bar (40–56px): small venue logo left, “The Kookaburra Lounge” right.
2. Headline block (center):  
   **WILD WEDNESDAYS**  
   Subline: *Comedy at The Kookaburra Lounge*
3. Date/Time badge: **TUE · OCT 15 · 8:00 PM**
4. Host pill: small circular photo + “Hosted by Katie Cazorla”.
5. Headliner focus: one strong black-and-white portrait (Tiffany Haddish) full-bleed right; soft gradient mask into background.
6. Lineup text (left column, flush left):  
   **Tiffany Haddish** (largest)  
   Charlotte McKinney · Kira Soltanovich · Crystal Marie · JessiMae Peluso · Fatimah Taliah
7. Footer bar: **6801 Hollywood Blvd, 4th Floor** · “RSVP — Link in bio” (or QR for print). Sponsor logo (Juliette) 24–28px high, monochrome, right-aligned.

**Type**
- Display: **Bebas Neue** (or Druk Condensed if licensed) — caps, tight tracking −10 to −20.  
- Body/Meta: **Inter** Medium/Regular.  
- Scale: 44/28/16/13 (Major Third). Line-height 1.1/1.2.

**Color**
- Background: #0E0F14 (near-black).  
- Accent gradient (subtle): #7C3AED → #06B6D4 at 8–12% opacity.  
- Text: #FFFFFF; Secondary meta: #B5BDC7.  
- Date badge: #7C3AED with white text.

**Imagery**
- Convert portraits to crisp B/W; add gentle dodge/burn; avoid cutout collage.  
- Use 1 hero image; keep others in text for clarity.  
- Add 2% film grain to background for texture.

**Spacing & Grid**
- 8-pt grid; outer margin 64px (feed) / 96px (story).  
- 6-column grid, 24px gutters.  
- Keep max line width ~28–34 chars for lineup.

**Hierarchy rules**
- Headline ≥ 2× date size.  
- Headliner name ≥ 1.4× other talent.  
- Venue/sponsor never competes with headline/date.

**Accessibility**
- Contrast WCAG AA+ (use #FFFFFF on dark).  
- Avoid text on faces; minimum body size 28px (feed), 32px (story).
