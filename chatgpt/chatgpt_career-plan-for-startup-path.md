## User

Plan next 6 months career : I want you to lay out a 6-month plan that reconciles the career split between experiential marketing and software, targets a base salary of at least $150K, and maps steps to build a startup-focused path.

---

## Assistant

Mr. White, here’s a clear 6-month path structured to:

- Reconcile experiential marketing + software
- Position you for **≥ $150K base**
- Build toward a **startup-focused trajectory**

---

## **High-Level Strategy**
You’re aiming at a hybrid role category:  
**“Technical GTM / Product / Growth + Startup Ops”** or **“Tech-Enabled Experiential Marketing / Productized Service Startup.”**

Roles aligned with $150K+ comp in this crossover:

- **Product Manager (Growth or GTM-focused)**
- **Technical Program Manager (Experiential / Consumer / XR)**
- **Solutions Engineer / Technical Strategist**
- **Startup Head of Marketing / Growth**
- **Founder path using prior experiential domain expertise + tech leverage**

You’ll build toward both:  
**Employability + Startup runway.**

---

## **Month-by-Month Plan**

### **Month 1 — Positioning & Rebrand**
**Goal:** Clarify positioning, narrative, and materials.

**Actions**
- Create a **one-sentence personal brand**:  
  *Example: “I build tech-driven experiential solutions that scale consumer engagement and bring startup innovation into real-world brand execution.”*
- Update resume + LinkedIn to emphasize **results, systems, and technology**, not just creative execution.
- Write **one strong case study**: experiential → measurable results → tech leverage potential.

**Output**
- Cohesive professional narrative linking marketing + software.
- Updated digital presence signaling **startup-ready, tech-aligned skillset.**

---

### **Month 2 — Skill Tightening & Tech Baseline**
**Goal:** Fix any skill gaps that block PM, technical growth, or startup credibility.

**Recommended Focus**
- **Technical Product Fundamentals**
- **AI + automation + low/no-code stacks**
- **Startup ops tools:** Airtable, Zapier, Notion workflows, analytics.

**Micro-Credentials (pick 1–2 max)**  
- Google Product Manager Certificate  
- Reforge Growth Series (if available)  
- AWS or Azure Foundational Badge (optional but signaling value)  

**Output**
- Ability to speak the language of engineering, product prioritization, and startup growth strategy.

---

### **Month 3 — Portfolio + Proof of Tech Competence**
**Goal:** Build tangible assets that demonstrate crossover expertise.

**Actions**
- Build **3 micro-projects** demonstrating software-meets-experiential thinking:
  1. **AI-driven event engagement system** (chat-driven booth automation, lead capture)
  2. **Metrics dashboard for brand activations**
  3. **Internal workflow automation demo**

- Publish **2–3 short thought pieces** on LinkedIn or Medium:
  - Future of experiential tech  
  - AI workflows for activation scale  
  - Bringing software principles to live brand experiences  

**Output**
- Startup-style proof of work — not just resume bullets.

---

### **Month 4 — Networking + Market Entry**
**Goal:** Get into rooms that hire at $150K+ and value hybrid talent.

**Actions**
- Pick 2–3 focus industries:  
  **AI SaaS, Consumer Tech, XR/AR, Gaming, CPG tech-enabled, Events SaaS**
- Join relevant communities (Slack, LinkedIn groups, Product school groups).
- Reach out to **startup founders + heads of product/marketing** (NOT recruiters).
- Attend **one startup / venture networking event weekly.**

**Messaging:**  
Position yourself as the person who can **translate product → activation → adoption.**

**Output**
- 15–25 warm connections inside relevant tech ecosystems.

---

### **Month 5 — Applications + Income Positioning**
**Goal:** Secure interviews and/or consulting offers while building leverage.

**Actions**
- Target roles with **$150K+ base** (PM, Growth, GTM, Strategist, Solutions Engineer).
- Pitch fractional/consulting offers to early-stage companies:
  - **$4K–$10K/month retainers** for go-to-market systems, brand activation tech, and product-market experimentation.

**Scripts available if needed.**

**Output**
- Active interview pipeline + revenue runway (reduces desperation and increases negotiation strength).

---

### **Month 6 — Execution & Transition**
**Goal:** Land role or accelerate startup formation.

Two pathways depending on outcomes:

#### **If hired:**
- Continue building internal product competencies
- Identify startup-worthy pain points from inside the company
- Validate niche for later founder transition

#### **If independent:**
- Formalize your solution as a **productized service startup**:
  - Pick a wedge: e.g., **AI-driven experiential analytics**  
  - Validate with 3 paying customers
  - Start converting manual services → software processes → MVP

**Output**
- Either a $150K+ role or a validated path to an experiential-tech venture.

---

## **Final Milestone Targets**

| Category | Target by Month 6 |
|---------|-------------------|
| Salary Path | $150–200K base roles in pipeline |
| Skills | PM fundamentals + tech stack fluency |
| Proof of Work | 3 projects, 1–2 published case studies |
| Network | 30–50 meaningful startup contacts |
| Startup Momentum | 1 validated niche + paying clients (optional but ideal) |

---

## Summary

This plan positions you as a **rare crossover operator**—someone who understands product, tech, brand engagement, and experience design. That type of profile is highly desirable in **AI startups, XR, experiential SaaS, and emerging consumer tech**, and can command $150K+ salary ranges or founder-level opportunities.
