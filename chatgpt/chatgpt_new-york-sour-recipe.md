## Assistant

A New York Sour is a classic cocktail that's essentially a variation of the traditional Whiskey Sour, with the addition of a red wine float on top. Here's how to make it:

### Ingredients:
- 2 oz Rye or Bourbon Whiskey
- 3/4 oz Fresh Lemon Juice
- 1/2 oz Simple Syrup
- 1/2 oz Red Wine (preferably a dry red wine)
- Ice

### Instructions:

1. **Shake**: In a cocktail shaker, add the whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup, and ice. Shake well until the mixture is chilled.

2. **Strain**: Strain the mixture into a rocks glass filled with ice.

3. **Red Wine Float**: Slowly pour the red wine over the back of a spoon so that it floats on top of the cocktail.

4. **Garnish**: Optionally, garnish with a lemon twist.

5. **Serve**: Enjoy your New York Sour! 

The red wine float adds a beautiful color contrast and a rich, fruity complexity to the cocktail.

## Assistant

A Negroni is a classic Italian cocktail known for its perfect balance of bitter, sweet, and aromatic flavors. Here's how to make it:

### Ingredients:
- 1 oz Gin
- 1 oz Campari
- 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
- Ice
- Orange peel (for garnish)

### Instructions:

1. **Mix**: In a mixing glass filled with ice, combine the gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth.

2. **Stir**: Stir the mixture well until chilled.

3. **Strain**: Strain the cocktail into a rocks glass filled with ice.

4. **Garnish**: Express the oils from an orange peel over the top of the drink, then drop the peel into the glass.

5. **Serve**: Enjoy your Negroni!

The Negroni is a strong, bold cocktail with a bittersweet profile, making it a favorite among classic cocktail enthusiasts.
