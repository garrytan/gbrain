## Assistant

Mr. White, **worse than the current YC repo description**.

Current YC-ready line I have:

> **Music release software for independent artists, starting with DJs and electronic artists who release frequently and already manage promo lists by hand.**

That is stronger because it says **what it is**, **who it is for**, and **why that customer feels pain**.

### Candidate 1: “Release & promote music at scale”

**Worse for YC.**  
It is cleaner as a slogan, but too generic for an application. “At scale” sounds vague and investor-pitchy. YC will wonder: for whom, using what, and what specific workflow?

Better version:

> **Release and promotion software for independent artists, starting with DJs and electronic artists who manage promo lists by hand.**

### Candidate 2: “AI for music business ops”

**Much worse for YC.**  
This sounds like a category, not a company. “AI for X” is broad, and “music business ops” could mean labels, managers, publishers, booking agents, rights admin, royalties, CRM, accounting, or release promotion.

Better version if you want AI included:

> **AI release ops software for independent artists, starting with DJs and electronic artists who release frequently and manage promo by hand.**

### Best YC description

I would use this:

> **Release and promotion software for independent artists, starting with DJs and electronic artists who release frequently and still manage promo lists by hand.**

Slightly sharper if the AI angle is central:

> **AI-assisted release and promotion software for independent artists, starting with DJs and electronic artists who release frequently and manage promo by hand.**

My ranking:

1. **Best:** “Release and promotion software for independent artists, starting with DJs and electronic artists who release frequently and still manage promo lists by hand.”
2. **Current repo version:** Very strong, slightly less broad because it says “music release software” rather than “release and promotion software.”
3. **“Release & promote music at scale”:** Good marketing headline, weak YC description.
4. **“AI for music business ops”:** Too abstract for YC.
