## User

What would you define a lead bartender as?

---

## Assistant

A lead bartender is typically a senior or experienced bartender who takes on a leadership role in a bar or restaurant. They are responsible for overseeing the bar area, managing other bartenders, creating and updating drink menus, ensuring quality and consistency in drinks, and often handling customer service issues related to the bar.

---

## User

Would you have a lead bartender at a large event?

---

## Assistant

Yes, at a large event or venue with a substantial bar operation, it's common to have a lead bartender or bar manager overseeing the bar staff. Their role is essential in ensuring efficient service, maintaining quality, and handling any issues that may arise during the event.
