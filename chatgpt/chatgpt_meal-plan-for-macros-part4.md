French 75


The French 75 is a classic cocktail with a refreshing and bubbly taste. Here’s how to make it:

### Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 ounces gin
- 3/4 ounce fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 ounce simple syrup (or to taste)
- 3 ounces Champagne (or a similar sparkling wine)
- Ice cubes
- Lemon twist, for garnish

### Instructions:
1. **Combine Gin, Lemon Juice, and Syrup**: In a cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine the gin, fresh lemon juice, and simple syrup.
2. **Shake**: Shake well until the mixture is well chilled, about 15 seconds.
3. **Strain**: Strain into a chilled champagne flute or a coupe glass.
4. **Top with Champagne**: Carefully top the drink with Champagne.
5. **Garnish**: Twist a strip of lemon peel over the drink to release the oils, and drop it into the glass as garnish.
6. **Serve**: Enjoy immediately.

The French 75 is a light and effervescent cocktail that’s perfect for celebrations or as an aperitif.
