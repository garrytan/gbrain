## Assistant

Mr. White, your instinct is close. The issue with **“sounds like we’re negotiating…”** is it sounds a little performative and can make him defensive.

Chris Voss style: calm, curious, slightly confused, and make him improve the offer.

Best reply:

:::writing{variant="chat_message" id="91427"}
How did you get to $100?
:::

That’s the cleanest calibrated question. It makes him explain the lowball without you rejecting it, and it keeps you from countering too early.

A slightly more direct version:

:::writing{variant="chat_message" id="63852"}
Sounds like you’re interested. What’s the most you could do for just the mic?
:::

My pick: **send the first one.** It creates productive discomfort and forces him to either justify the number or raise it.
