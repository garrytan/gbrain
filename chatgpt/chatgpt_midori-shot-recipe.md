## Assistant

If you mean a **Midori shot (cocktail)**, here’s the standard recipe:

**Ingredients:**
- 1 oz Midori melon liqueur  
- 1 oz vodka  
- ½ oz pineapple juice (optional for balance)  

**Instructions:**
1. Fill a shaker with ice.  
2. Add Midori, vodka, and pineapple juice.  
3. Shake well until chilled.  
4. Strain into a shot glass.  

**Optional garnish:** Small melon ball or lime wedge.
