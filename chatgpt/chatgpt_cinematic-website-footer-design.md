```text
Create a seamless cinematic image-to-video loop using the uploaded image as the exact visual reference.

Aspect ratio: 3:1 ultra-wide horizontal website footer background. Preserve the same composition, lighting, black negative space, electric-blue perspective road lines, central vanishing point, haze, high contrast, premium Apple-style/A24 cinematic darkness, and realistic photorealistic look.

Scene:
A deep black void with thin electric-blue road lines starting at the bottom edge and converging into the central vanishing point. Keep the upper center mostly empty, clean, dark negative space for website CTA text. No typography, no logos, no watermark.

Character:
Use the same woman from the reference image as Jovie: young white blonde woman with blue eyes, Swedish/Dutch look, charismatic, confident, warm, approachable, edgy casual punk-inspired style, black leather jacket, dark fitted streetwear, subtle silver jewelry, modern boots, realistic hair, realistic skin texture, realistic fabric. She should feel like a magnetic street musician with fan-connected energy.

Motion:
Single continuous shot. Locked camera. No cuts. No zooms. No camera shake.

Start frame:
The scene begins almost as an empty version of the reference background: black void, electric-blue road lines, subtle haze, central vanishing point. Jovie is barely visible in the shadows at the vanishing point, as if emerging from darkness. Her smartphone is held low at her side, screen softly glowing blue-white.

Action:
Jovie slowly walks toward the camera from the shadows with a confident, charismatic expression. The electric-blue road lines create a soft rim light around her body and hair. As she approaches, she gradually raises the smartphone from her side toward the viewer. The phone screen glows with an abstract blue-white music app interface, but with no readable text.

Final movement:
As Jovie reaches the foreground, she extends the smartphone toward the camera. The phone screen grows larger until it completely fills the entire frame. The image displayed on the phone screen must transform into the same ultra-wide background scene: the black void, electric-blue perspective road lines, central vanishing point, haze, and Jovie barely visible in the distance again.

Loop requirement:
The final frame must be visually identical to the start frame, so the video loops perfectly forever. When the phone screen fills the frame, it becomes the opening background scene again. Make the loop seamless, hypnotic, elegant, and polished.

Style:
Photorealistic, cinematic, premium commercial lighting, subtle atmospheric haze, tasteful lens bloom, high contrast, realistic skin, realistic blonde hair, realistic leather and fabric, sophisticated minimal composition, futuristic but elegant, dark luxury website footer aesthetic.

Avoid:
No text, no subtitles, no logos, no watermark, no extra people, no readable phone UI, no distorted hands, no warped phone, no extra fingers, no cartoon look, no overexposure, no colorful cyberpunk clutter, no background city, no visible brand marks, no jump cut, no sudden motion, no camera shake.
```

Aanbevolen instellingen voor Grok/video:

```text
Duration: 6–8 seconds

Loop: seamless / perfect loop
```

Sterkere versie voor de loop, als Grok moeite heeft met de overgang:
