## Assistant

Here’s a script you can use to handle debt collection phone calls without revealing your identity or incriminating yourself:

---

**Debt Collector:** "Hello, is this [Your Name]?"

**You:** "Who is calling, please?"

**Debt Collector:** "This is [Debt Collector's Name] from [Company Name]. I’m calling in regard to an outstanding balance on your account."

**You:** "I don’t confirm or deny any information over the phone. If you have something you need to communicate, please send it in writing to the address you have on file."

**Debt Collector:** "We need to verify your identity before discussing this matter further."

**You:** "As I mentioned, I don’t confirm or deny any information over the phone. Please send any necessary information or documentation in writing."

**Debt Collector:** "We need to resolve this matter quickly. You could be at risk of legal action if this is not addressed."

**You:** "I understand. Again, please send all communications in writing, and I will review them accordingly."

**Debt Collector:** "We need a good contact number or email to reach you."

**You:** "I don’t provide personal information over the phone. Please send everything in writing to the address you have on file."

**Debt Collector:** "Are you refusing to cooperate?"

**You:** "I’m not refusing anything. I am simply requesting that all communication be done in writing. Is there anything else?"

**Debt Collector:** "We really need to speak with you to resolve this."

**You:** "I’ve stated my preference for written communication. Thank you, and have a good day."

---

**Key Points:**

1. **Stay Calm and Neutral:** Keep your tone polite and neutral, avoiding any emotional reaction.
2. **Never Confirm or Deny Information:** Reiterate that you don’t confirm or deny anything over the phone.
3. **Request Written Communication:** Consistently direct them to send all communication in writing.
4. **End the Call:** If they continue to press, politely end the call.

This script helps protect your identity and ensures that all communication is documented and traceable.
