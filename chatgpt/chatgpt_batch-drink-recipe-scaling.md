## Assistant

To scale this recipe for an **8-liter batch**, we first calculate how many single drinks are in 8 liters:

- **1 drink total volume before ice and soda** ≈ 5 oz (1.5 + 2 + 1.5)  
- Assuming **2 oz soda** per drink, total drink volume ≈ 7 oz  
- 8 liters = 270.5 oz  
- 270.5 ÷ 7 ≈ **38.6 servings** → round to **38 servings**

Now multiply each ingredient by 38:

- **Simple syrup:** 1.5 oz × 38 = **57 oz** (≈ **1.69 liters**)  
- **Whiskey:** 2 oz × 38 = **76 oz** (≈ **2.25 liters**)  
- **Lime juice:** 1.5 oz × 38 = **57 oz** (≈ **1.69 liters**)  
- **Mint leaves:** 3 × 38 = **114 leaves**  
- **Blackberries:** 10 × 38 = **380 blackberries**

**Instructions for batching:**

- Muddle the **mint and blackberries** in advance (optional: strain solids for smoother service).
- Mix the **simple, whiskey, and lime juice** in a large container.
- Keep chilled. When serving, **pour 5 oz over ice** and **top with ~2 oz club soda** per drink.

Let me know if you want to pre-batch the soda or keep it separate.
