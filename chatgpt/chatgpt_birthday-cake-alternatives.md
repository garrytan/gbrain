## Assistant

Here’s how you can handle it smoothly, Mr. White:  

**1. Quick Fix (Store-Bought):**  
Run out and grab a cake, pie, or even donuts/cookies from a nearby grocery store or bakery. Stick some candles in and call it a birthday cake. Nobody cares what form it takes—just that there’s a celebration.  

**2. Airbnb Hack:**  
If the Airbnb has a kitchen, you can pick up a boxed cake mix, brownie mix, or even cookie dough and bake something quick. Make it fun by involving Liv or the group.  

**3. Creative Substitutes:**  
- Stack pancakes, waffles, or even Oreos and decorate with whipped cream/candles.  
- Arrange fruit, chocolates, or pastries in the shape of a cake.  
- Scoop ice cream into a big bowl and put candles in it.  

**4. Spin the Narrative:**  
Play it off as intentional:  
- *“I thought we’d do something different and make our own.”*  
- *“Cake is overrated, this year we’re going gourmet with ___.”*
