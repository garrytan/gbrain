A White Russian is a classic cocktail made with the following ingredients:

- **Vodka**
- **Coffee liqueur** (such as Kahlúa)
- **Heavy cream or milk**

### Instructions:
1. Fill a glass with ice.
2. Pour in 2 oz (60 ml) of vodka.
3. Add 1 oz (30 ml) of coffee liqueur.
4. Top off with 1 oz (30 ml) of heavy cream or milk.
5. Stir gently to combine.

The White Russian is known for its creamy texture and smooth, rich flavor.

### Oaxaca Old-Fashioned

**Ingredients:**
- 1 1/2 oz mezcal
- 1/2 oz reposado tequila
- 1 tsp agave syrup (or simple syrup)
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters
- Orange twist, for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. In a mixing glass, combine the mezcal, reposado tequila, agave syrup, and bitters.
2. Fill the glass with ice and stir until well-chilled.
3. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube.
4. Express the oils from the orange twist over the drink, then use it as a garnish.

This cocktail balances the smoky flavor of mezcal with the smoothness of tequila, creating a unique and sophisticated drink.
