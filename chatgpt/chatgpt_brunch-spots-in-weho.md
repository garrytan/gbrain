- **The Butcher's Daughter** is perfect for those seeking a vegan brunch, offering dishes like a tater tot-stuffed breakfast burrito and jackfruit "crab cake" benedict, along with an all-you-can-eat breakfast bar on weekdays【6†source】.
- **La Boheme** presents a romantic setting with outdoor seating, bottomless mimosas, and decadent dishes like brioche French toast with fresh berries【7†source】.
- **Gracias Madre** is a top choice for plant-based dining, featuring a light and airy atmosphere with innovative dishes like jackfruit carnitas tacos【7†source】.
- **The Butcher, The Baker, The Cappuccino Maker** is known for its Instagram-worthy lattes and delicious breakfast dishes, making it a popular spot for a Sunday brunch【8†source】.
- **Eveleigh** transforms a 1923 home into a charming eatery with an open-air patio offering dishes like the French omelette and breakfast pizza【8†source】.

Each of these spots provides a unique brunch experience, from luxurious and upscale dining to cozy and casual atmospheres, catering to both traditional and plant-based preferences.
