## Assistant

Certainly, Mr. White. Here's a classic margarita recipe:

### Ingredients:
- 2 oz Tequila (preferably 100% agave)
- 1 oz Lime juice (freshly squeezed)
- 1 oz Triple sec (Cointreau is a good choice)
- Ice cubes
- Lime wedge and salt for garnish (optional)

### Instructions:

1. Rim the glass: If you like a salted rim, run a lime wedge around the edge of the glass and dip it into a plate of salt.
2. Fill a shaker with ice cubes.
3. Add tequila, lime juice, and triple sec to the shaker.
4. Shake vigorously for about 15-20 seconds.
5. Strain into the glass, over ice if preferred.
6. Garnish with a lime wedge, if desired.

Enjoy your margarita!
