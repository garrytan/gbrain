# Idea: “AI Outbound SDR for Agencies”

### What it does

An AI system that:

1. Finds hyper-qualified leads for niche B2B agencies  
2. Researches each company deeply  
3. Writes highly personalized cold emails  
4. Sends + follows up  
5. Books meetings directly on the founder’s calendar  

Not a generic “AI email tool.”  
It’s **fully managed outbound infrastructure for small agencies**.

Agencies already:

- Cold email
- Live off outbound
- Pay freelancers for this
- Try to automate this badly

They feel pain weekly.

You don’t need domain expertise. You need:

- Scraping
- LLM orchestration
- Email infra
- Simple dashboard

All learnable in 60 days.
