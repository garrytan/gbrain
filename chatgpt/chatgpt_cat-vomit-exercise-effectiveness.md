Ah, the "cat vomit exercise" is a term sometimes used by Tim Ferriss in the context of fitness, particularly in his book "The 4-Hour Body." It refers to a specific abdominal exercise, similar to the "spinal flexion" movement, which mimics the motion of a cat vomiting. The exercise is intended to work the rectus abdominis (the 'six-pack' muscles) intensely. The name comes from the rounded back and contraction of the abdomen that resembles a cat's posture during vomiting. This exercise is part of Ferriss's broader approach to fitness, which emphasizes minimalism and effectiveness.
