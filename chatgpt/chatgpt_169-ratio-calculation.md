## User

What would a 16-9 ratio be if it's 24 inches high?

---

## Assistant

To maintain a 16:9 aspect ratio with a height of 24 inches, the width can be calculated as follows:

\[
\text{Width} = \text{Height} \times \frac{16}{9}
\]

\[
\text{Width} = 24 \times \frac{16}{9} = 24 \times 1.777 = 42.67 \text{ inches}
\]

So, the width would be approximately 42.67 inches. Therefore, a 16:9 display that is 24 inches high would be 42.67 inches wide.

## User

Other way I need the max height to be 24, and it's a portrait.

---

## Assistant

If the display is in portrait orientation and you want the maximum height to be 24 inches, then the width would be calculated as follows:

\[
\text{Width} = \frac{\text{Height}}{ \frac{16}{9}} = \frac{24}{ \frac{16}{9}} = 24 \times \frac{9}{16} = 13.5 \text{ inches}
\]

In portrait mode, with a height of 24 inches, the width would be 13.5 inches to maintain a 16:9 aspect ratio.
