## Assistant

An Aperol Spritz is a classic Italian cocktail, perfect for sipping in the afternoon or as an aperitif. Here's a straightforward recipe used by bartenders:

### Ingredients:
- 3 parts Prosecco (90 ml)
- 2 parts Aperol (60 ml)
- 1 part soda water (30 ml)
- Ice
- Orange slice, for garnish

### Instructions:
1. **Fill a Wine Glass with Ice:** Use a large wine glass for the best presentation.
2. **Add Aperol:** Pour the Aperol over the ice.
3. **Add Prosecco:** Follow with Prosecco directly into the glass.
4. **Add Soda Water:** Top with a splash of soda water.
5. **Stir Gently:** Mix the ingredients gently with a stirrer or a spoon.
6. **Garnish:** Finish with a slice of orange.

This combination ensures a refreshing, slightly bitter, yet sweet drink, embodying the essence of Italian aperitivo culture.
