A 4x4 grid of 16 different female model candidates for a casting board. Layout: a perfect 4-row by 4-column grid with thin white margins between cells. Each cell contains one head-and-shoulders portrait of a unique person. Photorealistic — Hailey Bieber 2024 / Sofia Richie Grainge / Kendall Jenner off-duty / Emily Ratajkowski / Camille Rowe / Skims campaign aesthetic. Soft natural Instagram-girl photography, warm energy.

Casting brief: ADULT WOMEN, 26 to 30, who read warm and approachable but have full-on MODEL FACES. Each face is "sculpted bone structure with the warmest smile in the room." The cute comes from the EXPRESSION, the architecture stays high-fashion. Think the most-followed Instagram model accounts — high cheekbones, defined jaw, slim sculpted face, but the eyes crinkle when they smile.

AGE — most important rule:
- All 16 women are 26 to 30 years old
- NO teenagers, NO college-aged, NO babyface
- Faint laugh lines at the eye corners welcome

FACIAL ARCHITECTURE — model-grade, NOT round:
- HIGH cheekbones with visible cheekbone shadow underneath — every cell
- defined sharp-feminine jawline — never soft, never undefined
- narrow lower face, slim cheek-to-jaw line — model facial ratio
- elegant straight nose, naturally proportioned
- naturally full lips with a soft cupid's bow
- bright direct eyes, on the larger side, adult and self-possessed
- visibly low body fat in the face — the kind of face you get from being a working model

EXPLICIT ANTI-RULES — these will tank the casting:
- NO round faces, NO full apple cheeks, NO chubby cheeks, NO babyfat, NO doughy cheeks
- NO soft / undefined / weak jawlines
- NO wide lower face
- if a face looks like it could belong to a 19-year-old college student, reject it — push older and more sculpted

Shared across all cells: Framing: head and shoulders, three-quarter or straight-on, face fully visible and centered. Lighting: soft natural daylight, warm and clean — but with enough directionality to show cheekbone shadow, plain white or soft cream seamless background. Wardrobe: simple white tank, fitted white tee, ivory silk camisole, or oversized cream sweater — small gold studs or a thin gold chain OK, no logos. Skin: dewy, glowy, lit-from-within — natural texture with visible pores, no-makeup-makeup look, light tinted lip balm. Hair: lived-in, natural — slight messy waves, soft low ponytail, low bun, fresh blowout, or short shag. Build: lean working-model body, pilates-and-running not bulky-gym.

EXPRESSION DIRECTION — vary across the 16, all warm and adult:
- 4 cells: open warm smile, eyes crinkling — caught mid-laugh
- 4 cells: closed-mouth knowing smile, eyes warm and direct — inside-joke energy
- 4 cells: composed soft expression, lips relaxed, eyes confident and open
- 4 cells: gentle smirk, lips slightly upturned at one corner, eyes amused

Hair distribution:
- 6 cells: warm blonde — honey, golden, dirty blonde, beachy lived-in highlights with darker roots
- 6 cells: light brown / sandy / caramel brunette
- 4 cells: deep brunette / espresso / chestnut

Other variation:
- eyes: vary shape (almond / wide-set / cat-eye, all adult-proportioned) and color (hazel / warm brown / soft blue / green / gray)
- subtle differences in skin tone — porcelain, warm-tan, olive

Hard rules:
- ALL 16 ADULTS aged 26-30 with model-grade sculpted bone structure
- 16 visibly DIFFERENT people, no twins, no near-clones
- warm approachable energy in EXPRESSION only — bones stay sharp-feminine and sculpted
- NO round / soft / undefined faces under any circumstance
- exactly 16 cells in a perfect 4-row by 4-column grid; thin white margins; every face fully visible and centered
- closed-mouth smiles, open smiles, smirks, or softly parted relaxed lips
- white tank / white tee / silk cami / cream sweater only — no lingerie, no swimwear, no nudity
- soft Instagram-natural photography — NOT editorial fashion fierce, NOT glamour, NOT boudoir
- no text, no labels, no numbers, no watermarks

Photographic look: shot on a Sony A7 with natural window light, shallow depth of field, slightly warm clean color grade — feels like a candid iPhone photo by a really good photographer. Crisp on the face with cheekbone shadow visible, dreamy soft background. Healthy glowy adult skin, faint laugh lines welcome. Catchlights in eyes.
