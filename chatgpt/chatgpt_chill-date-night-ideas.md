## Assistant

Mr. White — you’re into laid-back vibes, so here are some *chill* date ideas that level up the cozy energy without needing a party scene:

### Relaxed at-Home
- **Themed movie night + snacks:** pick a genre (’80s sci-fi, thrillers, rom-coms), build a snack board, dim lights, maybe add a projector.
- **Home spa evening:** slow music, face masks, foot soaks, candles.
- **Puzzle + wine/tea:** a jigsaw or brain teaser together makes conversation effortless.
- **Cook together:** pick a new recipe, take your time, share a bottle of wine or mocktails.

### Low-Key Outside
- **Sunset picnic in a park:** a blanket, charcuterie, and a thermos of something warm/cold.
- **Stargazing with a portable speaker:** simple, quiet, can be emotional-good.
- **Scenic walk + coffee/dessert:** easy to chat, no rush.

### Chill but Different
- **Visit a small museum or art gallery:** slow pace, something to talk about.
- **Bookstore date:** wander, pick books for each other, then read a chapter together over drinks.
- **Board game café or chill game night:** cooperative games are great.

If you want ultra low-stimulus, the home spa, puzzle night, or stargazing options are the most “recovery-friendly” vibes.
