## Assistant

Certainly, Mr. White — here are strong example prompts formatted in ways YC applicants actually use. They’re short, direct, and focused on problem, user, and execution.

---

### **01 — AI/Software**
- “AI assistant that replaces 90% of administrative work for nurses — charting, scheduling, billing — with guaranteed compliance.”
- “Verticalized AI agent that handles procurement for manufacturing plants and reduces purchasing time by 70%.”
- “Self-training AI model that learns a company’s internal systems and replaces SOPs, wikis, and onboarding docs.”

---

### **02 — B2B SaaS**
- “Automated compliance for fintechs entering the U.S., replacing consultants and legal teams at 1/10th of the cost.”
- “Universal API for logistics carriers, giving e-commerce brands real-time interchangeable shipping.”
- “Platform that eliminates manual vendor onboarding and due diligence for enterprises.”

---

### **03 — Consumer / Social**
- “A location-based app for spontaneous hangouts — no feeds, no followers — just meetups within 2 miles.”
- “A multiplayer learning platform where users learn faster by teaching others micro-skills.”
- “A friend-matching network for adults moving to new cities, based on shared habits instead of interests.”

---

### **04 — Healthcare & Bio**
- “Remote diagnostics platform for chronic conditions using smartphone sensors — no clinic needed.”
- “AI-driven drug formulation platform that reduces R&D timelines from years to months.”
- “Subscription-based preventative care for men over 40 focused on metabolic and cardiovascular risk markers.”

---

### **05 — Hardware / Robotics**
- “Low-cost autonomous warehouse robots that retrofit existing floors — no infrastructure changes.”
- “Commercial drone docking stations for continuous outdoor data collection and security monitoring.”
- “Handheld non-invasive spectroscopy device for instant food authenticity verification.”

---

### **06 — Fintech**
- “Insurance that automatically adjusts coverage based on real-time behavior and asset risk.”
- “Cross-border payroll that eliminates FX fees and compliance risk for remote teams.”
- “Unified financial operating system for SMBs — invoicing, tax, payroll, forecasting — all automatic.”

---

### **07 — Climate / Hard Tech**
- “Next-gen heat pump that installs in minutes and halves HVAC cost for landlords.”
- “Micro-nuclear systems for industrial heat with subscription pricing.”
- “Software + hardware optimizing energy storage and arbitrage for commercial solar operators.”

---

### **08 — Marketplaces**
- “Marketplace for vetted blue-collar labor with guaranteed same-day placement.”
- “On-demand maintenance network for rental properties with standardized service pricing.”
- “Reverse marketplace where OEMs bid for unused industrial capacity.”

---

### **Common Pattern These Follow**
Each prompt tends to be:
- **Specific user**
- **Painfully clear problem**
- **Quantified benefit**
- **Execution-focused**

YC doesn’t want poetry — they want inevitability.
