## Assistant

Here's your to-do list for setting up the bars, broken down for clarity:

1. **Inventory**
   - Inventory all specialty cocktails.
   - Record on the specialty cocktail inventory sheet.
   - Make a batch if anything is under 2 liters (4 liters for popular drinks).
   - If inventory is sufficient, move on.

2. **Stocking**
   - Stock napkins and straws at all bars.
   - Stock skewers for garnish at all bars.
   - Stock soft drinks at all four bars.
   - Stock soda water and club soda at all bars.
   - Stock Sour Patch Kids at all bars.

3. **Preparation**
   - Uncap all bottles.
   - Load all pre-batch cocktails into the well.
   - Ensure all bars have bar mats (including top bars).
   - Clean point-of-sale systems.
   - Clean bar tops.
   - Stock glassware.
   - Stock pineapple, cranberry, lime juice, and simple syrup.
   - Stock sour mix and grenadine.
   - Stock heavy cream.
   - Stock waters.
   - Cut garnishes.
   - Ensure every bar has a strainer and two clean shaker cups.
   - Dust printers and ensure they have paper.
   - Ensure trash cans are empty.
   - Ensure there are bus bins, dump buckets, and ice bins.

4. **Pre-Show**
   - Get ice.

## Assistant

Here's a logically ordered to-do list for setting up the bars:

### Initial Preparation
1. **Inventory and Batching**
   - Inventory all specialty cocktails and record on the inventory sheet.
   - Make a batch if anything is under 2 liters (4 liters for popular drinks).

2. **Stocking and Cleaning**
   - Stock napkins and straws at all bars.
   - Stock skewers for garnish at all bars.
   - Stock soft drinks at all four bars.
   - Stock soda water and club soda at all bars.
   - Stock Sour Patch Kids at all bars.
   - Ensure all bars have bar mats (including top bars).

3. **Preparation**
   - Uncap all bottles.
   - Load all pre-batch cocktails into the well.
   - Stock pineapple, cranberry, lime juice, and simple syrup (keep refrigerated until closer to the show).
   - Stock sour mix and grenadine.
   - Stock heavy cream.
   - Stock waters.
   - Cut garnishes (send dirty knives and cutting boards to the dish immediately after use).

4. **Equipment and Cleanliness**
   - Ensure every bar has a strainer and two clean shaker cups.
   - Dust printers and ensure they have paper.
   - Clean point-of-sale systems.
   - Clean bar tops.
   - Stock glassware.
   - Ensure trash cans are empty.
   - Ensure there are bus bins, dump buckets, and ice bins.
   - Make sure all bars have bar mats.

### Final Steps (Closer to Show Time)
5. **Final Stocking**
   - Load all pre-batch cocktails into the well.
   - Stock pineapple, cranberry, lime juice, and simple syrup.
   - Get ice.

By following this order, you ensure that all perishable items remain fresh, equipment is ready, and any dirty items are cleaned promptly.
