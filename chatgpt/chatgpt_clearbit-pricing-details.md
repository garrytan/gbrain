## Assistant

**Clearbit (now integrated as “Breeze Intelligence” in HubSpot)** uses **credit-based pricing**, and the exact price often depends on usage and a sales quote.

### Typical starting cost
- **~$45/month (annual billing)** for **100 credits**. citeturn0search0turn0search12  
- Requires a **HubSpot subscription** (Starter ≈ **$30/month**). citeturn0search1  
- **Minimum realistic cost:** about **$75/month total**. citeturn0search1  

### Credit model
- **1 credit ≈ 1 enrichment** (contact or company record). citeturn0search10  
- Credits are sold in packs such as **100, 1,000, or 10,000 per month**. citeturn0search0  
- Credits **reset monthly and do not roll over**. citeturn0search1  

### Example costs by usage (estimates)
- **100 enrichments/month:** ~$75/month total  
- **1,000 enrichments/month:** roughly **$4,500/month** in credits (plus HubSpot). citeturn0search1  

### API pricing (separate)
- Around **$99/month for ~275 API enrichment requests** reported by some sources. citeturn0search6  

### Enterprise pricing
- Larger teams often pay **$10k–$20k+ per year** depending on volume and features. citeturn0search11  

**Summary:**  
- Entry level: ~**$75/month**.  
- Mid usage: **hundreds to thousands per month** depending on enrichment volume.  
- Enterprise: **$10k–$40k+/year** typical range.

If relevant, specify the use case (e.g., **website visitor reveal, lead enrichment, or API enrichment**). Pricing varies significantly across those modules.
