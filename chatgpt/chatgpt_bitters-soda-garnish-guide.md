## Assistant

Mr. White — here’s the clean way to do a **bitters & soda** and what to garnish it with:

**Build**
- Collins glass with ice  
- 6–8 dashes bitters (Angostura is classic)  
- Top with 6–8 oz chilled soda water  
- Quick stir

**Garnish (pick one)**
- **Lemon twist** — most classic with Angostura  
- **Orange peel or wheel** — brighter, a touch sweeter on the nose  
- **Grapefruit twist** — great with Peychaud’s  
- **Lime wheel** — if you add a squeeze of lime  
- **Mint sprig** — extra-refreshing, especially with orange bitters

*Optional:* ¼ oz simple syrup if you want it softer; a tiny pinch of salt (or a few drops saline) to pop the flavors.
