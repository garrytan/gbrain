## Assistant

To get the URL for a Spotify song that is not yet released, follow these steps:

1. **Open Spotify**: Go to the Spotify app on your computer or web player.
2. **Find the Song**: Locate the song using its URI.
3. **Get the URL**:
   - Right-click (or click the three dots) on the song.
   - Select "Share".
   - Choose "Copy Song Link".

This link will be a URL that you can share, even though the song is not released yet. It will direct people to the song's page on Spotify once it's live.
