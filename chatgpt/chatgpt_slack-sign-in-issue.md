## Assistant

Mr. White, here are the **most likely reasons you’re not receiving a Slack login code** and the fastest fixes:

## **1. Check spam/junk**
Slack verification emails sometimes land there.

## **2. Make sure you used the correct email**
If you typed the wrong email or used a different one than your Slack workspace, Slack won’t send a code.

**Fix:** Try signing in again and confirm the email carefully.

## **3. Email filters or corporate firewall**
If you’re on a business or school domain, it may block automated Slack emails.

**Fix:**  
- Try again using a **personal email or mobile app login**, OR  
- Ask your IT team to whitelist:  
  `@slack.com`, `@slack-mail.com`, `@slackhq.com`

## **4. Delay on Slack’s end**
Codes can take **up to a few minutes**, especially if many users are signing in at once.

**Fix:** Wait 5 minutes and retry.

## **5. Use Slack’s mobile app instead**
If desktop/email is failing, open the Slack mobile app and request sign-in there — often bypasses blocked mail.
