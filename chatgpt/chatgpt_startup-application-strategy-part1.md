# Problem (today)
- **Distribution ≠ discovery.** Uploading to DSPs doesn’t create streams, sales, or sync. Artists must manually chase 20+ fragmented channels (radio, gyms/retail, sync, UGC, lyrics, Shazam, editors, influencers).
- **Metadata hell.** Credits/ISRC/PRO/publisher/lyrics timing are inconsistent or missing → lost searchability, lost royalties, lost placements.
- **Opportunity blindness.** No system actively scans for missed placements (apps, playlists, creators, radio shows, retail chains) or preps assets to submit instantly.
- **DIY teams are ops-constrained.** Even with AWAL/Kobalt, artists still handle submissions, QC, and follow-ups. It’s a time sink with low hit rate.
- **AI wave = creator surge.** Millions of new “artists” with zero industry know-how need a turnkey ops layer.

# What we’re building (wedge → platform)
- **Wedge (live now / MVP):** **Jovie** — 60-second **music-first link-in-bio** that converts better than generic link trees (auto-pulls discography, streaming buttons, pre-saves, tip page). Free + $5 Pro to remove branding.
- **Phase 2:** **Smartlinks that learn** (A/B layouts, per-audience routing, deep links, prefetch).
- **Phase 3:** **Lyrics/credits/metadata autopilot** (ingest, validate, distribute to lyric/credit networks; karaoke-timing assist).
- **Phase 4:** **Opportunity Radar** (AI scans playlists, creator trends, retail/fitness programming, radio shows, editors, apps needing demo tracks; alerts + one-click submit with correct assets/metadata).
- **Phase 5:** **Submissions & follow-ups CRM** for artists/managers; later: label/manager dashboards.

# Why now
- **Supply shock from AI music** → more tracks, more noise; operational gap widens.
- **Platforms atomized** (DSPs, UGC, short-form, retail programming, niche radio/podcasts). No single pane of glass.
- **Attribution tooling matured** (deep links, pixels, UTMs) → finally measurable conversion for music funnels.

# Scope (addon to wedge)
Ship as an **optional module** after the link-in-bio wedge proves lift. Start as a **managed beta** for 5–10 artists; productize only if it beats baseline.

# North-star & Kill rules
- **North-star:** **CQL = cost per quality listen** (30s+ stream or save).  
- Secondary: **Save rate**, **Follow rate**, **Tip CTR**, **L2L**, **Shazam geo-lift**.  
- **Kill/iterate:** If CQL isn’t **≥20% better** than artist’s last 30 days of paid within **14 days / $2k spend**, pause and reassess.

# v0 (2 weeks) — ship fast
- **Connectors:** Meta, TikTok, YouTube, Spotify Ads; UTMs/pixels everywhere.  
- **Creative factory:** Auto-generate 12–24 variants from your assets (hook-first cuts, captions, end cards, visualizer, lyric clip, square/vertical).  
- **Targeting presets:**  
  - Prospecting: lookalikes of savers/listeners + genre/interest bundles.  
  - Warm: site visitors, video viewers (25%+), IG engagers, email/SMS lists (Laylo/Jovie).  
- **Budgeting:** 60/30/10 split (prospecting / warm / experiments). Daily caps per ad set.  
- **Measurement:** Geo-holdout for MMO-style lift; Shazam + stream deltas in test geos.

# v1 (30–60 days) — learn & reallocate
- **Bandits for creatives:** Multi-armed bandit rotates ad variants; retire losers on **low 24h p75 CPC or view-thru < threshold**.  
- **Contextual priors (cross-artist):** Start winners by **micro-genre + tempo/energy/valence**, audience age/geo, placement type.  
- **Auto-routing:** Push budget toward platforms/placements with best CQL in the last 72h; enforce pacing.

# v2 (60–90 days) — smarter & cheaper
- **Creative scoring:** Model predicts CTR/CQL from features (hook time, text density, cut count, chorus presence).  
- **Audience clustering:** Cluster interests/creators/hashtags; auto-spin new ad sets when clusters pop.  
- **Cross-DSP feedback:** When playlisted or Shazam pops in a geo, auto-boost that geo/creative for 72h.

# Data layer (privacy-safe)
- **Inputs:** Track metadata (ISRC, tempo, key, energy/valence), creator tags, platform stats, Jovie L2L, Shazam, playlist events.  
- **Cross-artist use:** **Aggregated only** for priors; never expose artist PII or raw lists. Consent controls per channel.

# Creative automations (low lift, high output)
- Hook-first cuts (0–1s identity + chorus), auto-captions, dynamic CTAs, end-screen templates.  
- Variants: lyric, visualizer, performance, dancer seed, cover challenge, DJ edit teaser.  
- Auto-spin **“placement promo”** assets when playlisted.

# Targeting playbooks
- **Prospecting:** Lookalikes of savers/listeners; micro-genre + mood; creator lookalikes (fans of A/B/C).  
- **Warm:** Viewers 25–95%, site visitors, savers, email/SMS, IG engagers.  
- **Momentum boost:** Geo-burst around Shazam spikes, radio adds, or show markets.

# Measurement & attribution (practical, not fantasy)
- **On-platform:** CTR, CPV, ThruPlays, ATC analogs.  
- **Off-platform proxies:** L2L, saves per 1k sessions, Shazam/stream geo-lift, playlist adds.  
- **Design:** Always run **geo holdouts** and **creative A/Bs**; weekly MMM-lite to sanity-check.

# Compliance & guardrails
- Consent + quiet hours for SMS/email; IG DM throttles.  
- Platform policy checks; frequency caps; fatigue detector; brand safety lists.

# Pricing (beta)
- **% of spend + performance kicker** OR **flat + bonus for CQL beats**. Keep simple in beta.

1) 5 design-partner artists; $300–$700/day each for 14 days.  
2) Creative kit intake → generate variants → launch.  
3) Weekly: kill/scale list, CQL report, creative learnings, geo-lift chart.  
4) Gate to self-serve only if **≥70%** of artists hit CQL improvement target.
