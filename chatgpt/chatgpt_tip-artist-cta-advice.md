## Assistant

Mr. White,

For Jovie, the best default is **Tip artist**.

Use this framing:

- **Money type:** **tips**
- **Primary CTA:** **Tip artist**
- **Secondary/section language:** **Support this artist**

Reasoning:

- **Tip** clearly signals a voluntary creator payment.
- **Support** feels warmer and broader, but it is less specific about the action.
- **Payment** and **pay** sound transactional, like a purchase or invoice.
- **Send money** feels blunt and slightly sketchy.

The cleanest setup is:

- **Headline / module:** **Support this artist**
- **Button:** **Tip artist**

That gives you emotional framing plus a clear action.

I would avoid these as the main CTA:
- **Send money**
- **Pay**
- **Payments**

If you want a slightly softer alternative to **Tip artist**, use **Support this artist**. It is stronger for brand tone, but weaker for action clarity.

My recommendation:
**Use “tips” as the product concept and “Tip artist” as the main CTA.**
