### High-Value Questions to Ask  
1. **Studio Workflow**
   - “Can you walk me through your current recording and live-stream workflow from setup to delivery?”

2. **Gear & Technical Environment**
   - “Which Blackmagic cameras, ATEM switchers, and audio interfaces are you running right now?”

3. **Session Expectations**
   - “How many sessions per day does a technician typically handle, and what kind of turnaround times do you expect for edits?”

4. **Troubleshooting Scenarios**
   - “What are the most common technical breakdowns you run into during sessions, and how do you expect the tech to respond?”

5. **Editing Standards**
   - “Do you have established templates or style guides for Premiere Pro and Ableton, or is each project customized?”

6. **Client Interaction**
   - “How hands-on is this role with high-profile clients during sessions? What level of communication do you expect from the tech?”

7. **Growth Path**
   - “You mentioned potential to grow into a studio lead/manager. What specific skills or milestones define that progression?”

8. **Scheduling & Flexibility**
   - “What does ‘flexible hours’ mean in practice? Are sessions scheduled far ahead or often on short notice?”

9. **Team Structure**
   - “Who would I be working most closely with day-to-day—engineers, producers, editors?”

10. **Success Metrics**
    - “How do you evaluate strong performance in this role during the first 60–90 days?”

11. **Culture & Expectations**
    - “What makes someone thrive in this fast-paced environment, and what traits tend to clash with it?”

12. **Training & Onboarding**
    - “Is there a standardized onboarding period for learning your specific equipment and workflow?”
