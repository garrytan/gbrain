A **focus ring tattoo** typically imitates the **manual focus ring on a camera lens**. It usually looks like a circular band with small tick marks, numbers, or grip lines similar to the ring photographers rotate to adjust focus.

### Core Meaning
Common symbolic interpretations:

- **Clarity and concentration** – reminder to stay focused on priorities or goals. citeturn0search8  
- **Precision and craftsmanship** – associated with photography, engineering, or technical work.  
- **Perspective** – the idea of “bringing life into focus.”  
- **Personal discipline** – staying locked in on a mission or path.

Many photographers choose it because the focus ring is the **control that determines what becomes sharp and what stays blurred**, which translates metaphorically to choosing what matters in life.

### Typical Design Elements
Most versions include:

- **Band around forearm or wrist** resembling the lens ring  
- **Textured grip lines** like a real camera lens  
- **Distance numbers or marks** (e.g., ∞, 1.5m, 3m)  
- Sometimes **aperture or focal-length markings**

### Placement
Common placements:

- **Forearm band** (most realistic lens look)  
- **Wrist band**  
- **Finger ring style** (minimal version)

### Variations
- **Minimal line ring** – just textured lines  
- **Full lens marking ring** – numbers and focus scale
