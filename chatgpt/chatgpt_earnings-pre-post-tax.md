## User

I made $37 in cash tips, $75 in credit card tips, and I worked from 5pm to 10.45pm on L.A. minimum wage. How much money did I make pre- and post-tax?

---

## Assistant

Here’s your breakdown, Mr. White:

### 🧾 **Hours Worked**
- **Start:** 5:00 PM  
- **End:** 10:45 PM  
- **Total:** 5.75 hours

### 💵 **Wages (L.A. Minimum Wage)**
- **Rate:** $16.78/hour (as of 2025)
- **Total Wage Pay:**  
  $16.78 × 5.75 = **$96.99**

### 💰 **Tips**
- **Cash Tips:** $37  
- **Credit Card Tips:** $75  
- **Total Tips:** $112

### 🧮 **Total Pre-Tax Earnings**
- $96.99 (wages) + $112 (tips) = **$208.99**

---

### 🧾 **Estimated Post-Tax Income**

Assuming:
- 15% combined federal + state tax on wages + reported tips  
- Cash tips unreported (common, though technically required to report)

#### If you **report all tips**:
- **Taxed amount:** $208.99 × 15% = **$31.35**
- **Post-tax total:** $208.99 − $31.35 = **$177.64**

#### If you **report only credit card tips**:
- Taxed on $96.99 (wages) + $75 (credit tips) = $171.99  
- Taxes: $171.99 × 15% = **$25.80**
- Post-tax total: $208.99 − $25.80 = **$183.19**

---

### ✅ Summary

| Scenario                  | Pre-Tax | Post-Tax |
|---------------------------|---------|----------|
| All tips reported         | $208.99 | $177.64  |
| Only credit tips reported | $208.99 | $183.19  |
