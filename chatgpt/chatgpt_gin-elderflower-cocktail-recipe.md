## Assistant

Here's a simple recipe for a gin and elderflower cocktail:

### Ingredients:
- 2 oz gin
- 1 oz elderflower liqueur (such as St-Germain)
- 0.5 oz fresh lemon juice
- Club soda
- Ice
- Lemon twist or fresh herbs (like mint or basil) for garnish

### Instructions:
1. **Fill a shaker**: Add the gin, elderflower liqueur, and fresh lemon juice.
2. **Shake well**: Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for about 10 seconds.
3. **Strain into a glass**: Use a fine strainer to pour the mixture into a chilled glass filled with ice.
4. **Top with club soda**: Add a splash of club soda to taste.
5. **Garnish**: Add a lemon twist or a sprig of fresh herbs for garnish.

Enjoy your refreshing gin and elderflower cocktail!

### Peachy Keen Cocktail

#### Ingredients:
- 1.5 oz vodka
- 1 oz peach schnapps
- 2 oz orange juice
- 2 oz cranberry juice
- Ice
- Maraschino cherry for garnish

#### Instructions:
1. **Fill a shaker**: Add the vodka, peach schnapps, orange juice, and cranberry juice.
2. **Shake well**: Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for about 10 seconds.
3. **Strain into a glass**: Use a fine strainer to pour the mixture into a chilled glass filled with ice.
4. **Garnish**: Add a maraschino cherry on top.

Enjoy your Peachy Keen cocktail!
