## Assistant

Here is the recipe for the "Summer Scandal":

### Ingredients:
- 2 oz vodka
- 1 oz raspberry liqueur
- 4 oz lemonade
- Ice
- Lemon wheel and fresh raspberries for garnish

### Instructions:
1. Fill a shaker with ice.
2. Add vodka, raspberry liqueur, and lemonade to the shaker.
3. Shake well until chilled.
4. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice.
5. Garnish with a lemon wheel and a few fresh raspberries.
6. Serve and enjoy your "Summer Scandal"!
