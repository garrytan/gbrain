## For the Sigma 85mm Art

Your “Sigma Art f/1.8 85mm” is probably the **Sigma 85mm f/1.4 DG HSM Art** in Canon EF mount. Sigma lists the Canon EF version and gives it a **φ86mm filter size**, with supplied front cap **LCF-86mm III**. So for that lens, buy:

**1 × 86mm front lens cap**  
**1 × Canon EF rear lens cap** citeturn117980view3

## Quantity formula

Let **N = number of Canon EF lenses** you’re selling.

Buy:

- **1 × Sony E-mount body cap** for the a7 III
- **1 × Sony E-mount rear lens cap** for the Sony side of the Metabones
- **1 × Canon EF body cap** for the Canon side of the Metabones
- **N × Canon EF rear lens caps**
- **N × front lens caps**, each matching the lens’s printed **Ø** filter size

For example, if you have **3 EF lenses**, buy:

- 1 Sony E body cap
- 1 Sony E rear lens cap
- 1 Canon EF body cap
- 3 Canon EF rear lens caps
- 3 front caps — one of them likely **86mm** for the Sigma 85mm Art

Check the front rim of each lens for the **Ø##mm** marking before ordering the front caps. Generic caps are fine for resale; OEM caps look cleaner in listing photos.
