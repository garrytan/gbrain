To calculate your target weight to achieve 8% body fat without losing muscle mass, you can use the following formula:

1. First, calculate your lean body mass (LBM), which is your total weight minus the weight of your body fat. 
   \[ \text{LBM} = \text{Current Weight} \times (1 - \text{Current Body Fat Percentage}) \]
   
2. Then, calculate the target weight, which is your LBM divided by the desired lean percentage (100% minus the target body fat percentage).
   \[ \text{Target Weight} = \frac{\text{LBM}}{1 - \text{Target Body Fat Percentage}} \]
