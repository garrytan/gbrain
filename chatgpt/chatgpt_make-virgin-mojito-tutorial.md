How do I make a virgin mojito? 

---

## Assistant

Ah, the Virgin Mojito! A classic mocktail that's as refreshing as a dip in a cool pool on a hot summer day! 🌞🏊 Here's how you'll whip up this minty masterpiece:

### Ingredients:
- Fresh mint leaves (about 10 should do the trick 🌿)
- 1 lime, cut into wedges 🍋
- 2 tablespoons of sugar (or to taste) 🍬
- Ice cubes (lots of them!) ❄️
- Soda water (for that fizzy fun! 🌬️)
- Optional: a splash of simple syrup for extra sweetness 🍯

### Instructions:
1. **Muddle the Mint:** In a sturdy glass, muddle about 8-10 mint leaves with the sugar. This means gently smashing them together to release the mint's oils and essence. It's like giving the mint a little massage! 😌

2. **Squeeze the Lime:** Squeeze in the juice from the lime wedges. Toss the wedges into the glass after squeezing for extra flavor. Lime is the zesty soul of your mojito! 🎉

3. **Ice, Ice, Baby:** Fill the glass with ice cubes. Almost to the top!

4. **Top with Soda:** Pour in the soda water to fill the glass. This adds the sparkle to your mocktail! ✨

5. **Mix it Up:** Give everything a good stir. This mixes all the flavors and makes magic happen. Stir like you're conducting a symphony! 🎶

6. **Garnish:** Add a sprig of mint and maybe a lime wheel on the rim for that fancy touch. You're not just making a drink; you're creating a work of art! 🎨

7. **Sip and Enjoy!** Take a sip and let the fresh, zesty, and minty flavors dance in your mouth. Congratulations, you're now a Mojito Maestro! 🕺💃

Remember, the key to a great Virgin Mojito is balance. Adjust the sweetness, lime, and mint to your taste. Have fun with it and enjoy your creation! 🥳🍹
