## Assistant

**Lymphatic drainage: Fact check**

### ✅ What’s true:
- **The lymphatic system** is real and essential. It helps clear waste, toxins, and excess fluids from tissues and supports immune function.
- **Manual lymphatic drainage (MLD)** is a gentle massage technique developed to help reduce lymphedema, especially after surgeries like mastectomy or radiation therapy.
- It **can temporarily reduce swelling** and improve comfort in people with impaired lymphatic function.

### 🚫 What’s pseudoscience:
- Claims that lymphatic drainage "removes toxins" or "melts fat" are **unproven and misleading**.
- If your lymphatic system is functioning normally, it **doesn't need help**. Your body drains lymph on its own.
- **MLD does not detox your body**, boost metabolism, or cause permanent weight loss.

### 🧠 The science:
- Most healthy people **don't benefit significantly** from lymphatic massage.
- Your **liver and kidneys** do most of the “detoxing.”
- Temporary water weight reduction ≠ fat loss or "toxins leaving your body."

**Bottom line:** Helpful for specific medical conditions like lymphedema. Useless for "detox" or aesthetics in otherwise healthy individuals.
