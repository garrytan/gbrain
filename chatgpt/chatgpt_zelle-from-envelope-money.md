You can’t Zelle directly with physical “envelope money” (cash). Zelle only moves money between bank accounts. Here’s how you can turn that cash into Zelle funds:  

1. **Deposit the cash** – Take your envelope money to your bank or an ATM that accepts cash deposits. Put it into the checking account that’s linked to your Zelle.  
2. **Verify the funds** – Make sure the deposit clears and shows in your account balance.  
3. **Send with Zelle** – Open your bank app (or the Zelle app if you use it directly), select the recipient, enter the amount, and send.
