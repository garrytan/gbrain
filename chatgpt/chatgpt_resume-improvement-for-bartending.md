## Assistant

To enhance your resume for a position as a bartender or barback, consider the following improvements:

1. **Professional Summary Revision**: Streamline your professional summary to focus on your bartending skills and experience. Mention your social media following as a unique asset that could bring value to the establishment through potential promotional opportunities.

2. **Contact Information**: Remove your full address for privacy reasons; the city and state are sufficient. Ensure your email and social media handles are professional.

3. **Education**: List your education in reverse chronological order, focusing on relevant coursework or activities related to hospitality if applicable.

4. **Relevant Experience**: Highlight specific skills and achievements in your bartending roles, such as any specialties in mixology, customer service accolades, or high-volume handling capabilities.

5. **Skills Section**: Tailor this section to the job you're applying for. Emphasize skills relevant to bartending and the hospitality industry, such as customer service excellence, cash handling, inventory management, and knowledge of health and safety regulations.

6. **Physical Attributes**: Remove height, hair color, and eye color as they are not relevant to your ability to perform the job unless specifically requested for a themed establishment or role.

7. **Formatting**: Ensure consistent formatting throughout the resume for a professional look. Use bullet points for easy reading, and keep the design clean and straightforward.

8. **Experience Details**: For each position, include bullet points that quantify your impact, such as "Managed bar operations for a high-volume nightclub with a capacity of X guests."

9. **Certifications**: Clearly list any relevant certifications such as TIPS or LEAD certification, which are valued in the industry.

10. **Social Media**: If your social media presence is professional and relevant to the industry, consider creating a separate section that outlines your influence and how it can be leveraged for marketing purposes.
