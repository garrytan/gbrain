## User

If tips for the night were roughly $300, and I get 12.5% of that, how much do I get?

---

## Assistant

You get **$37.50** (12.5% of $300).

---

## User

And that would be on top of an hourly, and I arrived at 5.30, and I left the tent, and it's L.A. minimum wage. So, what's my total pay?

---

## Assistant

L.A. minimum wage (as of 2025) is **$16.78/hour**.  
Assuming you left at **10 PM**, that’s **4.5 hours** of work.

**Hourly pay:**  
4.5 hours × $16.78 = **$75.51**

**Tip share:**  
12.5% of $300 = **$37.50**

**Total pay:**  
$75.51 + $37.50 = **$113.01**
