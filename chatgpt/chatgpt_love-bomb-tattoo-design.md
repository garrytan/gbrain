## Assistant

Here’s a clean concept for a **"Love Bomb"** tattoo design:

**Visual Elements:**
- **Main Shape:** A classic cartoon-style bomb (round with a fuse) or a WWII-style aerial bomb silhouette.
- **Heart Motif:**  
  - The bomb is *shaped like a heart*, or  
  - It has a **large heart** painted/stenciled on its side, reminiscent of nose art on vintage planes.
- **Fuse Detail (if cartoon-style):**  
  - Instead of sparks, the fuse ends in a **burst of tiny hearts**, like an explosion of affection.
- **Explosion Style (optional):**  
  - A small **cloud shaped like a heart** or filled with romantic symbols (Cupid's arrow, roses, kisses).
- **Typography (optional):**  
  - The words “LOVE BOMB” in military stencil font below or wrapped around the bomb in a banner.

**Style Options:**
- **Traditional:** Bold lines, red/pink hearts, dark gray bomb body, yellow/orange fuse spark.
- **Minimalist:** Clean outline of a bomb with a single heart in the center.
- **Neo-traditional/Illustrative:** More detail, shading, maybe even a sexy pin-up style Cupid dropping the bomb.

Want it more romantic, ironic, aggressive, or surreal?
