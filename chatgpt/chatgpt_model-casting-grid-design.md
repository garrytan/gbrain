A 4x4 grid of 16 different female model candidates for an agency casting board. Layout: a perfect 4-row by 4-column grid with thin white margins between cells. Each cell contains one head-and-shoulders portrait of a unique person. Photorealistic editorial photography — agency comp card / model digitals aesthetic.

Shared across all cells: Framing: head and shoulders, three-quarter turn, same camera angle every cell, face fully visible and centered in its cell. Expression: neutral relaxed expression, mouth closed, calm direct eyes. Lighting: even soft studio lighting, no harsh shadows, no environment, plain white seamless cyclorama background. Style: agency comp card / model digitals / casting book aesthetic — clean, photorealistic, sharp focus, magazine quality. Age range: 18 to 24 years old. Build: lean athletic Scandinavian-coded build, model proportions, swimmer not crossfitter. Makeup: minimal — no foundation, no contour, no eyeshadow, no lipstick. Light mascara only. Natural brows.

Each of the 16 women is a UNIQUE PERSON — vary independently across these axes:
- hair color: distribute across all 16 cells — natural blonde, platinum blonde, ash blonde, honey blonde, strawberry blonde, light brown, sandy brunette
- facial structure: vary jawline (sharp / soft / angular), cheekbones (high / pronounced / flat), face shape (oval / heart / angular / rounded)
- eyes: vary shape (almond / round / wide-set / close-set) and color (blue / blue-gray / green-blue / hazel)
- lips: vary fullness (full / medium / fuller upper / fuller lower) and shape (cupid bow / soft / wide)
- nose: vary bridge (straight / slight upturn / Roman) and width (narrow / medium)
- brow: vary thickness and arch (natural / thicker / arched / straight)

Hard rules:
- all 16 women must be visibly DIFFERENT people — no twins, no near-clones, no repeats
- exactly 16 cells in a perfect 4-row by 4-column grid
- thin white margins between cells
- every face fully visible, centered in its cell, not cropped at cell edges
- no text, no labels, no numbers, no watermarks anywhere in the image

Color: natural editorial tones, NOT warm, NOT cinematic. Clean, neutral color grade that feels like real life but elevated. Slightly cool if anything. High-end fashion editorial look — think Hasselblad commercial photography that pops without looking filtered. Rich but never oversaturated. Deep rich blacks, lifted blacks contrast. Film grain: prominent and visible in background and out-of-focus areas, but nearly invisible on the subject's face and skin in the foreground. The subject should remain tack sharp with clean skin detail. Shot on Hasselblad medium format. Shallow depth of field. Natural light, slightly cool and clean. High-end commercial editorial fashion photography. Not AI-looking. No uncanny valley artifacts. Natural skin texture with pores visible. Catchlights in eyes. 8K resolution, magazine quality.
