Known for its strong flavor and creamy consistency, espresso serves as the foundation for a wide range of coffee drinks, such as lattes, cappuccinos, macchiatos, and Americanos. Originating from Spain, Café Bombón is a sweet and rich coffee drink made by combining equal parts of espresso and sweetened condensed milk. The preparation typically involves adding the condensed milk to the bottom of a clear glass and then slowly pouring hot espresso over it, creating a striking layered effect. The two components are then stirred together before drinking, resulting in a creamy, sweet coffee experience.
