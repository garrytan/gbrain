## Why Nickelodeon abandoned dedicated studios — and where production went

### Why the studio model ended
1. **Cost efficiency**
   - Owning and operating a dedicated studio (like Sunset) is expensive.
   - Renting soundstages **per production** is cheaper and more flexible.

2. **Industry-wide shift**
   - Networks stopped vertical integration.
   - Production moved to **third-party studios** (CBS Radford, Warner Bros., Sunset Gower).

3. **Content strategy change**
   - Decline of multi-camera kids sitcoms.
   - Rise of **animation, single-camera shows, streaming-first content**.

4. **Real estate value**
   - Sunset Blvd land became more valuable for **commercial redevelopment** than TV production.
