## Assistant

A Long Island Iced Tea is a popular cocktail known for its strong mix of spirits and its surprisingly smooth taste. Despite the name, it doesn’t actually contain tea. Here's a basic recipe:

**Ingredients:**
- 1/2 oz Vodka
- 1/2 oz White Rum
- 1/2 oz Gin
- 1/2 oz Tequila
- 1/2 oz Triple Sec
- 1 oz Lemon Juice (freshly squeezed)
- 1 oz Simple Syrup
- Splash of Cola

**Instructions:**
1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
2. Pour the vodka, rum, gin, tequila, and triple sec over the ice.
3. Add the lemon juice and simple syrup.
4. Stir gently.
5. Top with a splash of cola.
6. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

This drink has a high alcohol content, so it’s important to enjoy it responsibly.
