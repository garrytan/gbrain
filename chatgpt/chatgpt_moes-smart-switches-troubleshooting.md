## Assistant

If all your Moes smart switches stopped working simultaneously, there are several potential causes and solutions to consider:

1. **Reset the Switches**: Resetting your Moes smart light switches can resolve issues such as lights not turning on or off. To reset, locate the reset button on the back or side of the switch, press and hold it for 5 seconds until the lights blink twice, release the button, and then wait for 30 seconds before attempting to use the switch【15†source】.

2. **Wi-Fi Connectivity**: Ensure your switches have a strong Wi-Fi signal. If there's a metal box or metal surface-wall board near the switches, it may block the Wi-Fi signal. Also, make sure you've entered the correct Wi-Fi password. The system might take up to 3 minutes to search for the device, connect, and configure it【41†source】.

3. **Other Common Issues**: 
   - **Voice Assistant Non-Responsiveness**: If you use voice assistants like Google Assistant, Siri, or Alexa, ensure a stable internet connection and that your microphone is working. If multiple voice assistants are used, ensure they're not capturing the same voice commands.
   - **Switch Not Connecting to All Lights**: This issue can arise if you're using different applications to control the lights. Using one application is recommended.
   - **Wi-Fi Connection**: Place your smart switch closer to your router for a stronger connection. Unplugging and replugging the switches might also help.
   - **Battery Drain**: If your switch is battery-operated, ensure it's connected to a stable Wi-Fi and positioned near your home network to prolong battery life.
   - **Control Difficulties**: Inability to control the switch properly might be due to improper wiring or an unstable internet connection【25†source】【26†source】【27†source】【28†source】【29†source】.

If these troubleshooting steps don’t resolve the issue, it might be a hardware defect or a more complex problem requiring professional assistance.
