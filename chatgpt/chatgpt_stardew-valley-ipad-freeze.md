## Assistant

Mr. White — quick fixes for **Stardew Valley on iPad freezing** (in order):

1) **Force close the game**
- Swipe up from the bottom → pause mid-screen → swipe **Stardew Valley** up to close.

2) **Restart the iPad**
- Hold **Top button + Volume** → slide to power off → turn back on.

3) **Free up RAM/storage**
- Close other apps.
- Make sure you have **a few GB free** (Settings → General → iPad Storage). Low space can cause hangs.

4) **Update everything**
- **App Store → Updates** (update Stardew Valley)
- **Settings → General → Software Update** (update iPadOS)

5) **Toggle Game Center / iCloud (common culprit)**
- Settings → **Game Center**: toggle off/on.
- Settings → your name → **iCloud**: ensure iCloud Drive is stable; try toggling iCloud Drive off/on (only if you know what you’re doing).

6) **Reinstall (only after confirming your save is safe)**
- If your save is synced/confirmed, delete and reinstall.  
  *Do this last*—reinstalling can risk saves if they’re only local.

If you tell me:
- your **iPad model**, **iPadOS version**, and **when it freezes** (loading save, entering a building, sleeping, co-op, etc.)
…I can narrow it down to the most likely cause.

## Assistant

Mr. White — if you can still move items in menus but the **world/character is mostly stuck**, that’s usually a **soft-freeze** (UI thread alive, gameplay loop stuck). Try these, in order:

1) **Try to “unstick” without losing progress**
- Tap the **… / menu** (pause) → **Exit to Title** (not Quit app yet) → reload your save.
- If you can open **Inventory**, try **switching to another tool** (pickaxe/hoe) and tap an empty tile once.

2) **If you can’t Exit to Title: force close**
- Swipe up → app switcher → swipe **Stardew Valley** away.
- Reopen and load again. (You’ll lose anything since the last overnight save.)

3) **Change controls (common fix for “half-responsive” freezes)**
In-game → **Options**:
- Toggle **Controls** to a different mode (e.g., from joystick+buttons → tap-to-move, or vice versa).
- Toggle **Auto-attack / tool hit location** off/on.
Sometimes the control layer gets stuck and switching modes resets it.

4) **If it happens right after resuming the app**
- Don’t leave it suspended mid-action: **force close** and relaunch.
- Turn off **Low Power Mode** (if enabled).
- Make sure you have **2–5 GB free storage**.

5) **If it always happens in a specific spot/event**
- Avoid that trigger, **go to bed to save**, then relaunch.
- Update Stardew + iPadOS (patch-level bugs are common with specific areas/events).

One quick detail so I can pinpoint it: when it “freezes,” does the **clock keep moving** (top-right time changes), or does **time stop** too?
