## Assistant

Yes—grab **#10 or #12 wood screws**, **2.5" to 3" long**, with **flat or pan heads** (depending on your mount bracket's holes).

**Recommended at Home Depot:**

- **Everbilt #10 x 3 in. Flat Head Phillips Wood Screws (25-Pack)**
- **GRK Fasteners R4 #9 x 3-1/8 in. Multi-Purpose Screws (Contractor Grade)**

Make sure to also get **washers** if your mount’s holes are oversized—**#10 or #12 flat washers** will work.

Bring a **drill with a pilot bit** (~1/8") to pre-drill into the studs for easier screwing and to prevent splitting.
