## User

How can I kill the flys in my Airbnb right now 

---

## Assistant

Here’s the fastest way to deal with them **right now**, Mr. White:

### 1. Cut off what’s attracting them
Do this first or they’ll just keep coming back.

- Take out **all trash**, food scraps, empty cans, and bottles.  
- Cover/put away **fruit, juices, wine, beer, and sugary stuff**.  
- Wipe counters, table, and sink with soapy water.

### 2. Trap as many as you can (with kitchen stuff)

Pick one or two of these depending on what you have:

**Vinegar + soap trap (great for small fruit flies)**
1. Pour some **apple cider vinegar or wine** in a glass/bowl (any vinegar is better than nothing).  
2. Add **a few drops of dish soap** and swirl (breaks surface tension so they sink and drown).  
3. Leave it where you see the most flies.

**Plastic-wrap trap**
1. Put some **fruit, juice, or vinegar** in a cup.  
2. Cover tightly with **plastic wrap**, poke a few small holes with a fork or toothpick.  
3. Flies go in and struggle to get out.

### 3. Kill the big ones directly

- Use a **fly swatter, rolled magazine, or towel** to smack them.  
- Turn off most lights, leave one light on in one area to draw them there, then **close that room** and hunt.

If there’s a window nearby:
- Open the window, turn off room lights, leave outside light (or daylight) as the brightest source, and **chase them toward it**.

### 4. Use spray if you have it

If there’s **bug spray** in the Airbnb:

- Read the label fast, then spray **away from food and dishes**.  
- Keep windows open for **ventilation**.  
- Wipe any surfaces where food will touch afterward.

No bug spray? A **mist of water + a bit of dish soap in a spray bottle** can knock them down, then you squash/wipe them up.

### 5. Make the room hostile to them

- Turn on a **fan blowing across the room** – flies hate strong air currents and will land less.  
- Keep **windows/doors closed or screened** while you have lights on at night.

### 6. If it’s a lot (not just a few)

If you’re seeing **dozens** or they keep coming from one spot (drain, trash area, etc.), message the host and say there’s a **fly problem/infestation** and they need to get pest control or deep cleaning done.

If you tell me what you have in the kitchen (vinegar, fruit, plastic wrap, spray, etc.), I can give you a super-specific “use this, put it here” plan.
