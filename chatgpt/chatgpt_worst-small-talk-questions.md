## Assistant

Here's a list of small talk questions that are generally considered poor choices:

1. **"Why are you still single?"** – This can be intrusive and imply there's something wrong with being single.
2. **"How much do you earn?"** – Discussing salary can be uncomfortable and inappropriate in many social contexts.
3. **"When are you going to have kids (or more kids)?"** – This is a personal topic that can be sensitive for many reasons.
4. **"Have you gained/lost weight?"** – Comments on someone's body can be perceived as rude or offensive.
5. **"Why don't you drink?"** – This can be a sensitive topic for those who abstain for personal, health, or religious reasons.
6. **"Are you pregnant?"** – This is a very personal matter and asking can be embarrassing or offensive.
7. **"How old are you?"** – Age can be a sensitive subject for many people.
8. **"Who did you vote for?"** – Political discussions can be divisive, especially in a casual setting.
9. **"Why aren't you married yet?"** – This assumes that marriage is a goal for everyone, which it may not be.
10. **"What religion do you practice?"** – Religion can be a very private and personal topic.

These questions are generally best avoided in casual conversation, especially with people you don't know well.
