## Assistant

For a classic tequila pineapple garnish:

1. **Pineapple Wedge:** Cut a fresh pineapple into wedges. Place a wedge on the rim of the glass.

2. **Pineapple Leaf:** Take a few pineapple leaves and tuck them next to the wedge for a tropical look.

3. **Skewer Option:** Alternatively, use a cocktail skewer to thread a pineapple chunk and a maraschino cherry, and place it across the glass.

These garnishes add a fresh and vibrant touch to your tequila cocktail.
