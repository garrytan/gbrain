### SOP: Gym and Fitness Routine Management

#### Objective:
To maintain a consistent gym and fitness routine that aligns with Mr. White’s hypertrophy goals and body composition targets using the Macro Factor app for nutrition tracking. 1. **Cut to 8% Body Fat**:
   - **Target**: Reach 8% body fat through a caloric deficit while maintaining muscle mass.
   - **Macro Tracking**: Use Macro Factor to adjust caloric intake based on weekly progress. 
   - **Workout Routine**: Follow RP Hypertrophy program focused on hypertrophy with moderate intensity.
   - **Duration**: The cut phase lasts until you reach 8% body fat.

   - **Duration**: Maintain body fat at 8% for 50% of the length of the previous cut.
   - **Macro Tracking**: Increase caloric intake to maintenance levels using Macro Factor, ensuring no significant fat gain during this period.
   - **Workout Routine**: Continue with the RP Hypertrophy program, adjusting intensity as necessary to maintain muscle mass.

3. **Gaining Phase to 12% Body Fat**:
   - **Target**: Gain lean muscle mass while gradually increasing body fat to 12%.
   - **Macro Tracking**: Increase caloric intake using Macro Factor to support muscle growth, ensuring controlled fat gain.
   - **Workout Routine**: Increase volume or intensity in the RP Hypertrophy program to promote muscle gain.
   - **Duration**: Gain phase continues until body fat reaches 12%.

   - **Target**: Brief cut to reduce excess fat without losing muscle mass.
   - **Macro Tracking**: Decrease caloric intake to induce a deficit, but keep it brief to prevent muscle loss.
   - **Duration**: The mini-cut lasts long enough to bring body fat down slightly before resuming the gain phase.

5. **Return to Gaining Phase**:
   - **Target**: Gain again until body fat reaches 12% after the mini-cut.
   - **Macro Tracking**: Return to surplus calories to support muscle growth.
   - **Workout Routine**: Continue the RP Hypertrophy program with adjustments for gaining strength and size.

6. **Cut Back to 8% Body Fat**:
   - **Target**: Once body fat reaches 12%, initiate a cut to bring body fat back down to 8%.
   - **Macro Tracking**: Adjust caloric intake using Macro Factor to create a sustainable deficit.
   - **Duration**: Follow the cut until body fat reaches 8% again.

7. **Active Rest Periods**:
   - **Frequency**: Two per year, ideally timed around vacations.
   - **Duration**: 1 week each.
   - **Routine**: Engage in light physical activities like walking or swimming but avoid heavy lifting. Use this time to recover mentally and physically.

   - **Routine**: Follow the RP Hypertrophy app for workout programming, focusing on hypertrophy goals (abs, arms, shoulders, chest).
   - **Duration**: 30-50 minutes.
   - Ensure progressive overload and follow any periodization strategies recommended by the RP app.

   - **Meal**: Eat a post-workout meal immediately, ensuring a mix of protein and carbs to aid muscle recovery.

   - Use Macro Factor to track caloric intake and adjust based on weight/body fat progress.
