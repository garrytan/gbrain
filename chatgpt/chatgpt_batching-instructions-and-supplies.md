## 🥃 Batching Instructions

**Prep First**  
- Reserve a kitchen area with floor drains (plastic folding table or kitchen only).  
- Make simple syrup first in a large pot on the stove (preferred) or kettle if small batch.  
- Print labels with the VaultOperations label generator *before* filling bottles.

**Tools to Pull**  
- 22 L Cambros (use for 18 L batches) or 18 L Cambros (use for 9 L/half-batches) – with lids  
- 4 L measuring cup + 1 L measuring cup  
- Large trash can for empty bottles  
- Bus bin or large plastic bin on a bar cart (to hold filled bottles)  
- 2 funnels  
- Bar rags  
- Pump-lid setup *or* 4 L measuring cup + funnel for transferring liquid  
- Big pot for simple syrup  
- Danton 1 L bottles – pull 20 per 18 L batch  
- 20 matching caps (or pour spouts if caps run out)  

**Process**  
1. **Batching** – Combine all ingredients in Cambro.  
2. **Bottling** – Fill labeled bottles immediately using pump or measuring-cup + funnel.  
   - Clean pump between batches to avoid cross-contamination.  
3. Store finished bottles in bar cart bins; move to fridges.  
4. If storing Cambros instead, wrap top in cellophane before lidding to prevent slosh.
