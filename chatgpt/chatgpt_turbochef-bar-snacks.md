2. **Chicken Wings**: Pre-cooked wings can be reheated and crisped up in a TurboChef in about 3-4 minutes.

3. **Mozzarella Sticks**: Frozen mozzarella sticks can be cooked in a TurboChef in about 2-3 minutes, coming out crispy on the outside and gooey inside.

4. **Quesadillas**: Assemble quesadillas with cheese and fillings, then heat for 1-2 minutes until the tortilla is crispy and the cheese melted.

5. **Sliders**: Pre-cooked burger patties can be quickly reheated, and the buns lightly toasted for mini burgers or sliders.

6. **Pretzel Bites**: Frozen pretzel bites or dough can be baked in 1-2 minutes for a warm, soft snack served with mustard or cheese dip.

These options take advantage of the TurboChef's rapid cooking to deliver hot, fresh bar snacks quickly.
