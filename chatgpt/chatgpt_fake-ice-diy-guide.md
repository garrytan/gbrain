## Assistant

For your cocktail photoshoot, you can create your own large fake ice cube using clear resin or acrylic. Here's a quick guide on how to make a large fake ice cube:

### Materials Needed:
1. **Clear resin or acrylic kit** (available at craft stores or online)
2. **Ice cube mold** (silicone molds work best)
3. **Mixing cups and stir sticks**
4. **Gloves and safety goggles**
5. **Sandpaper** (optional, for finishing touches)

### Steps:
1. **Prepare the Mold**: Ensure your silicone mold is clean and dry. You can find molds specifically designed for large ice cubes online or in craft stores.

2. **Mix the Resin**: Follow the instructions on the clear resin or acrylic kit. Typically, you'll mix a resin and a hardener in equal parts. Wear gloves and safety goggles during this process.

3. **Pour the Resin**: Slowly pour the mixed resin into the mold, filling it to the top. Tap the mold gently on the table to release any air bubbles.

4. **Let it Cure**: Allow the resin to cure as per the manufacturer's instructions. This can take anywhere from 24 to 48 hours.

5. **Demold and Finish**: Once the resin is fully cured, carefully remove the fake ice cube from the mold. If there are any rough edges, you can smooth them with sandpaper.

### Tips:
- If you want to add a bit more realism, you can embed small bubbles or imperfections by carefully blowing air into the resin before it fully sets.
- For an extra touch of realism, you can lightly frost the surface of the cube using a matte spray finish or lightly sand it to give it a frosty appearance.

If DIY isn't an option, you might want to contact prop supply stores or rental houses that specialize in photo shoot props, as they might have what you need.
