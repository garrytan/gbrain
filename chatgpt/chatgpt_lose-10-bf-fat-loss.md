## Assistant

To calculate the amount of fat you need to lose to reach 10% body fat from 20% at your current weight of 179 lbs, you can follow these steps:

1. Calculate your current fat mass: 179 lbs * 20% = 35.8 lbs of fat.
2. Determine your lean body mass: 179 lbs - 35.8 lbs = 143.2 lbs.
3. Calculate the target weight at 10% body fat, keeping the lean body mass constant: 143.2 lbs / (1 - 10%) = 159.1 lbs.
4. Calculate the weight loss required to reach your goal: 179 lbs - 159.1 lbs = 19.9 lbs.

Therefore, you would need to lose approximately 19.9 lbs of fat to reach 10% body fat, assuming your lean body mass remains constant.
