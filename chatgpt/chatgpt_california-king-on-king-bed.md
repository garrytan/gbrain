Will a California king comforter fit on a California king on a regular king bed?

---

## Assistant

Mr. White — short answer: **yes it will physically fit, but it won’t fit optimally.**

Here’s why:

- **California King comforter:** ~ 102–106″ wide × ~ 90–100″ long (longer & narrower).
- **Regular King mattress:** ~ 76″ wide × 80″ long.

A CA-King comforter will **cover the width** of a regular king bed and hang over the sides, but compared to a regular king comforter it will be:

- **Not as wide** in “drop” left-to-right (so the sides may hang less).
- **A bit longer** front-to-back (extra length at foot/head).

If you want *full side coverage* and a tailored look, a **regular king comforter** is better. But a CA-King comforter will still work.
