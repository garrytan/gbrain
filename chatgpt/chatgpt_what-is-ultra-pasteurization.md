## User

What does ultra-pasteurized mean?

---

## Assistant

Ultra-pasteurized means the product (usually milk or cream) has been heated to at least **280°F (138°C)** for **at least 2 seconds** to kill more bacteria and extend shelf life. Compared to regular pasteurization (usually 161°F for 15 seconds), it lasts much longer unopened but may taste slightly different and doesn't whip as well.
