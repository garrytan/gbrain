## Assistant

Here’s a solid essentials list for bringing a kitten home when you already have an adult cat:  

**Core Supplies**  
- **Separate litter box** (rule of thumb: 1 box per cat +1, so you’ll need 3 total).  
- **Kitten food** (wet + dry, formulated for kittens).  
- **Food and water bowls** (best to keep separate from your adult cat’s at first).  
- **Carrier** (safe for vet visits and transport).  
- **Bedding** (soft blanket or bed in their own safe area).  

**Health & Safety**  
- **Kitten-safe space** (a bathroom or small room for the first days/weeks).  
- **Scratching post/pad** (helps redirect from furniture).  
- **Kitten-proofing** (hide cords, toxic plants, small objects they can swallow).  

**Enrichment & Comfort**  
- **Toys** (wand toys, balls, small plush toys—burns kitten energy).  
- **Cat tree or climbing structure** (helps them establish territory and play).  
- **Feliway diffuser** (optional, helps smooth introductions with your adult cat).  

**Care**  
- **Brush/comb** (depending on coat type).  
- **Nail clippers**.  
- **Enzyme cleaner** (for accidents).
