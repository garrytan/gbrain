does mike isratel recommend a maintance phase when finishing a diet if you intend to go right into a bulk phase? or only if you are not bulking next

---

## Assistant

Dr. Mike Isratel generally recommends a maintenance phase after finishing a diet, even if you plan to go into a bulking phase. This maintenance phase, often referred to as a "maintenance break" or "diet break," allows your body to adjust to a new, higher calorie intake and stabilize at your new weight. It helps to mitigate rapid fat regain, allows for hormonal and metabolic recovery, and can improve psychological well-being. After this period of stabilization, you can then transition more effectively into a bulking phase.
