## Assistant

Mr. White, **“Plug in. Sync Once. Turn it up.”** has strong rhythm. It feels like Jovie. It sounds musical, simple, and product-led.

My read: **good section headline, weak hero headline.**  
It is too abstract for the top of the page because “Plug in” and “Turn it up” do not immediately tell the buyer what changes. It works better near the setup/import moment: claim profile, connect catalog, auto-sync, fan workflows start running.

The cleaner Apple/YC version:

## Best version of your line

**Connect once.  
Stay in sync.  
Bring fans back.**

Subhead:  
**Jovie imports your catalog, keeps every artist surface current, and turns each release, scan, stream, or show into a reason for fans to return.**

Page fit: **Homepage setup section** or **Artist Profiles “Live in 60 seconds” section.**

Why this wins: it keeps your cadence, makes the product clearer, and lands on the actual business outcome: fans coming back.
