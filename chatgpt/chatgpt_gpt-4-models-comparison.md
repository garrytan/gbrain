## Assistant

Here’s a concise breakdown of the different GPT versions you asked about:

---

### **GPT-4.0 (March 2023)**
- The original GPT-4 model.
- Available only through ChatGPT Plus and API.
- Multimodal (accepts text + image) but image input not available until later.
- Better than GPT-3.5 at reasoning, but slower and more expensive via API.

---

### **GPT-4.1 (Not officially released)**
- No public release or official documentation.
- Some assumed it was an internal update or optimization, but it's not a distinct model.

---

### **GPT-4.5 (Research Preview – Rumored)**
- Not officially confirmed.
- Some internal or developer previews were mistakenly referred to as "4.5".
- If anything, it was likely an iteration of GPT-4 with improved latency or cost-efficiency, but not a new architecture.

---

### **GPT-4o (“Omni”) — May 2024**
- **The latest and real upgrade to GPT-4**.
- “o” = “omni” = **multimodal at its core** (text, image, audio, video input/output).
- **Much faster and cheaper** than GPT-4-turbo.
- Vastly improved **vision, speech, and real-time interaction** capabilities.
- Current default model in ChatGPT Plus (as of mid-2024).

---

### **o4-mini-high (OpenAI Internal Label)**
- A **specific deployment variant** of GPT-4o.
- “mini” and “high” refer to **scale or performance tiers**, likely for cost/load testing.
- Used internally or as part of limited rollouts — **not a distinct model** you can choose.
