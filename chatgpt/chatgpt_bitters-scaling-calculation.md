## User

If I use one 16-ounce bottle of bitters in an 18-liter Cambro, what would I use in a 4-liter?

---

## Assistant

Mr. White, scale it linearly:

- 18 L uses **16 fl oz**.  
- For **4 L**: \(4/18 \times 16 = 3.56\) fl oz ≈ **3.6 fl oz**.  
- That’s about **105 mL**, or **22% of the bottle** (roughly **1/4 bottle**).
