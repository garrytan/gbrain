## Assistant

A Lemon Drop is a type of cocktail, typically prepared with vodka, lemon juice, and sugar. The ingredients are shaken with ice, then strained and served in a sugar-rimmed glass. It can be served as a shot or a mixed drink. The Lemon Drop is known for its sweet and sour profile, balancing the tartness of lemon with the sweetness of sugar, often garnished with a lemon slice or twist.

## Assistant

For a classic Lemon Drop cocktail, follow this bartender's recipe:

### Ingredients:
- 2 oz Vodka
- 1/2 oz Triple sec (optional for extra sweetness and depth)
- 1 oz Fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 oz Simple syrup (adjust to taste for sweetness)
- Sugar, for rimming the glass
- Lemon twist, for garnish

### Instructions:

1. **Rim the Glass:**
   - Rub the rim of a martini glass with a lemon wedge.
   - Dip the rim in sugar to coat it evenly. Set aside.

2. **Mix the Drink:**
   - In a shaker, combine the vodka, triple sec (if using), fresh lemon juice, and simple syrup.
   - Fill the shaker with ice.

3. **Shake:**
   - Shake the mixture vigorously until well chilled, about 15-20 seconds.

4. **Strain:**
   - Strain the cocktail into the prepared martini glass.

5. **Garnish:**
   - Garnish with a lemon twist.

6. **Serve:**
   - Serve immediately and enjoy!

This recipe strikes a balance between tartness and sweetness, embodying the essence of a Lemon Drop cocktail. Adjust the simple syrup to suit your taste preferences.
