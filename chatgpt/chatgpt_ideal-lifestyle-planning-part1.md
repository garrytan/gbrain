## What the company should cover cleanly

The company should cover things that are clearly **ordinary, necessary, documented business expenses**. The IRS standard is that deductible business expenses are ordinary and necessary: common/accepted in the business and helpful/appropriate for the business. citeturn331984search1

| Expense | Company covers? |
