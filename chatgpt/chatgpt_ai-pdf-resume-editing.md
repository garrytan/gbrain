## Assistant

Mr. White, the quickest, most “vibe-coding”-style way to iteratively tweak a PDF resume with AI is:

1. **Upload & Convert**  
   • Use an AI-powered PDF assistant (e.g. ChatGPT’s PDF upload, Adobe Acrobat Pro + Firefly, or pdf.ai).  
   • These let you import your PDF and expose its text for prompting.

2. **Prompt for Edits**  
   • Give simple, natural-language commands:  
     – “Condense my summary to two sentences and highlight leadership metrics.”  
     – “Re-word these bullets to emphasize impact and use active verbs.”  
   • The AI will rewrite in-place and show you the diff.

3. **Review & Refine**  
   • Scan the AI’s suggestions, accept/reject line by line.  
   • Ask follow-up prompts (“shorten these four bullets to one line each”).

4. **Export Back to PDF**  
   • Once you’ve finalized the text and layout, hit “Export to PDF” in the same tool.  
   • All fonts, spacing and graphics stay intact.

---

### Tools & Services
- **ChatGPT (with PDF plugin)**: Drop in your resume, prompt edits, download fresh PDF.  
- **Adobe Acrobat Pro + Firefly**: AI-driven “Edit PDF” pane for layout and copy tweaks.  
- **pdf.ai / AskYourPDF**: Web-based PDF chats—great for quick grammar/style revisions.  
- **Rezi / Zety**: Resume-specific AI that parses your PDF, suggests ATS-friendly rewrites, then outputs a polished PDF.

All follow the same vibe-coding ethos: natural-language instructions → instant in-document edits → seamless export.
