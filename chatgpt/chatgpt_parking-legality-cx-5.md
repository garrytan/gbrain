## User

Is there a system in iOS for me to remember where I parked?

---

## Assistant

Mr. White, iOS already has a built-in “Parked Car” feature in Apple Maps:

- **Auto-mark**: If your phone disconnects from CarPlay or a paired Bluetooth car, Maps drops a “Parked Car” pin.  
- **Manual mark**: In Maps tap the blue location dot → “Mark My Location.”  
- **Widget**: Add the Parked Car widget to your Today View for one-tap directions back.  

If you want more control, you can create a Shortcuts Automation:  
1. In Shortcuts → Automation → “When CarPlay disconnects.”  
2. Add actions: “Get Current Location” → “Save to Notes” (or Reminders, or log in a chosen app).  

That gives you a timestamped record every time you leave your car.
