## Assistant

Mr. White, here’s the classic Negroni—equal parts, no fuss:

- **1 oz gin**  
- **1 oz Campari**  
- **1 oz sweet vermouth**  

**Method:**  
1. Combine ingredients in a mixing glass.  
2. Fill with ice and stir ~30 s until well-chilled.  
3. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube.  
4. Express an orange peel over the drink, rim the glass, then drop it in.  

Cheers.
