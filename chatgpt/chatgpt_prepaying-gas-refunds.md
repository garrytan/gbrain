## User

If you prepay gas at a pump, what happens if you prepay more than will fit in your car?

---

## Assistant

Mr. White, if you prepay more than your tank can hold:

- **Pump shut-off**  
  Once your tank’s full, the nozzle clicks off and you can’t pump any more gas.

- **You only pay for what you pump**  
  - **Credit/debit card:** the station “pre-authorizes” up to your prepaid amount, but only the actual gallons get billed; the unused hold is released back to your account (usually within 1–7 business days).  
  - **Cash prepay:** the pump will take only the dollars worth of gas you pump; you’ll need to go inside and ask for a refund or get a voucher for the balance.

- **Station policies vary**  
  Some places automatically issue a cash refund; others give you a credit slip or hold the balance for your next fill-up. Always check the posted instructions at the pump or ask the cashier.
