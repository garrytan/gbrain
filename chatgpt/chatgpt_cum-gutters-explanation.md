## Assistant

“Cum gutters” is a slang term for the **iliac furrows**—the v-shaped lines that run from the hip bones down toward the groin. They’re formed by well-developed lower abdominals and obliques, along with low body fat. Anatomically, they trace along the **inguinal ligament** and are visible when someone is lean and muscular.
