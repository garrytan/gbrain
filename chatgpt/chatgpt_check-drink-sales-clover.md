On the Clover point-of-sale system, how can I see how many of each drink was ordered?

---

## Assistant

To see how many of each drink was ordered on the Clover point-of-sale system, follow these steps:

1. **Log In to Your Clover Dashboard:**
   - Open your Clover app or access the Clover dashboard via a web browser.
   - Log in with your credentials.

2. **Access the Reports Section:**
   - On the dashboard, navigate to the "Reports" section.
   - This is usually found in the menu on the left-hand side.

3. **Select the Sales Report:**
   - Within the Reports section, look for "Sales" or "Item Sales."
   - Click on "Item Sales" to view detailed sales reports.

4. **Filter for the Desired Time Frame:**
   - Choose the time frame for which you want to see the sales data (e.g., today, yesterday, last week, custom date range).

5. **View Itemized Sales:**
   - The Item Sales report will display a breakdown of all items sold.
   - Look for the drinks section or filter by category if your drinks are categorized separately.

6. **Review the Quantity Sold:**
   - The report will list each drink and the number of units sold.
   - You can see the total quantities and, often, additional details like total sales amount and average price.

By following these steps, you should be able to see how many of each drink was ordered using the Clover point-of-sale system.
