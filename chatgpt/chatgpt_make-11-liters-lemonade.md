## Assistant

Here's a simple recipe to make 11 liters of lemonade:

### Ingredients:
- 2.75 liters of freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 55 lemons)
- 5.5 liters of water
- 2.75 kilograms of sugar (about 13 cups)

### Instructions:
1. **Juice the Lemons**:
   - Squeeze the lemons to get 2.75 liters of lemon juice. Strain the juice to remove seeds and pulp if desired.

2. **Dissolve the Sugar**:
   - In a large pot, combine the sugar with 1.5 liters of water. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring continuously until the sugar is completely dissolved, forming a simple syrup. Allow the syrup to cool.

3. **Mix Lemon Juice and Syrup**:
   - In a large container, mix the cooled simple syrup with the lemon juice.

4. **Add Water**:
   - Add the remaining 4 liters of water to the mixture. Stir well to combine.

5. **Chill and Serve**:
   - Refrigerate the lemonade until it is cold. Serve over ice for a refreshing drink.

Enjoy your homemade lemonade!
