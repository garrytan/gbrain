Mr. White, it is **not fully resolved**.

On current iPhone software, Messages can send photo previews first and then full-resolution images afterward, and if **Low Data Mode** is on, **only the preview** is sent. Apple also still treats **AirDrop** differently: in the Photos share options, **“All Photos Data”** sends the **original file with edit history and metadata**, and Apple says that option is available with **AirDrop and iCloud links only**. citeturn2search2turn2search3turn1search3

- **iMessage between iPhones:** usually much better than old-school MMS and often looks fine, but it is **still not the same guarantee as AirDrop**. citeturn2search2turn2search3
- **AirDrop:** best option when you want the **originals** preserved. citeturn2search3turn1search3
