- 0.5 ounces of lime juice
- 0.5 ounces of simple syrup

\[ 1.5 + 4 + 0.5 + 0.5 + 0.5 = 7 \, \text{ounces} \]

\[ 10 \, \text{liters} \times 33.814 \, \text{ounces per liter} = 338.14 \, \text{ounces} \]

**Number of Drinks:**
\[ \frac{338.14 \, \text{ounces}}{7 \, \text{ounces per drink}} \approx 48.31 \, \text{drinks} \]
