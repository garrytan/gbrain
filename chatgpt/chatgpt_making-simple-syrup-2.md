Concisely explain how to make 4-liters a simple syrup.

---

## Assistant

To make 4 liters of simple syrup (1:1 ratio):  

1. Combine **2 liters of water** and **2 kilograms of sugar** in a large pot.  
2. Heat gently, stirring until all sugar dissolves.  
3. Remove from heat—do **not** boil.  
4. Let cool, then transfer to a clean container or bottles.  

For richer syrup (2:1 ratio), use **2 liters water + 4 kilograms sugar** to yield roughly 4 liters of syrup.
