**Electron (physics)**  
An **electron** is a fundamental **subatomic particle** with a **negative electric charge** and is one of the basic building blocks of ordinary matter. It is an **elementary particle**—meaning it isn’t made of smaller components—and is a core part of atoms along with protons and neutrons. citeturn0search1turn0search3
