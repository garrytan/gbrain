In California, it's illegal to drive while under the influence of marijuana. This includes smoking or consuming cannabis in any form while driving or operating a vehicle. The laws are similar to those regarding driving under the influence of alcohol. If caught, you could face DUI charges.

## User

What about in Massachusetts?

---

## Assistant

In Massachusetts, it's also illegal to drive under the influence of marijuana. Similar to alcohol, you can be charged with operating under the influence (OUI) if you're found driving while impaired by cannabis. This applies even if you possess a legal amount of marijuana or are of legal age to use it.

## User

What about in New York?

---

## Assistant

In New York, it's illegal to drive while impaired by marijuana. Driving under the influence of any drug, including cannabis, can result in a charge of Driving While Ability Impaired by Drugs (DWAI-Drug). It's also illegal to have an open container of cannabis in the passenger area of a vehicle while on the road.
