## Assistant

Renaissance Periodization (RP) recommends different rates of gain based on the individual's training status. For beginners, a monthly weight gain of about 1-1.5% of body weight is advisable. Intermediates might aim for a 0.5-1% increase per month, and advanced lifters should consider a slower rate of 0.25-0.5% per month. These guidelines help balance muscle gain with minimizing fat accumulation during a bulk. Always adjust based on personal progress and goals.
