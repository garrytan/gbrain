There are several alternatives to Mood Media and PlayNetwork for commercial music streaming and background music solutions. Here are a few options:

2. **Pandora for Business**: Provides commercial-friendly music streaming with licensed music for businesses.

3. **Spotify for Business**: Allows businesses to use Spotify's extensive music library with proper licensing for commercial use.

4. **Rockbot**: A customizable music streaming service for businesses that also allows customer interaction with the playlist.

5. **Cloud Cover Music**: Offers a variety of music genres and playlists, all properly licensed for business use, with easy scheduling and control.

6. **Qsic**: An intelligent in-store music and audio management system that allows businesses to curate their own playlists.

These services typically offer various features such as remote management, custom playlists, and integration with existing audio systems to provide a seamless background music experience for businesses.

To get your new song "Never Say A Word" played in stores, you can consider the following strategies:

1. **Submit to Music Aggregators**:
   - **Mood Media**: They offer a submission process for artists to get their music considered for in-store play.
   - **PlayNetwork**: They also accept submissions from artists and labels for in-store music programming.
   
2. **Use Digital Distribution Services**:
   - **CD Baby**: They have partnerships with various in-store music providers and can help distribute your music to these platforms.
   - **TuneCore**: Similar to CD Baby, TuneCore can distribute your music to a wide range of platforms, including in-store music services.

3. **Direct Submission to In-Store Music Services**:
   - **Soundtrack Your Brand**: Check if they have a submission process for independent artists.
   - **Rockbot**: Some services like Rockbot allow artists to submit their music directly for consideration.

4. **Partner with Brands**:
   - Approach brands or retail chains directly and propose including your song in their in-store playlists. Highlight how your song's vibe matches their brand identity.

5. **Music Licensing Companies**:
   - **Music Dealers**: They specialize in licensing music for commercial use, including in-store play.
   - **Audiosocket**: They can help license your music for use in retail environments.

6. **Networking and Promotion**:
   - Leverage your existing network to connect with store managers or decision-makers in retail chains.
   - Promote your song to DJs or music programmers who curate in-store playlists.

Ensuring that your song is properly tagged and metadata is complete will help in the submission and selection process.
