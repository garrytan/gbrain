## Assistant

Fastest way in **Capture One** to make the ocean look cinematic blue:

1. **Color Editor – Basic Tool**  
   - Grab the **Color Editor > Basic** tool.  
   - Click directly on the ocean with the color picker.  
   - Shift the **Hue** slightly toward teal/blue.  
   - Increase **Saturation**.  
   - Lower **Lightness** just a bit for depth.

2. **Color Balance – Midtones**  
   - In the **Color Balance** tool, drag the **Midtones wheel** toward cyan/teal.  
   - This gives that film-like cinematic toning.

3. **Levels/Curves**  
   - Add contrast with **Luma Curve**.  
   - In **RGB Curves**, pull down the **Red curve** in shadows and lift the **Blue curve** in shadows for a subtle teal push.

4. **Optional Gradient Mask**  
   - If sky and ocean need separation, use a **linear gradient mask** over the water.  
   - Apply the color grading only to that mask for precision.

That combo (color picker → hue shift → saturation → color balance tweak) is the fastest way to get that cinematic ocean look.
