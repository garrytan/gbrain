- Vodka: 1.5 oz
- Raspberry liqueur (Chambord): 1 oz
- Lemonade: 4 oz

### Total Volume per Serving:
1.5 oz + 1 oz + 4 oz = 6.5 oz

To find the scaling factor:
\[ \text{Scaling Factor} = \frac{9000 \text{ mL}}{6.5 \text{ oz} \times 29.5735 \text{ mL/oz}} \]
\[ \text{Scaling Factor} \approx \frac{9000}{192.22775} \approx 46.82 \]

Now, multiply each ingredient by the scaling factor.

### Scaled Quantities:
- Vodka: \( 1.5 \text{ oz} \times 46.82 \approx 70.23 \text{ oz} \)
- Raspberry liqueur (Chambord): \( 1 \text{ oz} \times 46.82 \approx 46.82 \text{ oz} \)
- Lemonade: \( 4 \text{ oz} \times 46.82 \approx 187.28 \text{ oz} \)

Convert these quantities to liters (1 oz = 0.0295735 L):
- Vodka: \( 70.23 \text{ oz} \times 0.0295735 \text{ L/oz} \approx 2.08 \text{ L} \)
- Raspberry liqueur (Chambord): \( 46.82 \text{ oz} \times 0.0295735 \text{ L/oz} \approx 1.38 \text{ L} \)
- Lemonade: \( 187.28 \text{ oz} \times 0.0295735 \text{ L/oz} \approx 5.54 \text{ L} \)

### Final Batch Recipe (9L):
