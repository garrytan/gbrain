## User

what about framing as like your ai music manager? or artist manager?

---

## Assistant

Mr. White — **yes, “AI artist manager” is a stronger customer-facing frame** than “vertical AI” or “release workflow automation.”

The core tradeoff:

**“AI artist manager”** is emotionally obvious. Artists instantly understand: someone helping me stay organized, release consistently, promote, make decisions, and keep momentum.

**“AI music manager”** sounds slightly off. People usually say **artist manager**, **music manager**, or **manager**, but “AI music manager” can sound like it manages files/music libraries.

**“AI release manager”** is more precise, but less ambitious. It frames Jovie as a release ops tool rather than the career operating system.

My preferred positioning:

# **Your AI artist manager.**

Subhead:

**Jovie automates the planning, coordination, and promotion work behind every release — so you can drop more music, learn what hits, and build momentum before you burn out.**

That is clean, understandable, and ambitious.

A sharper version:

# **The AI artist manager that keeps you releasing.**

**Jovie turns every song into a release plan, automates the manual work, and helps you build compounding momentum before time, money, or motivation runs out.**

Even better if you want to own the release cadence thesis:

# **Your AI artist manager for every release.**

**Plan drops, generate assets, coordinate rollout tasks, and keep your career moving — without drowning in release admin.**

I’d avoid making the H1 only **“Your AI artist manager”** if the product does not truly cover broader manager duties like booking, deal negotiation, fan strategy, revenue, partnerships, and career decisions. You can still use it, but the subhead needs to anchor the promise around releases.

Best final hero in my view:

# **Your AI artist manager for every release.**

**Jovie automates the manual work behind each drop, helping you release faster, test what works, and build the momentum artists need to survive.**

CTA: **Plan my next release**

Secondary: **See how Jovie works**

A more aggressive founder-mode version:

# **Your AI artist manager. Built to keep you alive.**

**Jovie helps artists release faster, automate the busywork, and compound momentum before money, time, or willpower runs out.**

That one is memorable, but “built to keep you alive” may feel too intense for the homepage unless the whole brand leans into the “don’t die” protocol.
