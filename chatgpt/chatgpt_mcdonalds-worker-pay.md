---

### ✅ Why McDonald’s Is Covered
- **Chain size requirement:** The law applies to any “fast food restaurant chain” with **60 or more establishments nationwide**.  
  - McDonald’s has over **13,000 U.S. locations**, so it easily qualifies.

- **Ownership structure:** It applies to **both corporate-owned and franchise-owned** McDonald’s restaurants in California. Franchisees aren’t exempt just because they’re independently operated.  
  - The only exceptions are restaurants **inside grocery stores, airports, or similar facilities** that meet narrow exclusion criteria.  

---

### 💵 Result
All McDonald’s restaurants in California — including Los Angeles — must pay **at least $20.00/hour** to covered employees (crew members, cooks, cashiers, etc.) as of **April 1, 2024**.
