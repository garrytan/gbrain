A "Sea Breeze" is a classic cocktail made with vodka, cranberry juice, and grapefruit juice. Here’s how you can make it:

### Sea Breeze Cocktail Recipe

#### Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 ounces vodka
- 3 ounces cranberry juice
- 1 1/2 ounces grapefruit juice
- Lime wedge or a slice of grapefruit for garnish

#### Instructions:
1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
2. Pour the vodka over the ice.
3. Add the cranberry juice and grapefruit juice.
4. Stir gently to combine.
5. Garnish with a lime wedge or a slice of grapefruit.
6. Serve and enjoy!
