### **FollowsBack** (Web Tool – Browser)  
- **Key Features:** FollowsBack.com offers a **web-based follower analysis** that requires no login and **no installation**. - **Privacy & Security:** Extremely privacy-friendly – **all processing is done locally in your web browser**. This means the follower data in your ZIP never gets uploaded to a server; the website’s JavaScript just runs on your machine to parse the lists. Because of this design, your Instagram data cannot be misused or saved by the service.  
- **Limitations:** Since it only analyzes the snapshot you provide, FollowsBack mainly identifies one-way follows (people you follow who aren’t following back). It doesn’t automatically track over time or list who unfollowed you *when* – you’d have to download a new data file later and re-check to see new changes. The site does advertise a “Pro” option (daily reports, notifications, etc.), which likely involves more automation (possibly requiring some form of account connection), but the basic free tool is manual and local. Also, very large accounts might find the manual data download cumbersome (and potentially slow to process in-browser if you have tens of thousands of entries). For most personal or small-business accounts, however, FollowsBack provides a quick, safe snapshot of your non-mutual follows.
