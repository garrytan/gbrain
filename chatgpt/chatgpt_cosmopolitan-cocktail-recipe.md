## Assistant

Here's a classic Cosmopolitan recipe:

### Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 oz vodka
- 1 oz Cointreau or triple sec
- 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
- 1/4 oz cranberry juice
- Lime twist or lemon twist, for garnish

### Instructions:
1. **Chill a martini glass**: Fill it with ice water and set it aside while you prepare the drink.
2. **Mix the ingredients**: In a cocktail shaker, combine the vodka, Cointreau, lime juice, and cranberry juice. Add a handful of ice.
3. **Shake**: Shake vigorously for about 15 seconds until the shaker is frosty.
4. **Strain**: Discard the ice water from the martini glass. Strain the mixture into the chilled glass.
5. **Garnish**: Add a twist of lime or lemon for garnish.

Enjoy your Cosmopolitan!
