Mr. White — from what you describe (two gold charging contacts on the bottom), you almost certainly have one of Arlo’s magnetic-charging models: Ultra/Ultra 2, Pro 3/4/5S, Go 2 or the Pro 3 Floodlight. These use Arlo’s snap-on magnetic cable (not USB) for in-place charging. citeturn0search0turn0search5

Good news: on those models the battery is still removable/swappable. You can pop the camera out of its shell with the release button next to the charging port and swap the pack; Arlo also sells a dual battery charger. citeturn1search13turn2search0

The only Arlo cams with **non-removable** batteries are the Essential series (including Essential/Essential Spotlight/Essential XL and the newer Essential 2nd/3rd gen), which charge via **micro-USB**, not the two-pin magnetic contact—so that doesn’t match what you’re seeing. citeturn0search1turn0search4

### How to confirm the exact model (fastest)
- In the Arlo app: **Settings → My Devices → [your camera] → Device Info** to see the model number. citeturn0search12

### If you need the charger
- Arlo Indoor Magnetic Cable (8 ft): **VMA5000C**; Outdoor Magnetic Cable (25 ft): **VMA5600C**. Both work with Ultra/Ultra 2, Pro 3/4/5S, Go 2, etc. citeturn0search5turn0search0
- Optional: **Dual Charging Station (VMA5400C)** to charge spare batteries off-camera. citeturn2search0
