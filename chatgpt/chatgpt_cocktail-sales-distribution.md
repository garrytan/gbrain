## Assistant

Let’s estimate the liquid needed per drink.

**1. Glass size**  
Typical “standard wine glass” ≈ **12 oz** capacity.

**2. Ice displacement**  
Filling with ice leaves roughly **half the total volume for liquid**.  
- So usable liquid space ≈ **6 oz**.

**3. Pour rules**  
- **Non-carbonated cocktails:** fill **¾ of the cup** with the mixed drink.  
  *¾ × 6 oz ≈ 4.5 oz liquid* per serving.
- **With carbonated mixer:** pour only **½ of that amount** of base liquor/mix, then top to the same ¾ mark with soda.  
  Base ≈ **2.25 oz**, soda ≈ **2.25 oz** → total still about **4.5 oz**.

**4. Total volume for 2,500 drinks**  
Average liquid per cocktail ≈ **4.5 oz**  
→ 2,500 × 4.5 oz = **11,250 oz total liquid**  
→ **~88 gallons** (11,250 ÷ 128).

**5. Per cocktail type (equal split, 417 ea.)**  
417 × 4.5 oz ≈ **1,876 oz**  
→ **~14.7 gallons** of total liquid each.
