## Assistant

For a classic Bellini cocktail, here's a simple recipe:

### Ingredients:
- 2 oz Peach purée or peach nectar
- 4 oz Prosecco (chilled)

### Instructions:
1. **Prepare the Peach Purée:** If you're using fresh peaches, blend peeled peaches until smooth to make purée. You can strain it for a smoother texture or leave it as is for more body.
2. **Mix the Drink:** Pour the peach purée into a chilled champagne flute. It should fill about one-third of the glass.
3. **Add Prosecco:** Gently pour the Prosecco over the peach purée. Pour slowly to prevent the Prosecco from overflowing.
4. **Stir Gently:** Lightly stir to mix the purée with the Prosecco. Be careful not to stir too vigorously to avoid losing the bubbles.
5. **Serve Immediately:** Garnish with a peach slice or a raspberry for an extra touch and serve immediately.

### Tips:
- For the best taste, use ripe peaches for the purée. If peaches are not in season, high-quality canned peach nectar is a good alternative.
- You can adjust the ratio of peach purée to Prosecco based on your taste preference. Some enjoy a sweeter drink with more purée, while others prefer a dryer cocktail with more Prosecco.
- Always chill your Prosecco and serving glasses before making the Bellini to ensure it's refreshingly cold.

Enjoy your classic Bellini!

For a classic Cosmopolitan cocktail, here’s how to make it:

### Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 oz Vodka (preferably citrus-flavored)
- 1 oz Cranberry juice
- 1/2 oz Cointreau (or any good quality triple sec)
- 1/2 oz Fresh lime juice
- Ice
- Lime wheel or twist, for garnish

### Instructions:
1. **Chill the Glass:** Start by chilling your martini glass. You can do this by filling it with ice water and setting it aside while you prepare the drink.
2. **Combine Ingredients:** In a shaker, combine the vodka, cranberry juice, Cointreau, and fresh lime juice. Fill the shaker with ice.
3. **Shake:** Shake the ingredients vigorously for about 15 seconds. The shaking not only mixes the cocktail but also chills it thoroughly.
4. **Prepare the Glass:** Discard the ice water from your martini glass, ensuring it's nicely chilled.
5. **Strain:** Strain the cocktail into the chilled martini glass. A fine mesh strainer can be used to ensure a smooth pour without any ice shards or pulp.
6. **Garnish:** Garnish with a lime wheel or a lime twist on the rim of the glass or floating on top.

### Tips:
- The quality of ingredients matters. Use good quality vodka and Cointreau to make sure your Cosmopolitan tastes great.
- The balance of sweet and tart can be adjusted to taste. If you prefer a sweeter Cosmo, you can add a bit more cranberry juice or Cointreau. For a tarter drink, increase the lime juice slightly.
- For a clear, professional look, make sure to strain the cocktail well to avoid ice chips in the drink.

Enjoy your Cosmopolitan!
