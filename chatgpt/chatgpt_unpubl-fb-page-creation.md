## Assistant

Creating an unpublished Facebook page means setting up a Facebook page that is not yet visible to the public. Here's how you can do it:

1. **Create the Page:**
   - Go to Facebook and log in to your account.
   - Click on the "Menu" button in the top right corner and select "Create" and then "Page."
   - Fill out the necessary details for your page (e.g., Page Name, Category, Description).

2. **Adjust Visibility Settings:**
   - Once the page is created, navigate to the page's settings by clicking on the "Settings" button in the bottom left corner of the page.
   - In the "General" tab, look for the "Page Visibility" section.
   - Change the visibility to "Page unpublished."

3. **Save Changes:**
   - After adjusting the settings, make sure to save your changes.

Your Facebook page will now be unpublished, meaning it will not be visible to the public until you decide to publish it.
