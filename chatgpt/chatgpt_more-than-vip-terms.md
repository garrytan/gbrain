## Assistant

Terms that suggest a step above “VIP” vary by context. Commonly used examples include:

- **VVIP** (Very Very Important Person) – often used for top-tier guests at events.
- **Elite / Executive / Platinum / Black** – names often used in loyalty or credit-card programs to signal a level higher than standard VIP.
- **Ultra / Premier / Prestige / Chairman’s Club** – hospitality and travel industries use these for their highest-spending clientele.
- **Inner Circle / Founders Circle** – conveys exclusive membership or early-access status.

These labels are marketing choices rather than formal rankings, but they’re widely understood as “beyond VIP.”
