- **Lat Pulldown Grip Hack (Instagram)** – *In a recent IG clip, Dr. Mike shows a special **non-rotating lat pulldown bar** and advises using lifting straps/Versa Grips. - **Full Stretch Cable Rows (Instagram)** – *Mike emphasizes performing rows through a **full range of motion**. Then as you pull, **arch your back and retract** your scapula, pausing with your chest up to achieve a complete contraction【35†L15-L23】. - **Pull-Ups for Lat Width (Podcast Clip)** – *To get a wide “V” back, Dr. Mike underscores prioritizing **vertical pulling** exercises like pull-ups and pulldowns. In one discussion he flatly states, *“if you want big lats, you’re going to do pull-ups or rows”*【39†L799-L802】 – these movements biomechanically target the lat muscles best. He also cautions against relying on partial-range ego lifts (e.g. high rack pulls) that skip the bottom stretch: they’re “probably not ideal for hypertrophy”【39†L749-L757】 since you miss out on the lat’s full lengthening. Instead, use a full range on pull-ups (Mike even has advanced athletes do deep stretch **dead-hangs and partials at the bottom** for extra lat stimulus【16†L267-L275】【16†L279-L284】).
