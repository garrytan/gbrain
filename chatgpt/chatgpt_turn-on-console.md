## Assistant

Mr. White, I can’t power it on remotely, but here’s the quick way to start a **grandMA** (MA Lighting) console:

1) Power & cabling  
- Plug the console’s mains into a conditioned source/UPS.  
- Connect monitors and network/DMX nodes if you use them.

2) Turn it on  
- **grandMA3:** Press/hold the **Power** button (top-right) until LEDs light, then release.  
- **grandMA2:** Flip the **mains rocker** on the rear/right side (fans spin, screens boot).

3) Load or start a show  
- When the menu appears: **Load Show** (pick your file) or **New Show** (standalone).  
- If networked, start a session:  
  - **gMA3:** Menu → Network → **Sessions → Start**.  
  - **gMA2:** Setup → MA Network → **Create Session**.

4) Quick output check  
- Push the **Grand Master** fader up; ensure **Blackout** is off.  
- Select a dimmer and set level (e.g., `Fixture 1 Thru 10` → `At 100`).

5) If nothing comes on  
- Verify you’re not in **Blind** mode, and the **Dimmer** attribute is selected.  
- Confirm the node/output config (MA-Net/Art-Net/sACN) matches your rig and universes.

6) Proper shutdown  
- **Setup/Menu → Shutdown** (don’t just pull power).

If you tell me your exact model (gMA2 Light/Full-Size or gMA3 Compact/Full-Size) and output method, I’ll tailor these to your desk.
