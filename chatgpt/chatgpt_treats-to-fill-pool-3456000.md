## Assistant

To find out how many treats you would need to fill a swimming pool, we first need to convert the units to a consistent measurement. Let's convert the volume of a treat from cubic inches to cubic feet.

1 cubic inch = (1/12)^3 cubic feet
            = 1/1728 cubic feet

Now we can calculate the number of treats required to fill a swimming pool.

Volume of a swimming pool = 2000 cubic feet

Number of treats = Volume of swimming pool / Volume of a treat
                = 2000 cubic feet / (1/1728) cubic feet
                = 2000 * 1728
                = 3,456,000

Therefore, you would need approximately 3,456,000 treats to fill a swimming pool.
