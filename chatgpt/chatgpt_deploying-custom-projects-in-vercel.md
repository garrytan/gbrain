## User

Yo, I'm trying to use Versal's vo.dev platform, and whenever I go to the portfolio and open a project, it gives me a deploy button up on the right, but whenever I make my own from scratch, I don't get a deploy button up top on the right, and I'm confused as to how I'm supposed to deploy my own projects.

---

## Assistant

Mr. White, the “Deploy” button only appears once you’re working inside a v0 Project (your Portfolio items are Projects, but scratch chats aren’t). To get it:

1. **Create a new Project**  
   - Open the sidebar → **Projects** → **New Project** (top-right)  
   - Give it a name and click **Create Project** citeturn5view0  

2. **Attach your chat to that Project**  
   - Either continue your work in that project’s chat thread, or click **Deploy** in any chat (v0 will auto-create the project if it doesn’t exist) citeturn5view0  

3. **Deploy**  
   - With the project open, you’ll now see a **Deploy** button at the top-right of the interface. Click it to push a new deployment to Vercel.  
   - Once it’s built, a toast notification will give you the live URL, or you can find it in your Vercel dashboard.  

That will let you deploy your own from-scratch projects just like your portfolio templates.
