## Assistant

To get your music distributed to Rockbot, follow these steps:

1. **Sign up with a music distributor**: Use a music distribution service that partners with Rockbot. Popular distributors include DistroKid, TuneCore, and CD Baby.

2. **Upload your music**: Submit your music through the chosen distributor. Ensure your tracks, artwork, and metadata (like song titles and artist names) are correctly formatted.

3. **Select Rockbot as a platform**: During the distribution process, choose Rockbot as one of the platforms for your music to be distributed to. Some distributors may not list Rockbot specifically, so check their partnerships or contact their support to confirm.

4. **Monitor your submission**: Once submitted, it may take a few weeks for your music to appear on Rockbot. Keep an eye on your distributor’s dashboard for updates on your release status.

5. **Promote your music**: Once your music is live on Rockbot, promote it through your social media, website, and other marketing channels to increase visibility and streams.

If your current distributor doesn't support Rockbot, consider switching to one that does or reaching out to Rockbot directly to inquire about their submission process for independent artists.
