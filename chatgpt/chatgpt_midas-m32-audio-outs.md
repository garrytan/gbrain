## Assistant

That’s a **Midas M32** digital console.  
With the built-in USB interface acting as the internal sound card, you can send:

* **32 discrete USB outputs** (32×32 channel bidirectional interface at 48 kHz/24-bit).  
* These appear to the computer as 32 separate DAW channels, so you can route any combination of inputs, buses, or matrices to those 32 USB outs.

Beyond the USB card, the desk itself also provides:

* **16 mix buses + 6 matrix buses + L/R + Mono/C** as assignable analog/AES outputs.  
* Physical XLR outs on the rear are 16, but you can expand with AES50 stage boxes if needed.

So with only the internal USB card, your max “unique audio outs to the computer” is **32 channels**.
