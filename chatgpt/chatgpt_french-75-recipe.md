**French 75 – Bartender Recipe (Standard Build):**

- **1 oz gin** (London Dry preferred)  
- **0.5 oz fresh lemon juice**  
- **0.5 oz simple syrup** (1:1 ratio)  
- **2–3 oz Champagne** (or dry sparkling wine)  

**Instructions:**
1. Shake gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup with ice.  
2. Strain into a chilled flute.  
3. Top with Champagne.  
4. Garnish with a lemon twist.

*Optional variation:* Use Cognac instead of gin for a richer profile.
