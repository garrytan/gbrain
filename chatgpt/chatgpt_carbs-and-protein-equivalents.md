## Assistant

Certainly! Here's a recipe for a Raspberry Lemon Drop cocktail as a bartender would make it:

Ingredients:

- 2 oz vodka (preferably citrus-infused vodka)
- 3/4 oz triple sec (orange liqueur)
- 1 oz fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 oz raspberry liqueur (e.g., Chambord)
- 1/2 oz simple syrup (made by dissolving equal parts sugar and water)
- Fresh raspberries and lemon twist for garnish
- Ice

Instructions:

1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.
2. Add 2 ounces of vodka, 3/4 ounce of triple sec, 1 ounce of fresh lemon juice, 1/2 ounce of raspberry liqueur, and 1/2 ounce of simple syrup to the shaker.
3. Shake vigorously until well-chilled, typically for about 10-15 seconds.
4. Strain the mixture into a chilled martini or cocktail glass.
5. Garnish with fresh raspberries and a twist of lemon peel.
6. Serve and enjoy your Raspberry Lemon Drop cocktail!

This cocktail combines the tartness of lemon with the sweetness of raspberries for a delightful and colorful drink. Please remember to drink responsibly.
