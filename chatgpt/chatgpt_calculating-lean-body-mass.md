## User

Hey, how's it going? So I'm currently 173 pounds and 14% body fat. What's my lean body mass?

---

## Assistant

To calculate your lean body mass, you can use the following formula:

Lean Body Mass (LBM) = Total Body Weight × (1 - Body Fat Percentage)

So, with your current weight of 173 pounds and 14% body fat:

LBM = 173 pounds × (1 - 0.14) = 173 pounds × 0.86 ≈ **148.8 pounds**

Your lean body mass is approximately 148.8 pounds.
