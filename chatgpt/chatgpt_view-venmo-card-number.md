That screen is normal. Venmo hides the card number by default.

**To see your Venmo Debit Card number:**
1. Tap **Account & routing** (eye icon) on that screen.
2. Select **View card details**.
3. Complete Face ID / Touch ID / PIN.
4. Your **card number, expiration date, and CVV** will appear.

**If you don’t see “View card details”:**
- Make sure the **physical card is activated**.
- Update the Venmo app to the latest version.
- If it’s a **virtual-only card**, the number still appears there once verified.

If it still doesn’t show after that, the account may be restricted and you’ll need Venmo support to unlock it.

Mr. White,

That screen is **not** your card number. It’s your **bank account & routing** info for ACH transfers and direct deposit.

**Why you can’t see the card number:**
- You’re in **Account & Routing** (ACH).
- Venmo separates **bank numbers** from **card details**.

**To see your Venmo debit card number:**
1. Go back one screen.
2. Tap the **Venmo Debit card image** at the top (not Account & Routing).
3. Look for **View card details**.
4. Authenticate (Face ID / PIN).

**If “View card details” does not exist:**
- The card is **not activated**, or  
- Venmo has **restricted virtual card access**, or  
- You’re using a **teen/business profile** (card number hidden).

In that case, only Venmo Support can enable it. The numbers shown in your screenshot **cannot be used** as a debit card number.
