Cannabis is legal for adults **21+** in California. Hollywood follows California state law and Los Angeles city ordinances.

### What You Can Legally Do

**1. Possess (age 21+)**
- Up to **28.5 grams (1 oz) of cannabis flower**
- Up to **8 grams of concentrated cannabis** (vape oil, hash, edibles extract)

**2. Use in Private**
- Consume cannabis **inside a private residence**
- Property owners/landlords may prohibit use on their property

**3. Grow at Home**
- Up to **6 plants per residence** (not per person)
- Must be grown **indoors**
- Not visible to the public
- Landlords can prohibit cultivation

**4. Buy from Licensed Retailers**
- Purchase from state-licensed dispensaries in Los Angeles
- Valid government ID required (21+ recreational; 18+ with medical recommendation)

**5. Transport**
- Legal amounts allowed in a vehicle
- Must be in a **sealed container**
- Cannot consume while driving or riding in a vehicle

---

### What Is Illegal

**Public consumption**
- No smoking, vaping, or consuming in public places
- Includes sidewalks, parks, beaches, restaurants, bars
- Los Angeles generally does not allow on-site consumption lounges (rare exceptions if specially licensed)

**Driving under the influence**
- DUI laws apply to cannabis

**Possessing more than legal limits**

**Taking cannabis across state lines**
- Illegal even to other states where it is legal

**Federal property**
- Cannabis is illegal on federal land and in federal buildings

---

### Special Situations

**Hotels/Airbnbs**
- Usually prohibited by property policy

**Apartment balconies**
- Typically considered public-facing; often prohibited by lease or HOA rules

**Workplace**
- Employers can prohibit use and discipline for impairment
