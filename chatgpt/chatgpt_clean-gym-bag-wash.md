## Assistant

Hey, it's going well. To wash your gym bag and get rid of the smell, follow these steps:

1. **Empty the Bag**: Remove all items from your gym bag, including any removable compartments or pockets.

2. **Read the Care Label**: Check for any washing instructions on the care label inside the bag.

3. **Pre-treat Stains**: If there are any visible stains, pre-treat them with a mild detergent or stain remover.

4. **Hand Washing**:
   - Fill a large basin or sink with lukewarm water.
   - Add a small amount of mild detergent.
   - Submerge the bag and use a soft brush or cloth to scrub the inside and outside gently.
   - Pay special attention to the areas around the zippers and the plastic compartment.
   - Rinse thoroughly with clean water to remove all soap residue.

5. **Machine Washing** (if care label permits):
   - Place the bag in a laundry bag or pillowcase to protect it.
   - Use a gentle cycle with cold water and a mild detergent.
   - Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners.

6. **Drying**:
   - Air dry the bag by hanging it upside down to allow water to drain out.
   - Ensure it's completely dry before using it again to prevent mold or mildew.

7. **Deodorize** (optional):
   - Sprinkle baking soda inside the bag and let it sit overnight before shaking it out.
   - You can also use a fabric-safe deodorizer spray.

By following these steps, you should be able to clean your gym bag effectively and get rid of any unpleasant smells.
