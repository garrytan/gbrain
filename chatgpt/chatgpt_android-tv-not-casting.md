## Assistant

To troubleshoot your Android TV not showing up in the Apple TV app as a casting option, follow these steps:

1. **Check Network Connection**: Ensure both your Android TV and the device running the Apple TV app are connected to the same Wi-Fi network.

2. **Restart Devices**: Restart your Android TV, the device running the Apple TV app, and your Wi-Fi router.

3. **Update Software**: Ensure that both your Android TV and the Apple TV app are running the latest software versions.

4. **Enable Casting**: On your Android TV, ensure that casting options (such as Chromecast) are enabled. Go to Settings > Apps > See all apps > Show system apps > Google Cast Receiver > Enable.

5. **Reinstall Apple TV App**: On the device running the Apple TV app, uninstall and then reinstall the app.

6. **Check Permissions**: Make sure the Apple TV app has the necessary permissions to access your network and devices.

7. **Reset Network Settings**: On your Android TV, go to Settings > Network > Network settings reset.

If the issue persists, there might be a compatibility issue or a specific problem with the app on your device. You may need to contact customer support for further assistance.
