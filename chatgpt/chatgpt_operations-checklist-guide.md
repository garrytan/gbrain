## Assistant

Here’s a concise operations checklist and explanation you can drop straight into a staff handbook or SOP doc.

---

### Floor Plan  
Provide a labeled diagram of the venue: entrances/exits, main bar, kitchen, storage, bathrooms, emergency exits, and staff break area. Post a printed copy near the manager’s office and in the kitchen.

### Tip Pool Math  
Example using your stated points:  
- **Bar Management:** 12.5 pts  
- **Lead Bar:** 11.75 pts  
- **Bartenders/Servers:** 10 pts  
- **Support Staff:** 1 pt  

**Formula:**  
1. Add all points for that shift.  
2. Total tips ÷ total points = tip value per point.  
3. Each person’s payout = their points × tip value.  
Apply deductions (late arrival, phone use) by subtracting those fractions of a point before calculating.

### Uniforms  
- **In-season:** Black pants, black shoes, branded top, minimal jewelry, hair tied back.  
- **Off-season:** Same base uniform; swap branded top for plain black.  
- **Notes:** No large logos other than house brand. Piercings only studs. Hair neat, no strong scents.

### Toast Setup  
- Create merchant account.  
- Upload menu with modifiers.  
- Add staff with roles/permissions.  
- Link kitchen/bar printers.  
- Set up 86-list notifications so items can be marked “out” in real time.

### 86 Items  
If an item is out, staff must immediately inform the **manager or lead bartender** so it’s 86’d in Toast and verbally communicated to servers.

### Evacuation Plan  
- All guests exit through main entrance unless unsafe.  
- Secondary exit: back-of-house door.  
- Manager or lead bartender does headcount at assembly point (parking lot).  
- Keep exits clear at all times.

### Break Policy  
Breaks taken in the staff lounge or designated outdoor area only. Notify manager before leaving floor. Cover your station.

### Kitchen Cutoff  
Kitchen stops taking orders **15 min before show end** or as posted by management.

### Lost & Found  
- During show: bring items to **main bar**.  
- End of night: move all unclaimed items to locked lost-and-found box.  
- Log each item with date/time.

### Food Allergy One-Sheet  
List all menu items with top 9 allergens. Post in kitchen, bar, and POS notes.

### Server Checkout  
Servers close tabs, print end-of-night report, reconcile cash, and get manager sign-off before leaving.

### Setup Photos  
Include clear photos of a fully set table and bar station in the handbook for reference.

### Hair & Jewelry  
Hair secured away from face; no dangling jewelry. Simple studs and wedding bands only.

### Intoxicated Guests & Carding  
- Follow **California RBS** standards.  
- Check IDs with UV light or scanner.  
- Refuse service if signs of intoxication: slurred speech, unsteady gait, aggressive behavior.  
- Document refusals.

### W-2 Forms  
All employees are W-2. HR or payroll provider (e.g., Gusto, ADP) collects I-9, W-4, and issues annual W-2s by Jan 31.
