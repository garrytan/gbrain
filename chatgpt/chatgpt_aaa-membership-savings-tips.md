## Assistant

Mr. White, here are a few straight-ahead ways to trim your AAA signup cost:

- **Pick the leanest tier**  
  Only pay for the level of service you’ll actually use (Basic vs. Plus vs. Premier).

- **Leverage employer/affinity discounts**  
  Many employers, alumni groups or credit-card issuers negotiate 5–10 % off AAA dues.

- **Buy via third-party deals**  
  Watch Groupon, Costco gift cards or AAA partner sites—memberships often appear at 5–15 % off face value.

- **Opt for multi-year renewals**  
  Some regions offer a small discount if you pay for two years up front instead of rolling annually.

- **Bundle with insurance**  
  If you insure your car or home through a AAA-partner carrier, you may qualify for a free or reduced-rate membership.
