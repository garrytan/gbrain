### Spotify / Apple Music Reality
- ❌ **No raw user-level conversion data**
- ❌ No email, phone, or persistent user ID
- ✅ Aggregates (source of stream, playlist adds, saves)

**What *is* happening now (new vs 24 months ago):**
- Better **pre-save attribution modeling**
- Improved **event matching** via Meta/TikTok CAPI
- More reliable *directional* lift measurement

**Consensus workaround**
- Capture identity *before* platform redirect
- Treat Spotify/Apple as **conversion endpoints, not data sources**

This is why **smart links + CRM** are non-optional.
