2. **Wi-Fi Issues**: Make sure you're on a 2.4 GHz network (Ring 2 doesn’t support 5 GHz).
3. **Weak Signal**: Move the router closer temporarily, or check RSSI in the app (you want closer to 0, not worse than -60).
4. **Power Supply**: If it’s battery-powered, make sure the battery is fully charged. Flashing blue can also mean restarting due to low power.
