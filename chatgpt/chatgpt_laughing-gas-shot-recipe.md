## Assistant

Here’s a recipe for the **Laughing Gas Shot**:

### Ingredients:
- 1 oz. Vodka (chilled)
- 1/2 oz. Blue Curaçao
- 1/2 oz. Lemon juice
- 1/4 oz. Simple syrup
- A dash of soda water

### Instructions:
1. In a shaker, add ice, vodka, blue curaçao, lemon juice, and simple syrup.
2. Shake well until chilled.
3. Strain the mixture into a shot glass.
4. Add a quick dash of soda water on top for a fizzy finish.
5. Serve immediately.

This shot should have a fun, vibrant look with a refreshing and slightly sweet, citrusy flavor.

## Assistant

Here’s a recipe for a **Blue Hawaiian Shot**, which combines rum, pineapple juice, and blue curaçao:

### Ingredients:
- 1 oz. White rum (chilled)
- 1 oz. Pineapple juice
- 1/2 oz. Blue Curaçao
- A dash of lime juice (optional for extra citrus)

### Instructions:
1. In a shaker, add ice, white rum, pineapple juice, and blue curaçao.
2. Shake well until chilled.
3. Strain into a shot glass.
4. Optionally, add a dash of lime juice for extra zing.
5. Serve immediately.

This creates a tropical, sweet, and vibrant shot perfect for a fun event.
