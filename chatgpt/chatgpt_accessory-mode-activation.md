## Assistant

On that Harley switch:  

1. **Insert the key** fully into the slot.  
2. **Push down slightly** on the key while turning—these switches often need a little downward pressure to rotate smoothly.  
3. **Turn counterclockwise** to reach **ACC** (Accessory).  
   - Clockwise from OFF goes toward **LOCK** or **FORK LOCK** (those physically lock the steering).  
   - Counterclockwise from OFF goes to **ACC**.  

⚠️ If it feels stuck, don’t force it—make sure the handlebars aren’t turned hard against the fork lock, because that can bind the mechanism.
