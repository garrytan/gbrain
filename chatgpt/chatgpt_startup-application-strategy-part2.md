## Section 4: Product & Technology

**What is your company going to make (max 200 words)**  
Jovie is the operating system for getting music heard. We start with a 60-second, music-first link-in-bio that converts social traffic into listens and tips. From there we automate the unsexy ops that actually drive discovery: Profile Sync (images/bios/links across top DSPs with territory-claim concierge), Lyrics & Credits Autopilot (ingest, validate, time-sync, distribute), and a Submissions CRM (one-click packets to promo YouTube channels, DJs, college radio, blogs, and tastemakers with tracked replies and follow-ups). Jovie productizes these workflows, measures Link→Listen conversion, and learns what wins where.
