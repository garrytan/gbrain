- **SUSTAIN-6 (Semaglutide):** In this trial, semaglutide *surpassed expectations*. Patients on semaglutide had a **26% lower risk of MACE** than those on placebo【7†L60-L69】. Importantly, semaglutide led to a **39% reduction in the risk of stroke**【8†L37-L45】 – a remarkable finding – and confirmed overall superiority for preventing cardiovascular events【7†L60-L69】.
