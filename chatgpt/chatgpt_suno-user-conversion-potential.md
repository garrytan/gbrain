## Assistant

If you assume 100 million total Suno users and Jovie converts 2% of them into paying customers at $40/month:

- 100,000,000 × 2% = 2,000,000 paying users
- 2,000,000 × $40/month = $80,000,000/month
- Annualized = $960,000,000 ARR

So that’s basically a billion-dollar ARR business from a 2% conversion assumption.

Suno has publicly been reported around the “100 million users” mark multiple times in 2025–2026. citeturn0search1turn0search8turn0search15

What matters more strategically:

- You do not need 2% of all Suno users.
- You need the serious repeat creators.
- Suno reportedly has ~2 million paid subscribers already. citeturn0search8turn0search9

If Jovie became infrastructure for even:
- 10% of Suno paid users = 200k users
- at $40/month = $8M MRR
- = $96M ARR

That already becomes a very large SaaS company.

And your positioning is strong because Suno solves:
- making songs

Jovie solves:
- turning songs into fans, revenue, merch, tickets, campaigns, releases, and business momentum

Those are complementary, not competitive.
