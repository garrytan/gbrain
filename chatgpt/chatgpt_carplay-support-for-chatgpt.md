Mr. White, for ChatGPT in **CarPlay**, the cleanest way is to remove it from CarPlay on your iPhone:

1. Open **Settings**  
2. Go to **General > CarPlay**  
3. Select your car  
4. Tap **Customize**  
5. Tap the **Delete** button next to **ChatGPT**  

Apple’s CarPlay settings page says this is where you add or remove apps that appear in CarPlay. citeturn710921view1

If the issue is that ChatGPT keeps reopening in the car, OpenAI says CarPlay often reopens the **last app you used**. ChatGPT also has a **“Start automatically in CarPlay”** setting under **ChatGPT app > Settings > Voice**. Turning that toggle off should stop it from launching straight into voice mode. citeturn710921view0

If you want **CarPlay itself** off for that vehicle, Apple says you can go to **Settings > General > CarPlay > [your car] > Forget This Car**. citeturn710921view2

If you meant **ChatGPT through Siri / Apple Intelligence** instead, OpenAI says to go to **Settings > Apple Intelligence & Siri > ChatGPT** and turn **ChatGPT** off. It also notes you can turn off **Setup Prompts** afterward if you do not want Siri suggesting it. citeturn710921view3
