## Assistant

Here's a simple recipe for an Aperol Spritz:

### Ingredients:
- 3 parts Prosecco
- 2 parts Aperol
- 1 part soda water
- Ice
- Orange slice (for garnish)

### Instructions:
1. Fill a large wine glass with ice.
2. Add the Prosecco, Aperol, and soda water in the specified parts.
3. Stir gently to combine.
4. Garnish with an orange slice.

Enjoy your refreshing Aperol Spritz!
