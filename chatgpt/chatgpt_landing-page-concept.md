## Assistant

Here’s a tight landing-page section concept that tells that story clearly (left = link stack chaos, right = Jovie focus + dynamic CTA), in a modern dark, Linear/Vercel-ish vibe.

---

## Section: “From link stacks to one adaptive action”

### Layout
**Two-column split (50/50)**
- **Left card:** “Traditional link page”
- **Right card:** “Jovie profile”

Add a subtle vertical divider between, plus a faint grid/noise background.

---

## Left: Traditional link page (stack of links → traffic splits → bounce)
**Card header**
- **Title:** Traditional link pages  
- **Subtitle:** “One visit → 12 choices”

**Visual**
- A tall list of pill links (8–12), each with tiny icons and inconsistent labels (to feel messy).
- A flow overlay:
  - One thick “Traffic” stream enters the card from the top.
  - It splits into many thin lines toward each link.
  - Most lines fade out with a “Bounce” label near the bottom.

**Microcopy**
- **Callout label:** Decision fatigue
- **Body:** “Visitors hesitate, click around, or leave. Too many options kills momentum.”

**Metric badge (bottom-right of card)**
- “High bounce”
- Example: “~70–90% drop-off” (or use “Most visitors bounce” if you don’t want numbers)

---

## Right: Jovie profile (one destination → converts, CTA adapts per visitor)
**Card header**
- **Title:** Jovie profile  
- **Subtitle:** “One visit → one best next step”

**Visual**
- A clean profile module (avatar, name, short bio line, social proof chips).
- **One primary CTA button** centered and prominent.
- Below it, a small “Dynamic per visitor” indicator with rotating examples:
  - “Book a demo”
  - “Start free trial”
  - “Get a quote”
  - “Download the guide”
  - “Join the waitlist”

**Traffic flow**
- One thick stream enters the card and goes straight to the CTA.
- A “Convert” label + small burst/glow at the button.

**Microcopy**
- **Callout label:** Personalized intent
- **Body:** “Everyone lands in the same place—Jovie chooses the CTA that fits the visitor.”

**Metric badge**
- “Higher conversion”
- Example: “2–5× lift” (or “Most visitors take action”)

---

## Main headline + supporting copy (top of section)
**Headline options**
1) **Stop sending people into a link maze.**  
2) **One profile. One action. Tailored per visitor.**  
3) **Less choice. More conversions.**

**Supporting copy**
“Traditional link pages split attention across a stack of links. Jovie routes every visitor to a single focused destination—then adapts the CTA to match who they are and why they came.”

---

## CTA for the section (below the two cards)
Primary button: **Create your Jovie**  
Secondary link: **See it in action**

---

## Style notes (dark, Linear/Vercel-ish)
- Background: near-black with subtle gradient (charcoal → black)
- Cards: slightly lighter than background, 1px border, soft shadow
- Accent: one electric color (blue/violet) used for the “Jovie” CTA glow + traffic line
- Typography: tight, geometric; lots of spacing; minimal icons
- Motion (if you want): animated flow lines + rotating CTA label on the right
