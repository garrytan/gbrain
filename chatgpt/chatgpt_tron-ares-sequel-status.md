**What happened to the *original* Tron 3?**

Disney green-lit a direct *Legacy* sequel (working title **Tron: Ascension**) in 2015 with Hedlund and Wilde attached, then scrapped it a few months later after *Tomorrowland* flopped. That project is dead; many of its ideas were cannibalized and reworked into *Tron Ares*. There’s no separate “missing sequel” still in active development. citeturn16view0

**Bottom line**

*Tron Ares* is a legacy sequel that shifts the spotlight to Jared Leto’s Ares while keeping continuity with the earlier films via Kevin Flynn. As of today, neither Olivia Wilde nor Garrett Hedlund is involved, and the earlier *Ascension* sequel is off the grid for good.
