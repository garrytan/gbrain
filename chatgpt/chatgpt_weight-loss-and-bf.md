Starting weight: 142 lbs  
Body fat percentage: 12.7%  
Total weight loss: 2% of 142 lbs = 2.84 lbs

**New weight** after one month = 142 lbs - 2.84 lbs = 139.16 lbs

Assuming no muscle loss, your lean body mass (LBM) remains the same:

Lean body mass (LBM) = 142 lbs × (1 - 0.127) ≈ 124.98 lbs

**New body fat percentage**:

New body fat weight = 139.16 lbs - 124.98 lbs = 14.18 lbs  
New body fat percentage = (14.18 lbs / 139.16 lbs) × 100 ≈ 10.2%

So, after one month of losing 0.5% of your body weight per week:

- **New weight**: 139.16 lbs
- **New body fat percentage**: Approximately 10.2%
