Do they issue DUIs for people riding Lime scooters?

---

## Assistant

Yes. Under California law e-scooters (like Lime) count as “vehicles,” so operating one while intoxicated can trigger a DUI.  Cal. Veh. Code §23152 makes it a misdemeanor to drive any vehicle under the influence, and there’s even a scooter-specific infraction under §21221.5.
