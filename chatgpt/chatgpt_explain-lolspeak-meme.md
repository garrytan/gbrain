**"Can I haz cheeseburger?"** is an intentionally misspelled, grammatically incorrect phrase made famous by the **LOLCats** meme genre. It's written in a style called **"LOLspeak"**, a mock form of English meant to mimic how a cat might speak if it could type.

### Origin:
- First gained popularity in **2007** on the website **icanhas.cheezburger.com**.
- The meme typically featured photos of cats with humorous captions written in LOLspeak.

### Meaning:
- Literally: "Can I have a cheeseburger?"
- Humorously: It conveys a cute, needy, or innocent request—usually paired with an image of a cat making a pleading face.

### Cultural significance:
- It became a cornerstone of early internet meme culture.
- It helped spawn many spin-off memes and contributed to the rise of image macros and meme-based humor online.
