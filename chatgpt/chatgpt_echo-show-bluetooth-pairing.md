To factory reset your Amazon Echo Show 10 (3rd Gen), follow one of these methods:

---

### ✅ **Method 1: Using the Screen**
(if the touchscreen is working)

1. Swipe down from the top of the screen.
2. Tap **Settings**.
3. Scroll down and tap **Device Options**.
4. Tap **Reset to Factory Defaults**.
5. Confirm when prompted.

---

### ✅ **Method 2: Using Buttons**
(if the screen is unresponsive)

1. **Press and hold both**:
   - **Mic mute button** (⨂ icon)
   - **Volume down button**  
2. Hold for **15 seconds**, until the Amazon logo appears.
3. Release the buttons. It will reset and restart.
