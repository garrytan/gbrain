## Assistant

Here’s how to **accurately measure your waist** for men’s underwear sizing, Mr. White:

### 1. Tools You’ll Need
- A **soft measuring tape** (cloth or flexible plastic)
- A **mirror** (optional, but helps ensure tape placement)
- Light clothing or none at all for accuracy

---

### 2. Identify the Correct Waist Location
- Don’t measure at your jeans waistline — that’s **too low**.
- Find your **natural waist**, which is typically **1 inch above your belly button** and the **narrowest part of your torso**.
- For **low-rise underwear**, measure around the **hips**, just above where the waistband would sit.

---

### 3. Take the Measurement
1. Stand relaxed, **don’t suck in your stomach**.
2. Wrap the tape **around your natural waist** level.
3. Keep the tape **snug but not tight** — it should rest against your skin without compressing it.
4. Make sure the tape is **level all around** (parallel to the floor).
5. Record the number in **inches** (most sizing charts use inches).

---

### 4. Compare to Brand Sizing
Each brand’s size chart differs slightly, so:
- Check the brand’s **specific chart** (e.g., Small = 28–30", Medium = 32–34", etc.).
- If between sizes, go **up one size** for comfort unless you prefer a compression fit.

---
