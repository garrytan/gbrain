## Assistant

Mr. White — YC-style audit: your current H1 is *clear-ish* but too generic, and “powered by Jovie AI” is doing the opposite of what you want in a hero (it raises questions before you’ve earned attention).

### What’s weak (YC lens)
- **“New way” is fluff**: Doesn’t say *what changes* or *why it wins*. Everyone says “new.”
- **“Market music” is vague**: Labels, indie artists, managers, DSP promo, ads, content, influencer seeding—this could mean 10 things.
- **“Powered by AI” isn’t a benefit**: It’s an implementation detail. YC wants *result + who it’s for + why now*.
- **No concrete outcome**: Faster? cheaper? more streams? more fans? more posts? higher ROAS? less time?

### What a strong YC hero usually includes
1) **Target user** (artist / label / manager / marketer)  
2) **Primary outcome** (streams, saves, fan growth, ROAS, content velocity)  
3) **Mechanism** (optional, but specific—e.g., “turns tracks into 30 short-form promos + runs ads”)  
4) **Credibility hook** (numbers, proof, “used by X,” or a sharp constraint)

### Better H1 directions (pick a lane)
Here are options depending on what Jovie *actually* does:

**If you generate + distribute content**
- **Turn every release into a month of promo content.**
- **Create, schedule, and publish music marketing content in minutes.**

**If you run paid acquisition**
- **Run profitable music ads—without a media buyer.**
- **Automate creative testing and targeting for music campaigns.**

**If you pitch playlists / PR workflows**
- **Find and pitch the right curators—automatically.**
- **Playlist outreach that scales without spamming.**

**If you’re full-stack “marketing autopilot”**
- **Music marketing on autopilot for independent artists.**
- **Everything you need to promote a release—done for you.**

### “Powered by Jovie AI” — what to do instead
- Make it a **subhead**, not part of the H1.
- Replace with *proof* or *mechanism*:  
  - “Generates high-performing creatives from your track + artist profile.”  
  - “Learns what converts and iterates weekly.”

### A tighter draft using your original structure
**H1:** *Market your music on autopilot.*  
**Subhead:** *Jovie turns each release into content + campaigns that grow streams and fans.*

### Quick heuristic
If I cover your logo and ask “what do you do and for whom?” your current H1 doesn’t answer it.

If you tell me in one line who the buyer is (indie artists vs labels) and the main measurable outcome (streams vs ROAS vs content volume), I’ll give you 5 final hero sets (H1 + subhead + CTA) tailored to that lane.
