For a classic Moscow Mule, you will need ginger beer, vodka, and lime juice. Here's a brief overview of how to make it:

1. Start by pouring lime juice into a chilled glass filled with ice. A copper mug is traditionally used, but a highball glass also works.
2. Add vodka to the glass.
3. Fill the rest of the glass with ginger beer.
4. Garnish with a lime wheel for a classic touch.

Additionally, there are various twists on the classic Moscow Mule that you can try. For instance, the Jalapeño Moscow Mule adds a spicy kick with jalapeño slices and fresh mint, while the Strawberry Moscow Mule incorporates sweet strawberries and a hint of black pepper. The Watermelon Mule is another refreshing variant that includes watermelon chunks.
