**HF-L** stands for **High Frequency, Low (calorie per meal)**.

It's the eating style often recommended by Dr. Mike Israetel and RP Strength, consisting of:

- Eating **smaller, more frequent meals** (typically 4–6 meals daily)
- Meals emphasize **high protein** to preserve muscle during dieting
- Helps control hunger and maintain steady energy and performance

The approach aims for sustained fullness and steady nutrient intake, ideal for body composition goals.
