## User

I need to pick up a laptop from my ex and copy some data off for her then put some stuff on it for her and then drop it back off. how do we do this without having to see each other.

---

## Assistant

Here’s a simple, low-drama way to handle it without needing to see her:  

### Options for Transfer  
1. **Neutral Drop-off/Pick-up Spot**  
   - Agree on a safe, neutral location (friend’s house, front desk of an apartment, coffee shop counter).  
   - She leaves the laptop with staff/a trusted person.  
   - You pick it up later, do the data work, and return it the same way.  

2. **Third-Party Relay**  
   - Ask a mutual friend or family member (someone you both trust) to act as the hand-off person.  

3. **Doorstep Exchange**  
   - She leaves it outside her door at a set time.  
   - You pick it up after she’s gone.  
   - Same process for returning it.  

4. **Courier/Delivery Service**  
   - Use Uber Connect, TaskRabbit, or a local courier.  
   - She schedules the pickup from her place and has it delivered to you.  
   - You schedule the return once you’re done.  

### Tech Side  
- Bring an external hard drive or USB so you can copy her data quickly and offline.  
- If she needs files uploaded, you can also transfer via cloud (Google Drive, Dropbox) instead of physically putting it on the laptop, if that simplifies things.
