## Assistant

Let’s calculate this step-by-step carefully:

1. **Convert liters to ounces**:  
   \( 1 \text{ liter} = 33.814 \text{ ounces} \)  
   So,  
   \( 3 \text{ liters} × 33.814 = 101.442 \text{ ounces} \)

2. **Divide total ounces by pour size**:  
   \( 101.442 ÷ 6 = 16.907 \)

✅ **Answer:** You can pour **16 full drinks**, with a little left over (about **0.9 of a drink**, or ~5.4 oz remaining).
