How do I descale my Nespresso machine with the Nespresso descaling agent?

---

## Assistant

Descaling your Nespresso machine is important for maintaining its performance and coffee quality. Here's a general guide on how to descale it using the Nespresso descaling agent:

1. **Preparation:**
   - Empty the drip tray and the used capsules container.
   - Fill the water tank with 500 ml of water and add the Nespresso descaling solution.

2. **Entering Descaling Mode:**
   - Depending on your Nespresso model, you'll need to enter the descaling mode. This often involves pressing one or more buttons simultaneously for a few seconds. Check your machine's manual for specific instructions.

3. **Starting the Descaling Process:**
   - Place a container large enough to catch all the descaling solution under the coffee outlet.
   - Start the descaling process. This usually involves pressing a button to start the water flow. The descaling solution will run through the machine to remove lime scale and mineral build-up.

4. **Rinsing the Machine:**
   - Once the descaling solution has fully run through, refill the water tank with fresh water.
   - Run the entire tank of clean water through the machine to rinse it. This step may need to be repeated to ensure all descaling solution is completely removed.

5. **Exiting Descaling Mode:**
   - Again, this will vary by model, but typically involves pressing the same buttons used to enter descaling mode.

6. **Final Steps:**
   - Once the process is complete, ensure that the drip tray and capsule container are reinserted.
   - Your machine is now ready to use.

Remember, these steps are general guidelines. Refer to your Nespresso machine's manual for model-specific instructions. Regular descaling, as recommended by the manufacturer, helps prolong the life of your machine and ensures the best coffee flavor.
