## User

The iv bags they make for cocktails, will they spill if people squeeze them

---

## Assistant

Yes, IV bags used for cocktails can potentially spill if squeezed, especially if they are not sealed properly or if the spouts are left open. These bags are typically designed to dispense liquids, so applying pressure by squeezing could cause the liquid to leak out, especially if the nozzle is loose or if there’s an open valve for pouring. If you're using them in a bar setting, it's a good idea to ensure they are sealed tightly and educate customers on handling them gently to avoid spills.

## User

Im looking for non spill containers for the cocktails people can take through the haunted house experience without spilling them when they get scared

---

## Assistant

For a spill-proof cocktail experience during the haunted house, here are some container options that minimize spilling even when people get startled:

1. **Spill-Proof Tumbler with Lid**: Insulated tumblers with snap-on lids and straw holes (with a plug) can prevent spills. The lid stays secure, and the straw can be covered or removed to avoid leaks.

2. **Screw-Top Bottles**: Small plastic or glass bottles with screw caps are secure and can be pre-sealed. Guests can drink directly from the bottle without needing a straw, reducing spill risk.

3. **Flexible Pouch with Resealable Cap**: Similar to children's juice pouches, these soft pouches with resealable spouts offer portability and are harder to spill, even if squeezed. You can use clear or themed pouches for added visual appeal.

4. **Collapsible Water Bottles**: These bottles are often made of soft, foldable materials with secure caps, designed for minimal spills even when compressed. They can be branded to fit the theme and are easy for guests to carry.

5. **Squeeze Bottles with Valves**: Similar to sports drink bottles, these have a valve that only opens when pressure is applied. This ensures drinks don't leak if the bottle is tipped or squeezed.

Choosing one of these options can keep drinks safe and enjoyable during the haunted house experience without worrying about spills.
