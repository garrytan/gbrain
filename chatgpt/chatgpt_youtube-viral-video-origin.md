## Assistant

Mr. White,

**It is a YouTube-era viral video, not Vine-era.**

**“Charlie Bit My Finger”** was first uploaded to **YouTube in May 2007** by Howard Davies-Carr. It became one of the earliest and most iconic YouTube viral videos, predating Vine by several years.

**Key timeline facts:**
- YouTube launch: **2005**
- “Charlie Bit My Finger” upload: **2007**
- Vine launch: **2012**

Vine did not exist when the video went viral. It was later re-shared, referenced, and meme-ified during the Vine and TikTok eras, but its origin is firmly **early YouTube**.
