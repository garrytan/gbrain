Here's a checklist for everything your bar should have ready for the show:

### Front Bar:
1. **Mixers:**
   - Coke

   - Sugar-Free Red Bull
   - Ginger Beer
   - Club Soda
   - Tonic Water

2. **Rimming:**
   - Salt
   - Tajín

1. **Garnishes:**
   - Fresh Lime Wedges
   - Lemon Wedges
   - Cherries
   - Olives

2. **Bar Tools:**
   - Shakers
   - Strainers
   - Jiggers
   - Muddlers
   - Stirring Spoons
   - Bottle Openers
   - Corkscrews

3. **Ice:**

4. **Glassware:**
   - Highball Glasses
   - Lowball Glasses
   - Martini Glasses
   - Wine Glasses
   - Beer Glasses
   - Shot Glasses

5. **Other Essentials:**
   - Napkins
   - Straws
   - Stirrers
   - Coasters
   - Bar Towels
   - Trash Bins

6. **Liquor Stock:**
   - A variety of Vodka, Gin, Rum, Tequila, Whiskey, Bourbon, Scotch, and Liqueurs

7. **Beer and Wine:**
   - Selection of Beer (Bottled/Canned and Draft if applicable)
