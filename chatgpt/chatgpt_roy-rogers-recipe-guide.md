## Assistant

The classic Roy Rogers is a non-alcoholic mocktail made with cola and grenadine, typically garnished with a maraschino cherry. Here's the standard recipe:

**Ingredients:**
- 6 oz of cola (Coca-Cola or any other cola)
- 1/2 oz of grenadine
- Maraschino cherry (for garnish)

**Instructions:**
1. Fill a glass with ice.
2. Pour the grenadine over the ice.
3. Top off with cola and stir gently.
4. Garnish with a maraschino cherry.

Enjoy your refreshing Roy Rogers!
