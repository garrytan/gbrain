**Visual Inspection Checklist for 2016 Mazda CX-5 (At Dealer):**

**Exterior:**
- Panel gaps uneven = possible accident repair  
- Paint mismatches, bubbling, or overspray = repaint  
- Dents, scratches, or rust (especially around wheel wells, rocker panels)  
- Headlights/fog lights hazy or cracked  
- Tire tread even across all 4 (use penny test, should cover part of Lincoln's head)  
- Wheels scratched or bent  

**Interior:**
- Seat wear/tears, especially driver's bolster  
- Headliner stains or sagging = water leaks  
- Musty smell = flood/moisture history  
- Cracked dash, worn buttons = high use  
- Test AC, heater, radio, infotainment  

**Under the Hood:**
- Oil level clean and full (no milky color = no coolant contamination)  
- No fluid leaks underneath (check after test drive too)  
- Battery terminals clean, not corroded  
- Belts/hoses not frayed or brittle  

**Trunk:**
- Spare tire + jack present  
- Water stains = leak  
- Check for uneven trunk liner = hidden accident  

**Test Drive:**
- Smooth acceleration and braking  
- No clicks when turning (CV joint)  
- No excessive vibration at highway speed  
- Transmission shifts smooth  
- Steering aligned, no pull left/right  
- No dashboard warning lights  

If anything feels off, bounce.
