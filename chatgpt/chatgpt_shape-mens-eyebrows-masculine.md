How to shape men's eyebrows to look masculine

## Assistant

To shape men's eyebrows in a way that maintains a masculine look, follow these steps:

1. **Clean Up Stray Hairs**: Remove stray hairs under the natural brow line, especially between the brows, avoiding over-plucking or creating too high of an arch.
2. **Maintain Natural Thickness**: Preserve the natural thickness and shape of the brows, focusing on subtle cleanup rather than dramatic changes.
3. **Trim Length**: If the brow hairs are long, comb them upwards and trim only the tips that extend beyond the main brow line.
4. **Avoid Over-Defining**: Keep the brows looking natural. Sharp lines or over-defined shapes can appear less masculine.
5. **Consult a Professional**: If unsure, seek advice from a professional groomer or stylist.
