**Usually NOT allowed** as a company expense.

Why:
- They are **meal substitutes**
- They have **personal health / fitness value**
- They are commonly tied to **workouts, dieting, or macros**
- IRS views them closer to **meals or supplements**, not refreshments

Even if:
- You consume them while working
- You skip lunch because of them
- You’re coding all day

They still count as **personal nutrition**.

| Item | Why |
|----|----|
| Coffee | Incidental stimulant / refreshment |
| Protein bar | Nutritional intake / meal replacement |
| Protein shake | Dietary supplement |

| Meal replacement | Personal food |

Protein crosses the line from “refreshment” → “sustenance.”
