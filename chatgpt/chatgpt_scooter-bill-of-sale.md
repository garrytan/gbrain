## Assistant

Mr. White, here’s a concise California Bill of Sale template for your scooter. Fill in the brackets before printing and signing:

---

**CALIFORNIA BILL OF SALE**

**1. PARTIES**  
**Seller:**  
Name: Tim White  
Address: 932 Larrabee St, Apt 211, West Hollywood, CA 90069  

**Buyer:**  
Name: [Buyer Full Name]  
Address: [Buyer Address]  

**2. DESCRIPTION OF SCOOTER**  
Make: [Brand]  
Model: [Model]  
Year: [Year]  
Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) or Serial No.: [VIN/Serial]  
Color: [Color]  
Odometer Reading: [___] miles (Truthful to Seller’s knowledge)  

**3. SALE TERMS**  
Sale Price: $[Amount]  
Payment Received: ☐ Cash ☐ Check (# _____) ☐ Other: [Describe]  
Date of Sale: [MM/DD/YYYY]  

**4. CONDITION & “AS-IS”**  
The Seller hereby sells and the Buyer hereby buys the above-described scooter in “as-is” condition, with no warranties or guarantees, express or implied.

**5. TRANSFER OF TITLE & RISK**  
Title and risk of loss pass from Seller to Buyer upon full payment and delivery of scooter.

**6. SIGNATURES**

Seller’s Signature: ________________________ Date: _____/_____/_____  
Buyer’s Signature: ________________________ Date: _____/_____/_____  

---

You may have it notarized for added proof, though California doesn’t require notarization on a basic Bill of Sale. Let me know if you need any tweaks.
