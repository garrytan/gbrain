## Assistant

Campari is a bright red, bitter Italian liqueur made from a blend of herbs, fruits, and spices. It has a strong, complex flavor with notes of orange, cherry, and a distinct bitterness. Campari is often used in classic cocktails like the **Negroni** and the **Boulevardier**, adding depth and a vibrant color.

It's perfect for cocktails that need a balance of sweetness and bitterness, which fits well with the dark, sophisticated vibe of an **American Psycho**-inspired drink.

## Assistant

The **Día de los Muertos Margarita** sounds like a fantastic addition to the menu! Here's a summary of the recipe for ease of use:

### **Día de los Muertos Margarita**

#### Ingredients:
- 2 parts Hornitos plata or reposado tequila
- 1 part orange juice
- ½ part lime juice
- ¾ part honey syrup

#### Instructions:
1. Combine equal parts salt and chili powder on a plate.
2. Use an orange or lime wedge to wet the rim of a rocks glass, and roll the rim into the salt/chili powder mixture.
3. Combine the tequila, orange juice, lime juice, and honey syrup in a cocktail shaker with ice.
4. Shake well and strain into the rimmed glass over fresh ice.
5. Garnish with an orange wheel and edible marigold.
