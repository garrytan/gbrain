---

## 1. **The Exorcist: Levitation Horror**
- **Concept:** A performer strapped with a hidden harness writhes on a bed that suddenly levitates and spins mid-air, reenacting Regan’s possession.  
- **Effect:** Combine blackout strobe hits, guttural voice playback, and projection-mapped demonic faces.  
- **Twist:** End with the priest getting "dragged" across the stage by unseen forces (a quick-pull harness).  

---

## 2. **Beetlejuice Graveyard Cabaret**
- **Concept:** Comedy-horror number with Beetlejuice emceeing, backed by zombie dancers in striped suits.  
- **Effect:** Break the fourth wall—he heckles guests, then “summons” skeletons from trap doors/smoke.  
- **Song Options:** Danny Elfman’s theme mashed with a modern remix for energy.  

---

## 3. **The Shining: Hall of Mirrors**
- **Concept:** Jack Torrance “breaks through” mirrored panels on stage (breakaway plexi with safe edges).  
- **Effect:** Multiple Jacks appear—dancers in identical costumes and axes—swarming the audience aisles.  
- **Immersion:** Redrum projected across walls in dripping blood font.  

---

## 4. **Carrie: Blood Rain Finale**
- **Concept:** A prom-style setup where dancers celebrate—then “Carrie” gets doused in fake blood.  
- **Effect:** Instead of water, use LED-lit red fabric rain or confetti cannons for safety/cleanup.  
- **Twist:** Carrie retaliates with telekinetic moves (objects flying on wires, pyro pops).  

---

## 5. **Poltergeist TV Possession**
- **Concept:** A child performer crawls toward a vintage static-playing TV.  
- **Effect:** Giant ghostly arms (fabric puppetry with black-clad operators) “pull” them in; they vanish.  
- **Payoff:** They reappear suspended in the air, glowing under UV paint.  

---

## 6. **Hellraiser Chains Aerial**
- **Concept:** Aerialists perform wrapped in dangling chains instead of silks, with Cenobite-style costuming.  
- **Effect:** Chains lower from above, clanking soundscape, industrial score.  
- **Spectacle:** Pyro sparks as they “break free” mid-air.  

---

## 7. **Addams Family Séance Comedy**
- **Concept:** Morticia hosts a séance that goes wrong.  
- **Effect:** Lights flicker, table levitates, Uncle Fester shoots sparks from his mouth, Lurch plays eerie live organ stabs.  
- **Tone:** Balances macabre comedy with immersive spook factor.  

---

## 8. **American Horror Story: Freakshow**
- **Concept:** Classic circus freak vibes—contortionists, sword swallowers, fire breathers.  
- **Effect:** Spotlight a “bearded lady” who bursts into aerial flight.  
- **Twist:** Audience “recruitment” moment where actors drag guests into the troupe.  

---

## 9. **The Ring: Well Apparition**
- **Concept:** Build a false well on stage. Samara climbs out slowly, drenched in long black hair.  
- **Immersion:** Projection of static TV fills the walls.  
- **Payoff:** She crawls into the audience, then vanishes in a blackout, instantly reappearing overhead on a harness.  

---

## 10. **Ghostbusters Dance-Comedy**
- **Concept:** High-energy break in tension with Ghostbusters busting through fog with proton pack props.  
- **Effect:** Glow-in-the-dark Slimer puppet flies over the crowd.  
- **Audience Buy-In:** Big sing-along “Who you gonna call?”

## 🎪 Freakshow (AHS Circus Vibe)

- **Immersion:** Freak barkers work the aisles before curtain—audience gets “recruited.”

- **Tone:** Dark, but with tongue-in-cheek carnival patter so it balances your Chuckle number.
