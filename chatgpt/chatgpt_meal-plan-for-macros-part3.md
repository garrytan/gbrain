Moscow mule recipe


A Moscow Mule is a refreshing and simple cocktail. Here's how to make one:

### Ingredients:
- 2 oz Vodka
- 4 to 6 oz Ginger beer
- 1/2 oz Fresh lime juice
- Ice cubes
- Lime wedge for garnish
- Mint sprig for garnish (optional)
- Copper mug or highball glass

### Instructions:
1. **Prepare the Mug/Glass**: Fill a copper mug or a highball glass with ice cubes.
2. **Mix**: Pour 2 oz of vodka and 1/2 oz of fresh lime juice over the ice.
3. **Top Off**: Add 4 to 6 oz of ginger beer to fill the mug or glass.
4. **Garnish**: Stir gently to mix. Garnish with a lime wedge and, if you like, a sprig of mint.
5. **Serve**: Enjoy your Moscow Mule!

Note: The copper mug is traditional and believed to enhance the flavor and experience, but it's not mandatory. The essential part is the balance of the sharp ginger beer with the vodka and the freshness of the lime.
