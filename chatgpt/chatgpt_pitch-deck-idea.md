## Assistant

Yes, Mr. White. Strong hook.

Frame it as:

**“Every artist gets a manager. She’s tireless, strategic, plugged-in, and kind of a badass.”**

Deck spine:

1. **Title** — *The AI Manager for Every Artist*
2. **Problem** — Most artists are creative, under-managed, and overwhelmed
3. **Insight** — Management is the missing layer between talent and monetization
4. **Solution** — A personal AI manager that plans, books, promotes, follows up, and grows the artist
5. **Persona** — “She” as the badass manager: assertive, protective, strategic
6. **Product** — Calendar, opportunities, outreach, brand deals, releases, fan growth, task execution
7. **Why Now** — AI agents + creator economy + fractured music/artist tooling
8. **Market** — Independent musicians, creators, visual artists, performers
9. **Business Model** — Subscription + transaction fees + premium services
10. **Go-To-Market** — Start with indie musicians / creators who already self-manage
11. **Moat** — Artist graph, workflow data, deal intelligence, personalized agent memory
12. **Vision** — Every artist has a manager before they have a label, agency, or team

Best tagline options:

**“The manager every artist deserves.”**

**“Your career has a new right hand.”**

**“An AI manager for the next generation of artists.”**

**“She handles the business. You make the art.”**
