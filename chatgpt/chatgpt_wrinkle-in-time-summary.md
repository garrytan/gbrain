*A Wrinkle in Time* follows Meg Murry, her genius little brother Charles Wallace, and their new friend Calvin O’Keefe as they travel across space to find Meg’s missing father, a scientist who disappeared while experimenting with faster-than-light travel. Guided by three mysterious beings—Mrs. Whatsit, Mrs. Who, and Mrs. Which—they “tesser,” or warp through space and time, visiting strange worlds and learning that Mr. Murry is trapped on Camazotz, a planet under the control of a dark, mind-controlling force called IT that demands absolute sameness and obedience.

In the end, she realizes the only thing IT can’t understand or control is genuine love. Using that love—her fierce, stubborn affection for Charles Wallace—she breaks IT’s hold and rescues him, returning home with her family. The book doubles as a sci-fi adventure and a story about individuality, courage, and loving what makes you and others different.
