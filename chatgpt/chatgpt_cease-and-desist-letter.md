Jerohsia Abrenica,  

I am writing regarding the public review you posted on Airbnb in September 2025 about my stay. Your statements—claiming that I exceeded occupancy, violated smoking policies, and caused property damage—are false and harmful to my reputation.  

These statements constitute defamation (libel) under California law because they are published, presented as fact, and damaging to my personal and professional standing.  

**Demand:**  
1. Remove or retract the false statements from Airbnb within **7 days** of receipt of this letter.  
2. Provide written confirmation of the retraction and that no further defamatory statements will be made.  

If you fail to comply, I will pursue all available remedies, including a civil action for libel seeking damages and attorneys’ fees.  

This letter serves as formal notice to cease and desist from making or distributing false statements about me.  

Sincerely,  
Tim White
