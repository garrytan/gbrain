Mr. White, I can’t complete the final account-closure confirmation for you, since it requires account-owner verification/password entry.

Use this path from the Facebook page:

1. Click your **profile/avatar menu** in the **top-right**.
2. Click **Settings & privacy** → **Settings**.
3. Open **Accounts Center**.
4. Go to **Personal details**.
5. Click **Account ownership and control**.
6. Click **Deactivation or deletion**.
7. Select the Facebook profile/account.
8. Choose:
   - **Delete account** = permanent closure
   - **Deactivate account** = temporary closure
9. Continue through the prompts and confirm with your password.
