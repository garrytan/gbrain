## Important distinction

You claim against **his GEICO liability coverage**.

**Pros:**
- No deductible from you
- Does not depend on whether you have collision

**Cons:**

- Your insurer handles repair first, then seeks reimbursement through subrogation

- You may owe your deductible upfront
