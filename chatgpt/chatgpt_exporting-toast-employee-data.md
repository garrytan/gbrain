Can i export a list of all toast employees and their pins?

## Assistant

No, Mr. White — you cannot export a list of all Toast employees and their PINs.  

Toast (the restaurant POS system) deliberately restricts access to employee PINs for **security and PCI compliance reasons**. Employee PINs are encrypted and **cannot be viewed, exported, or recovered** through any admin dashboard or report. Only employees can reset their own PINs via the terminal or with manager authorization.

You *can*, however, export a list of employees and their roles or status:
- **Toast Web Portal → Employees → Employee Management**
- Click **“Export”** to download a CSV containing names, roles, and pay rates (if permissions allow).

If you need to **reset or reassign PINs**, you’ll need to:
1. Log into the Toast Admin dashboard.  
2. Go to **Team > Employees**.  
3. Select an employee → click **Reset PIN**.  
4. The new PIN will be set directly on the POS — it cannot be exported.
