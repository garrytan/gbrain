## Assistant

Finding collections of custom edits and mashups from big DJs can be tricky, but here are some methods and resources to streamline your search:

### **1. Tracklists and Platforms**
- **1001Tracklists**: As you mentioned, it's a great resource but time-intensive. To save time:
  - Use their **search filters** (e.g., genre, event, or artist).
  - Check the **Top Overall Tracks** and trending charts for popular edits.
  - Bookmark sets from DJs who frequently use custom edits.
  
- **SoundCloud**: Many DJs upload their edits and mashups here, including Kaskade and others.
  - Search directly for the artist name + "edit" or "mashup."
  - Follow DJ fan pages and repost accounts like **"HouseBootlegs"** or **"EDM Mashups."**

- **Mixcloud**: Similar to SoundCloud but often used for entire sets. Some DJs upload full sets with exclusive edits.

---

### **2. Dedicated Groups and Forums**
- **Reddit**: Subreddits like [r/EDM](https://www.reddit.com/r/EDM/) or [r/DJs](https://www.reddit.com/r/DJs/) occasionally share edits and bootlegs.
- **Discord Communities**: Many DJ communities host file-sharing groups for mashups, edits, and exclusive tracks.
  - Example: Discord servers for [DJ TechTools](https://djtechtools.com/).

---

### **3. DJ Pools and Services**
- **Direct Record Pools**: Some pools cater to custom edits and bootlegs. Examples include:
  - **DJ City**: Great for mashups and exclusive remixes.
  - **Club Killers**: Known for its mashups and festival-friendly tracks.
  - **Digital DJ Pool**: Offers mashups and remixes.
  
- **Private Edits Pools**: Some DJs run Patreon pages where they share exclusive edits and mashups.

---

### **4. Social Media & YouTube**
- **Instagram/TikTok**: Follow DJs and look for hashtags like #DJEdits or #Bootlegs.
- **YouTube Channels**: Many bootleg/mashup channels regularly upload collections (e.g., **"EDM Bootlegs"** or **"Festival Mashups"**).
  
---

### **5. Collaboration and Networking**
- Reach out to smaller DJs or producers who often create edits. Many are open to sharing their libraries for cross-promotion or feedback.
- Attend DJ meetups or industry events where producers share exclusive edits as promo material.

---

### **6. Automation with Tools**
- **RSS Feeds/Alerts**: Use tools like Feedly to create alerts for new edits posted on SoundCloud, 1001Tracklists, or specific websites.
- **Download Managers**: Tools like JDownloader can scrape SoundCloud or similar platforms if a DJ has multiple uploads.
