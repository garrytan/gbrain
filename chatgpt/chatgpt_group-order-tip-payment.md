## User

If i uber eats group order, who pays delivery fees and tip. If i start the group order and tip like 100 dollars, does that mean my 3 other group members now get stuck with a 25 dollar tip charge?

---

## Assistant

On Uber Eats group orders:  

- **Delivery fee**: The person who starts the group order pays it. It’s not split across members.  
- **Tip**: The same — the host sets the tip amount and covers it. Others can only pay for their own food.  

So if you start the order and leave a $100 tip, *you alone* are charged that $100. Your 3 other group members don’t get any portion of it added to their bill.  

👉 They only pay for their food + any sales tax/service fees on *their own items*.
