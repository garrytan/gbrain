**Total:** 4 + 3 = **7 hours**

## Assistant

Here’s a clear plan you can follow, Mr. White, based on your income sources, variable hours, and need for rent security:

---

### 1. Core Accounts & Roles
- **Envelope Money** – Use for all recurring bills (gym, insurance, subscriptions) because of the per-envelope virtual cards.  
- **Chase Checking** – Use only as the “rent hub” for your $800/month share and any Zelle transfers to Liv.  
- **Robinhood** – For investments/dollar-cost averaging.  
- **Cash Drawer** – For tip cash until deposited or spent intentionally.

---

### 2. Paycheck Split (weekly Vault direct deposit)
Use **percentage-based** allocation so it scales with variable income:

| Destination | % of net pay | Purpose |
|-------------|-------------|--------|
| **Chase** | **40 %** | Rent buffer & Zelle to Liv |
| **Envelope “Fixed Bills”** | **40 %** | Gym, insurance, subscriptions |
| **Envelope “Fun/Variable”** | **10 %** | Dates, going-out, small cash |
| **Robinhood** | **10 %** | Automatic weekly investing |

> Adjust percentages if hours rise/fall; priority is always rent first.

---

### 3. Rent Buffer
- Chase target: **$2,400** (3 months of $800) before you slow deposits.  
- Set an automatic **$50/week** *minimum* to Chase even in dark months to keep the buffer growing.

---

### 4. Envelope Setup
Create separate envelopes (each with its own virtual card):
- **Gold’s Gym** – $80/mo  
- **LA Fitness** – $45/mo  
- **Car Insurance** – $175/mo  
- **Subscriptions/Misc** – Spotify, iCloud, etc.  
- Optional: **Date Night / Vacations** – fund weekly.

Because each envelope has a dedicated card, no bill can pull from the wrong bucket.

---

### 5. Cash Handling
- End of each shift: put cash tips in the drawer.  
- Weekly: deposit excess beyond a set cap (e.g., keep $200 on hand) into the “Fun/Variable” envelope.

---

### 6. Other Income
- **Facebook Marketplace sales / random checks**: deposit straight to Chase to pad the rent buffer or send to Robinhood if rent buffer is full.

---

### Key Takeaways
- **Percent-based split + rent buffer** handles variable income and dark weeks.  
- **Chase = rent/Zelle only**, keeps Liv’s payment simple.  
- **Envelope cards per bill** guarantee no accidental overdrafts.  
- **Automatic Robinhood** ensures steady investing without thinking.

This setup keeps bills bulletproof, builds a cushion for slow months, and still funds fun and investing.
