## Assistant

Mr. White, since your iPad isn’t a “native” SMS device, you won’t get the SMS OTP unless your iPhone forwards it. Try this:

1. **Enable Text Message Forwarding**  
   On your iPhone:  
   • Settings → Messages → Text Message Forwarding → toggle on your iPad.  
   Now SMS OTPs to your number will mirror on the iPad.

2. **Use the BofA Mobile App**  
   Install the Bank of America app on your iPad and sign in. During checkout choose “Send push notification” or “Get code in app” instead of SMS.

3. **Switch to Email Delivery**  
   If the OTP prompt lets you choose email, switch delivery to your address on file—codes arrive instantly and don’t rely on SMS.

4. **Fallback: “Call Me” Option**  
   On the OTP screen select “Call me” if available; you’ll get an automated voice with the code.

If none of those land the code, call BoA at 1-800-432-1000 and have them confirm your contact methods and reset your OTP preferences.
