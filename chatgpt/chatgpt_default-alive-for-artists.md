## Core insight

Most artists **say** they want superstardom.

Most actually want this:

> “Can I work on music full-time and know I’ll survive?”

That is **default alive for artists**.

Paul Graham’s startup idea is: based on current revenue, expenses, growth, and runway, are you on track to survive without rescue? citeturn795558search0

For artists, the version is:

> Based on current fans, revenue, release cadence, touring, merch, and attention, are you on track to keep making music full-time?

## The enemy

The enemy is not obscurity.

The enemy is:

> being talented, working constantly, and still being **default dead**.

Default-dead artist:

- makes music
- posts content
- drops songs
- builds links
- sends emails
- books shows
- makes merch
- guesses what worked
- still cannot predict rent
