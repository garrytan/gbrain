Are you able to determine my waist-to-hip ratio from this DEXA scan?

## Assistant

Mr. White, BodySpec’s DEXA doesn’t record actual waist or hip circumferences, so it can’t give you a true waist-to-hip ratio. What it does report is your Android-to-Gynoid fat ratio (A/G ratio), which is 0.61 for your 5/17/2025 scan (Android fat 12.8 %, Gynoid fat 21.2 %) fileciteturn0file0. 

To get an actual waist-to-hip ratio you’ll need:

1. **Waist circumference** – measured at the narrowest point between rib cage and iliac crest.  
2. **Hip circumference** – measured at the widest point over the greater trochanters.  

Then divide waist (inches or cm) by hip (same units). That gives the standard anthropometric waist-to-hip ratio.
