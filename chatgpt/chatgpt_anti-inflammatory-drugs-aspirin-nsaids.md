## User

What drug is an anti-inflammatory?

---

## Assistant

Aspirin is a common drug that has anti-inflammatory properties, alongside its roles in pain relief and reducing fever. Other examples include ibuprofen and naproxen. These are part of a class of drugs known as nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs).

---

## User

I have back pain. Previously, my doctor has prescribed that I take Tylenol and something else together to deal with the back pain. What would the other drug be?

---

## Assistant

For back pain, doctors often recommend taking acetaminophen (Tylenol) along with a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) like ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin) or naproxen (Aleve) to manage pain. This combination can provide both pain relief and anti-inflammatory benefits. Always follow your doctor's instructions when taking these medications.
