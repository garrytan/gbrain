## User

 Vanilla vodka and frangelico gluten free?

---

## Assistant

Both are generally gluten-free:

- **Vanilla vodka** – Vodka is distilled, so even if it started from wheat or rye the gluten proteins are removed in the distillation process. Most major brands’ vanilla-flavored vodkas are considered gluten-free, though some may add flavorings after distillation.  
- **Frangelico** – This hazelnut liqueur is made from distilled spirits and natural flavorings. The producer states it contains no gluten.

If you’re highly sensitive (celiac), pick brands that explicitly say “gluten-free” on the label or on their website to avoid any risk of post-distillation additives.
