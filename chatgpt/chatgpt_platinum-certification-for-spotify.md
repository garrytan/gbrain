## Assistant

In Austria, the Performance Rights Organization (PRO) responsible for managing the rights of music creators, such as composers, lyricists, and music publishers, is called **AKM** (Autoren, Komponisten und Musikverleger). AKM is part of the global network of collective management organizations and works to ensure that music creators receive fair compensation for the public performance, broadcasting, and use of their works.

AKM's primary functions include:
- **Licensing:** Issuing licenses for the public performance of music, such as concerts, broadcasts, and background music in venues.
- **Royalty Collection:** Collecting royalties from licensed entities and distributing them to the rights holders.
- **Advocacy:** Representing the interests of music creators in legislative and industry matters.

For more detailed information, you can visit the AKM website or the International Confederation of Societies of Authors and Composers (CISAC) website, which provides a directory of global PROs, including AKM.
