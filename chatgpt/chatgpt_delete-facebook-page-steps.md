## User

How do I delete a Facebook page?

---

## Assistant

Here’s the simplest way to remove a Facebook Page you own:

1. **Log in** to Facebook on desktop and switch to the profile that owns the Page.  
2. Go to **Your Pages & profiles** (click your profile photo in the top-right corner → “See all profiles” → choose the Page).  
3. Once viewing as the Page, click **Settings & privacy → Settings → Privacy → Facebook Page Information**.  
4. Select **Deactivate and deletion**.  
5. Choose **Delete Page** and confirm.

Notes:  
- Facebook gives you **30 days** to cancel before it’s permanently deleted.  
- Only people with the **admin** role can delete a Page.  
- If you want to keep the Page but hide it, choose **Deactivate** instead.

That’s it—you don’t need to delete your personal profile to remove the Page.
