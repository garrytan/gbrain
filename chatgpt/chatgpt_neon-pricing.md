**Neon pricing model (usage-based):**  
- **Free plan:** $0 — includes up to *50 CU-hours per project*, *0.5 GB storage per project*, *10 projects*, autoscaling up to small sizes, and basic restore retention. citeturn0search2  
- **Launch (paid):** Usage charges begin here; minimum monthly spend around **$5** (previously $5 minimum on paid plans), then you pay based on actual **compute (CU-hours)** and **storage (GB-months)** used. citeturn0search2  
  * Compute commonly charged per CU-hour (e.g., ~$0.14/CU-h on earlier models). citeturn0search2  
  * Storage around **$0.35 per GB-month**. citeturn0search0  
  * Extra branches beyond quota cost ~**$0.002 per branch-hour**. citeturn0search2  
- **Scale & Enterprise:** Higher tiers still usage-based with more projects, larger autoscaling limits, longer restore windows, and SLAs at higher per-CU rates (e.g., ~**$0.26/CU-h** on Scale). citeturn0search2

Neon doesn’t sell fixed flat bundles anymore; instead, you pay **for actual compute, storage, and optional features** you consume, scaling down to near $0 when idle. citeturn0search2
