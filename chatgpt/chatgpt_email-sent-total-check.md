## User

In MailChimp, I want to see how many emails I've sent in the history of my account.

---

## Assistant

To view the total number of emails you've sent in the history of your MailChimp account, follow these steps:

1. **Log in to MailChimp:**
   Go to [MailChimp](https://mailchimp.com/) and log in with your account credentials.

2. **Navigate to Reports:**
   Once logged in, click on the "Reports" tab in the top navigation bar.

3. **View All Campaigns:**
   In the Reports section, you'll see a list of all your email campaigns. Look for a summary or overview section that provides aggregated data.

4. **Check Campaign Summary:**
   Click on "View Report" next to each campaign to see detailed analytics, including the number of emails sent. To get the total number, you might need to sum these figures manually if MailChimp doesn't provide an overall total in the summary.

5. **Use Account Data:**
   Alternatively, you can contact MailChimp support or check your account settings for any comprehensive historical data that includes the total number of emails sent.

If you need a specific total, exporting the campaign data to a CSV file and summing the "Emails Sent" column using a spreadsheet program like Excel or Google Sheets can also be helpful.
