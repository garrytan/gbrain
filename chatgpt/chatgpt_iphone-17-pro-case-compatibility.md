## User

Yo dawg, my baby cakes just got a 17 Pro iPhone. Is it compatible with any cases for any other model iPhones, or does it have to be made specifically for a 17 Pro?

---

## Assistant

Short answer: your baby cakes needs a 17 Pro–specific case.

iPhone cases are extremely tight tolerances: camera bump, button placement, thickness, corner radius, speaker cutouts—all of it is model-specific. Even “same size” phones from different years usually *don’t* share cases once you factor in the camera island and side button shifts.

So:

- **17 Pro ≠ 16 Pro, 15 Pro, etc.** You can’t reliably reuse older Pro cases.
- **Only exception:** Some super–stretchy silicone sleeves *might* physically go on, but the buttons, mute/action button, camera cutout, and MagSafe alignment will be off. Not worth it for a phone that expensive.

Tell her to look for cases that specifically say **“iPhone 17 Pro”** on the listing/package. Anything else is a gamble.
