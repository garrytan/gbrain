## The core issue

## Fraud vs scam distinction

- **Unauthorized fraud:** someone hacks/uses your account without permission. You may have legal protections under the Electronic Funds Transfer Act / Reg E. Zelle says consumers may be able to assert those rights for unauthorized payments. ŅłĆciteŅłéturn920491search7Ņłü  
- **Authorized scam:** you willingly send money after being tricked. This is where Zelle is especially bad. Refunds are uncertain.

## Rule

Use **Zelle only for people you know and trust**.

Use **Venmo Goods & Services** or a proper payment method with protection for strangers, marketplace deals, deposits, tickets, shipped goods, or anything where the other person can disappear.
