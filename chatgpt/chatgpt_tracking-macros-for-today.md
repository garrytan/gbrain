## Assistant

To bathe a dog effectively:

1. **Prepare the Bath Area**: Use a bathtub or a suitable outdoor area. Have dog shampoo, towels, a brush, and treats ready.
  
2. **Brush the Dog**: Before bathing, brush to remove loose hair and mats.

3. **Use Lukewarm Water**: Ensure the water is not too hot or cold.

4. **Wet the Dog Thoroughly**: Avoid direct water into ears, eyes, or nose.

5. **Apply Dog Shampoo**: Use a shampoo formulated for dogs. Lather and massage it into their coat.

6. **Rinse Thoroughly**: Ensure no shampoo residue remains.

7. **Dry the Dog**: Use towels or a low-heat hair dryer. Keep the dog warm.

8. **Brush Again**: Once dry, brush their coat.

9. **Reward Your Dog**: Treats and praise help make bath time a positive experience. 

Remember, frequency of baths depends on the dog’s breed, coat, and activity level.
