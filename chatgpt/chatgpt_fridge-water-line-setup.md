## Assistant

To add a water line for your fridge, follow these steps:

### Materials Needed:
- ¼" copper or braided stainless steel water line kit
- Saddle valve or tee valve (preferably tee for durability)
- Adjustable wrench
- Teflon tape

### Steps:
1. **Shut off main water supply**.
2. **Locate a nearby cold water pipe**, usually under the kitchen sink.
3. **Install a tee valve** on the cold water line if one isn't already available.
4. **Connect the water line** to the valve and run it to the back of the fridge.
5. **Attach the line** to the fridge’s water inlet (usually marked).
6. **Turn on the water** slowly and check for leaks.
7. **Secure the line** along its path to prevent kinks or damage.
