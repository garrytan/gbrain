## User

What happened to private mode

---

## Assistant

Mr. White, there isn’t (and hasn’t been) an official feature in ChatGPT called “Private Mode.” However, OpenAI introduced a setting called **Chat History & Training** in April 2023. Turning **Chat History & Training** off stops your conversations from being used to improve the model and hides them from your conversation history. If you previously heard someone call that setting “private mode,” it’s likely they just meant toggling off **Chat History & Training**—there was never a separate, dedicated “Private Mode” that got removed.
