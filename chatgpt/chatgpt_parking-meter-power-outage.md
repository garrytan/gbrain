## Assistant

Mr. White, if you park at a metered space and the meter itself has no power or display:

- **Los Angeles practice:** The City of LA treats a dead or blank meter as free parking. Their posted policy is “if the meter is inoperative, you may park up to the posted time limit with no payment required.”  
- **Proof:** Take a quick photo showing the screen is off or erroring and that you’re within the posted time limit.  
- **Check signage:** The street signs still control other restrictions (street sweeping, loading zones, etc.). You must still obey those.

Bottom line: in Los Angeles you can park for the signed time limit without paying when the meter is dark, but document it and follow all other posted rules.
