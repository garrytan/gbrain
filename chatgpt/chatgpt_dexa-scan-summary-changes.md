- If you feel **two horizontal grooves above the navel and one just above it**, that’s a six-pack.

If you want to test the eight-pack potential: drop **1–3 lb of fat** to nudge toward **~10–11%**, while keeping abs thick with **weighted cable crunches** and **reverse-crunch/pelvic-tilt** work **2–3×/week** (slow reps, full posterior pelvic tilt), plus an anti-extension move like **ab-wheel** or **RKC plank**.
