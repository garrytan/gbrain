**Carbs (whole)**
- Oats
- Brown rice, quinoa
- Sweet potato
- Whole-grain bread/pasta

**Fats (healthy)**
- Avocado
- Nuts & seeds

- Fatty fish (salmon, sardines)

**Veggies & Fruit**
- Load up non-starchy veggies (spinach, broccoli, peppers)
- Whole fruit (berries, apples, oranges)

### 🔹 What to Avoid
- Added sugars (sodas, candy, pastries)
- Refined grains (white bread, white rice)
- Processed meats (sausages, deli slices)
- Highly processed snacks

### 🧠 Simple Rules to Follow
1. **Plate balance:** ~40% veggies, 30% protein, 30% whole carbs + healthy fats.  
2. **Cook at home:** control ingredients and portions.  
3. **Drink water:** aim for ~3L/day; limit sugary drinks.  
4. **Minimize sauces:** many hide sugar/salt.  
5. **Prep meals:** batch cook to stay consistent.
