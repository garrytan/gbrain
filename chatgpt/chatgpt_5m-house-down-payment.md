Mr. White — for a ~$5,000,000 house, here’s the “moderate / conservative” picture in today’s jumbo-rate world.

## Down payment (typical for jumbo)
- **20% down:** **$1,000,000** (loan **$4,000,000**)
- **25% down:** **$1,250,000** (loan **$3,750,000**)
- **30% down:** **$1,500,000** (loan **$3,500,000**)

Many lenders want **20–25%+** on big jumbo loans (and sometimes more as the loan size climbs).

Also budget for:
- **Closing costs:** ~**2–4%** of price (**$100k–$200k**)
- **Cash reserves:** often **6–12 months** of payments in liquid assets (common jumbo requirement)

## Monthly payment (principal + interest) at current jumbo rates
As of **Jan 14, 2026**, national average **30-yr fixed jumbo** is roughly **~6.3–6.4%**. citeturn0search1turn0search3

Approx **P&I only** (30-year, ~6.38%):
- **20% down ($4.0M loan):** **~$25.0k/mo**
- **25% down ($3.75M loan):** **~$23.4k/mo**
- **30% down ($3.5M loan):** **~$21.8k/mo**

## The part people forget: taxes + insurance (+ HOA)
On a $5M home, very rough ballpark:
- **Property tax:** often around **~$5k–$6k/mo** (varies a lot by exact location/assessments)
- **Homeowners insurance:** **~$400–$1,000+/mo**
- **HOA (if any):** **$0 to $2,000+/mo**

So a realistic **all-in housing cost** is often:
- **20% down:** **~$31k–$34k/mo**
- **30% down:** **~$28k–$31k/mo**

## Income needed (so it doesn’t feel tight)
Two common yardsticks:

### Conservative (comfortable): housing ≈ **25% of gross income**
Using ~$6.5k/mo for taxes/ins/HOA:
- **20% down:** ~$31.5k/mo housing ⇒ **~$1.5M/yr gross**
- **30% down:** ~$28.3k/mo housing ⇒ **~$1.36M/yr gross**

### More “standard lender” (can qualify, not necessarily comfy): housing ≈ **30–36% of gross**
- **20% down:** roughly **~$1.26M/yr (30%)** down to **~$1.05M/yr (36%)**
- **30% down:** roughly **~$1.13M/yr (30%)** down to **~$0.95M/yr (36%)**

**Rule of thumb:** if you want this to feel *moderate/conservative*, you’re usually talking **$1.3M–$1.6M+ household gross**, *unless* you’re putting a lot more down or you have meaningful non-W2 income and low/no other debts.
