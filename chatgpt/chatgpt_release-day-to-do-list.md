   - Check all streaming platforms (Spotify, Audius, Apple Music, etc.) to ensure the song is live and available.
   - Post an early morning announcement on Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, and Facebook.
     - Caption example: "You lucky fux are the first to know that Never Say A Word drops today! 🎶🔥 #NewMusic #NeverSayAWord"
   - Update your bio links on social media to direct to the new release.
   - Share the song link in your Instagram Story with a "Swipe Up" link (if available) or direct link in bio.

2. **Morning:**
   - Post a reminder on all social media platforms about the release.
     - Caption example: "The wait is over! 'Never Say A Word' is officially out now. Stream it, share it, and let’s get it trending! 🎧 #NewMusicFriday #NeverSayAWord"
   - Engage with fans' comments and messages about the release.
   - Reach out to confirmed playlists to ensure the song is added and visible.

3. **Midday:**
   - Share a behind-the-scenes clip or teaser from the lyric video on social media.
   - Send an email blast to your mailing list announcing the release.
   - Check the status of your song on third-party playlists and blog placements.
   - Engage with comments and messages, thanking fans for their support.

4. **Afternoon:**
   - Post another update on social media highlighting the song's progress and thanking fans for their support.
   - Share screenshots or stories from fans listening to the song.
   - Collaborate with influencers and friends to share the release on their platforms.

5. **Evening:**
   - Host a live session on Instagram or TikTok to talk about the release, answer fan questions, and perform a snippet of the song.
   - Post a final reminder about the release on social media with a call-to-action to stream and share the song.
     - Caption example: "It’s been an incredible day! 'Never Say A Word' is out now and I couldn’t be happier with the response. Keep streaming and sharing! 🙌 #NeverSayAWord #ThankYou"

6. **Night:**
   - Check streaming statistics and initial feedback.
   - Plan thank you messages or posts for the following day to keep the momentum going.

### Additional Tips:
- Keep an eye on all platforms for any technical issues and address them promptly.
- Stay active and engaged with your audience throughout the day.
- Encourage fans to share their thoughts and tag you in their posts.

Good luck with the release!
