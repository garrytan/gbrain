  - **Pros:**  
    - Walking distance to Saint Felix (Liv) and Kookaburra (you).  
    - One-bedrooms around \$1,600–\$1,900.

    - Rents typically \$2,000+.

- **Koreatown**  
  - **Pros:**

  - **Cons:**

- **Venice Beach**  
  - **Pros:**  
    - Beach lifestyle.  
  - **Cons:**  
    - 45–60 min commute each way to Hollywood.  
    - Rents \$2,500+ for anything decent.

**Recommendation:** Stick around Hollywood proper or East Hollywood—especially near Bronson/Vine. You’ll save on rent, walk to both bars, and still be within a reasonable Uber or bus ride of the Beverly Center shows. Avoid Venice’s long commutes and Koreatown if the vibe isn’t for you.
