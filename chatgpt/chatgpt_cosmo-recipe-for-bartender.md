## Assistant

To make a classic Cosmopolitan cocktail, you'll need:

1. 1 1/2 oz vodka
2. 1 oz Cointreau (or triple sec)
3. 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
4. 1/4 oz cranberry juice

Here's how to make it:

1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
2. Add all the ingredients.
3. Shake well until chilled.
4. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
5. Garnish with a lime wheel or twist.

Enjoy your Cosmo, Mr. White!

If you only have rocks glasses, you can still enjoy a Cosmopolitan. Simply follow the same recipe and pour the shaken mixture into the rocks glass.
