# Landing Page Spec: Jovie

## 1. Page Goal

Primary goal: get artists to claim or start their Jovie profile.

Secondary goal: make managers, labels, and artist teams understand that Jovie is a fan-growth layer, not another link page.

The page should convert three mindsets:

| Visitor | What they think | What the page must prove |
|---|---|---|
| Independent artist | “I need a better link in bio.” | Jovie gives them a polished music profile that captures fans and keeps working. |
| Active releasing artist | “Every drop starts from zero.” | Jovie turns each visit, stream, QR scan, show, or tip into reachable audience. |
| Manager / label / team | “We need infrastructure.” | Jovie centralizes profile, releases, fan capture, notifications, analytics, and revenue actions. |

## Section 12 — FAQ

### Is Jovie a link-in-bio?

Jovie gives artists one shareable link, but the product is a music profile with releases, shows, payments, fan capture, analytics, and automated reactivation.

### How is this different from a smart link?

A smart link usually serves one release. Jovie gives the artist one permanent profile that keeps routing fans to the right action.

### What happens when a fan turns on alerts?

Jovie can notify them when new music drops or when a relevant show is coming up.

### Can I use Jovie for QR codes?

Yes. Use the same profile or tracked links for posters, merch tables, busking, shows, and street-team campaigns.

### Do I need to design my own page?

No. Jovie should be positioned as polished by default: claim, connect, and publish.

### Who is Jovie for?

Independent artists, active releasing artists, managers, labels, and teams that want one music-native profile and fan-growth system.

### What does Pro unlock?

Pro unlocks fan notifications, deeper analytics, pre-saves, countdown pages, reactivation, CRM, and advanced growth features.

# 12. What to Remove or De-emphasize

## De-emphasize as hero concept

**“Release more music with less work.”**

That is useful, but it makes Jovie sound like a release productivity tool. The product’s sharper wedge is fan capture and reactivation.

## De-emphasize early AI composer

The AI workspace is valuable, but leading with “Ask Jovie” risks making the product feel like a generic AI assistant. Lead with the artist profile and fan-return loop. Bring AI in as the engine that keeps the system moving.

## Remove vague claims

Avoid:
- “Grow your career”
- “Unlock your potential”
- “AI for artists”
- “Everything you need”
- “The future of music”

Use concrete artist actions:
- Listen
- Buy tickets
- Tip
- Subscribe
- Turn on alerts
- Open latest release
- Scan QR
- Save show
- Contact artist
