   - The real money ($5k–$20k per show) comes when festival promoters see you consistently in the online space, on credible playlists, and in media coverage.

In short, **combine consistent streaming content with a powerful PR push and a selective new release or two**. If you market it right—and leverage your existing social audiences by hammering them with new clips—this approach can substantially speed up the timeline back to high-paying gigs.
