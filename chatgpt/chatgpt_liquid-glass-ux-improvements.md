- **Contrast issues** — several screens (e.g., gender select) show poor legibility. Increase text brightness or reduce background blur intensity.
- **Unify visual hierarchy** — buttons (e.g., “Continue”) sometimes blend into the background; ensure they pop consistently.
- **Iconography inconsistency** — imported Health icons and buttons should feel part of a unified visual language.

### ⚙️ Engineering
- **Adaptive fallback** — add smooth fallbacks for non-iOS 26 environments to avoid broken UX.
- **Selective Liquid Glass** — restrict the blur to decorative areas (headers, progress bar backgrounds), not behind form fields or actionable content.
- **Reduce animation jitter** — “Setting up your dashboard” has visible loading stutter. Use smoother transitions or skeleton loaders.
