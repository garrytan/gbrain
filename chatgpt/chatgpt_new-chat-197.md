Here's a simple recipe for a French 75:

**Ingredients:**
1. 2 oz Gin
2. 1/2 oz Fresh lemon juice
3. 1/2 oz Simple syrup
4. 3 oz Champagne (or other sparkling wine)

**Instructions:**
1. Combine gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
2. Shake well until chilled.
3. Strain into a chilled champagne flute.
4. Top with champagne.
5. Gently stir to combine.
6. Optionally, garnish with a lemon twist.
