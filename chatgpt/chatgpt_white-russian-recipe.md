## Assistant

Here's a recipe for a classic White Russian:

### Ingredients
- 2 ounces vodka
- 1 ounce coffee liqueur (like Kahlúa)
- 1 ounce heavy cream

### Instructions
1. Fill a rocks glass with ice.
2. Pour the vodka and coffee liqueur over the ice.
3. Stir gently to combine.
4. Float the cream on top and stir slowly until it begins to mix with the other ingredients.

Enjoy your White Russian!

## Assistant

### Vanilla White Russian
- Add 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract to the drink for a subtle vanilla flavor.

### Caramel White Russian
- Drizzle caramel syrup into the glass before adding the ice.
- Stir in 1/2 ounce of caramel syrup with the vodka and coffee liqueur.

### Hazelnut White Russian
- Substitute the coffee liqueur with hazelnut liqueur (like Frangelico) for a nutty twist.

### Peppermint White Russian
- Add a splash of peppermint schnapps to the mix for a minty flavor.
- Garnish with a candy cane during the holiday season.

### Cinnamon White Russian
- Add a dash of ground cinnamon or cinnamon syrup to the drink.
- Garnish with a cinnamon stick.

### Chocolate White Russian
- Add 1/2 ounce of chocolate syrup to the vodka and coffee liqueur.
- Garnish with chocolate shavings.

Feel free to mix and match these ideas to suit your taste!
