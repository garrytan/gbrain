## Recommended “non-negotiables” to state explicitly
These translate directly into constraints you can declare up front to filter candidates.

**Role / authority**
- You are CEO (final decision-maker on company direction)

**Standards of behavior**
- High mutual respect is mandatory; no tolerance for adversarial or political dynamics  
- Direct communication and willingness to resolve conflict quickly  

**Work ethic**
- Consistent high-intensity commitment (define hours, pace, availability)  
- Bias toward action and shipping  

**Alignment**
- Shared long-term vision (company size, exit expectations, time horizon)  
- Agreement on risk tolerance (bootstrapped vs venture-scale outcomes)

**Operating style**
- Ability to handle stress without degradation in judgment or collaboration  
- Willingness to adapt roles as the company evolves  

**Capability**
- High learning velocity (ability to acquire missing skills rapidly)  
- Ownership mindset across functions, not narrow role protection

## Observations
- CEO conflict is a visible symptom; the root issue is unclear authority and misaligned ambition.  
- Work ethic mismatch consistently appears as a leading cause of breakdown.  
- Respect and communication failures tend to compound over time and become irreversible.
