## 📌 Slack Basics

**What Slack is:**  
Slack is a team communication platform built around **channels** (grouped topic chats) and **direct messages (DMs)**. It’s designed to keep conversations organized, searchable, and fast.citeturn0search0

**Core UI features:**  
- **Search bar:** find messages, files, people.citeturn0search0  
- **Plus (+) button:** create messages, channels, uploads, etc.citeturn0search0  
- **Profile menu:** set status, adjust preferences, notifications.citeturn0search0

---

## 💬 Messaging & Conversations

**Channels vs DMs:**  
- **Channels:** topic or team-based group chats.  
- **DMs:** one-to-one or small group private chats.

**Mentions:**  
- `@username` to notify someone.  
- `@here` notifies active members only.  
- `@channel` notifies everyone in the channel.  
- `@everyone` notifies all members of the workspace (usually restricted).citeturn0search1

## ⚙️ Notifications

**Manage notifications:**  
- Go to your profile → **Preferences** → **Notifications**.  
- Choose to get notified for all messages or only mentions/DMs.citeturn0search7

**Per-channel notification control:**  
- Open the channel → **Notifications icon** at the top → set preferences (all, mentions only, mute).citeturn0search3

## ⚡ Slash Commands (Productivity Shortcuts)

Type a `/` in any chat to use commands. Useful ones include:citeturn0search4turn0search11

- `/remind` — set a reminder (e.g., `/remind me at 3pm`).  
- `/msg @user message` — send a quick message.  
- `/search term` — search Slack content.  
- `/join #channel` — join a channel.  
- `/leave #channel` — leave a channel.  
- `/mute` — mute notifications for a chat.  
- `/status` — update your status.  
- `/dnd` — enable Do Not Disturb.  
- `/collapse` / `/expand` — hide/show media previews.  
- `/saved` — view your saved items.

👉 Many more available via `/shortcuts`.citeturn0search11

## 🧠 Tips to Stay Organized

- Use **threads** to keep discussions tidy.  
- **Leave or mute** channels you don’t need to reduce noise.  
- **Star or save** key messages to find them easily later.citeturn0search5
