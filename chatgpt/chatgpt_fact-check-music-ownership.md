## Assistant

This post is misleading.

**Fact check:**
- *Owning your masters* means owning the original sound recordings—not that you get "all the money."
- Nearly every modern pop album, especially by someone like Bieber, involves **multiple producers, songwriters, mixers, and featured artists**, all of whom are entitled to **royalties**.
- On top of that, **publishing rights** (lyrics and composition) are separate from master rights, and revenue from sync, mechanicals, and streaming gets split according to complex contracts.

**Accurate comment:**

> Owning the masters doesn't mean “all the money goes to him.” There are still splits with producers, writers, mixers, and publishers. This post is just pop fan fiction.
