**Can a refrigerator fit?**  
Most standard apartment-size fridges are roughly 65–70 in tall, 28–32 in deep and 30–36 in wide. If you tip the fridge onto its back (so its depth becomes its “height”) and slide it in:

1. Its “height” (depth) must be ≤ 31.5 in to clear the tailgate opening.  
2. Its “length” (fridge height) must be ≤ 67 in (or ≤ 77 in if you move the front seat forward).  
3. Its width must be ≤ 54 in (and ideally ≤ 40 in if you’re keeping it between the wheel wells).  

**Bottom line:** a typical fridge (~30 in deep × ~30 in wide × ~66 in tall) **will fit** laid on its back, provided you slide the front seat forward enough. Always double-check your specific fridge’s packaged dimensions before loading.
