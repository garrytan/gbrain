## User

what is the rules on EPs for DSPs. How many songs can i have

---

## Assistant

For most Digital Service Providers (DSPs), the guidelines for an EP (Extended Play) typically include the following:

1. **Number of Tracks**: An EP usually has 4-6 tracks.
2. **Total Duration**: The total length of an EP should be between 15 to 30 minutes.

Different DSPs might have slight variations in these rules, so it's a good idea to check specific requirements for each platform you're distributing to.

Here are the EP guidelines for major DSPs:

### Spotify:
- **Tracks**: 4-6 tracks.
- **Total Duration**: 15-30 minutes.

### Apple Music:
- **Tracks**: 4-6 tracks.
- **Total Duration**: Less than 30 minutes.

### Amazon Music:
- **Tracks**: 4-6 tracks.
- **Total Duration**: 15-30 minutes.

### YouTube Music:
- **Tracks**: 4-6 tracks.
- **Total Duration**: 15-30 minutes.

### Deezer:
- **Tracks**: 4-6 tracks.
- **Total Duration**: Less than 30 minutes.

These guidelines are generally consistent across most DSPs, but it's always best to verify with each platform as there can be occasional variations or updates to their policies.
