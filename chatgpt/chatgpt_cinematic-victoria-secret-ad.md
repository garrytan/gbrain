# Visual Rules

## Color

- Base image: monochrome, crushed blacks, silver highlights.
- Per shot: only one color accent.
- Acceptable accents:
  - Deep cobalt blue
  - Ultraviolet purple
  - Hot pink
- Never mix blue + purple + pink in the same shot.
- No rainbow cyberpunk. Keep it premium.

## Lighting

- Hard top light.
- Wet reflections.
- Edge-lit faces.
- Chrome UI glow.
- Black negative space.
- Faces partially obscured by rain, glass, shadow.

## Camera

- Locked-off symmetrical Fincher frames for product/UI.
- Bay-style kinetic movement for city/action inserts.
- 50mm and 85mm for face and fashion shots.
- 100mm macro for phone/UI/finger/rain details.
- Wide low-angle 24mm hero shot at 0:22.
