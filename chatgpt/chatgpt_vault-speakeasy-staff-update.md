### The Vault Speakeasy - Updated Staff Guidelines 2024

#### **Uniform and Appearance**
- **Masks:** Black lace masks have replaced the previous uniform accessories. Staff are required to wear these masks from the moment doors open until the guests have left.
- **Security:** Save the Beverly Center Security number to your phone: **310-854-0074**.

#### **General Service Expectations**
- **Bus Tables:** All staff must bus dirty dishes from tables immediately to maintain a clean environment.
- **Table Numbers:** Double-check table numbers when inputting orders and delivering food. Ensure accuracy in seating arrangements and orders.
- **Gratuity:** Remind guests that a 20% gratuity is automatically included. When closing out transactions, inform them that while additional tips are appreciated, they are not necessary.
- **Napkins:** Every drink and a la carte delivery must include napkins.
- **Extra Supplies:** Extra forks, knives, and napkins should be stored at the host stand. To-go boxes are available in the designated area.

#### **Drink Service**
- **Glassware:** 
  - Hard plastic cups are not trash. Please bus them to the designated table after use.
  - Specialty cocktails are served in 16oz stemless wine goblets, filled with ice. Singles should be filled ⅔, and doubles filled to the top. Mixed drinks and soft drinks are served in 12 oz rocks glasses. Double mixed drinks should include a double pour of liquor.
- **Comped Drinks:** 
  - Comped drinks are managed with small heart-shaped trinkets (red and pink). The Maitre D will distribute these to guests on the guest list. Guests exchange these tokens with servers for a free drink. Servers must return the trinkets to the Maitre D at the end of each show for reconciliation.
  - When closing out a comped drink, use the 100% drink ticket discount in the system. If it's the only item on the bill, click pay, then select 'drink ticket' on the screen.

#### **Seating Arrangements**
- **VIP Seating:** VIP sections include all of Sections 1 and 3, the first three rows of Section 2, and specific tables (150/151/152, and 401/402/403/404/405). 
- **Seating Protocol:** Seat guests from front to back, with the 400s seated last. Ensure that standard tables are split as required and that guests are informed of their designated seats.

#### **Upgrades and Door Sales**
- **Love Seat Upgrade:** Guests may upgrade to a Love Seat (if available) by purchasing a bottle of champagne for $200. Standard admission guests can upgrade to a closer seat by purchasing the Prix Fixe meal onsite for $95. 
- **Ticket Sales:** Door tickets are sold under the "Door" tab. Ensure you note the price difference for Saturday tickets.

#### **Flow of Service**
- **Transaction Closure:** Close out each transaction at the table as ordered.
- **Last Call:** Last call for food is announced after Cleopatra’s solo. Last call for drinks is during the Titanic intro.
- **Prix Fixe Service:** 
  - Prepaid Prix Fixe: Ask guests if they would like to start with a course of caviar. Ring in the meal using the “Prepaid Prix Fixe” tab, ensuring you discount the entree while collecting tax and gratuity.
  - Onsite Prix Fixe: Guests can purchase a Prix Fixe meal on site without needing a discount.
  - Off-Menu Item: Cheesecake can be offered as an a la carte item for $14 per slice.
  - **Allergen Information:** Be aware of allergen lists for Prix Fixe and a la carte items to ensure guest safety.

#### **Pre-Show Preparation**
- **Bartenders:** Stock the bar, batch cocktails, and assist with dining room setup as needed.
- **Servers:** Pre-roll silverware in napkins and place extra forks at the kitchen counter. Pre-set champagne buckets and polished flutes on Love Seat tables.
- **Production Support:** Set up stanchions, organize tables, and check table stability.

#### **Post-Show Duties**
- **Bartenders:** Deep clean bars, adjust bottled water inventory, polish silverware, glasses, and plates, and perform a Sunday inventory of liquor and juices.
- **Servers:** Wipe down chairs and tables, sanitize between shows, and bleach after the final show. Polish all service items.
- **Production Support:** Wipe down menus, flip chairs for mopping, refill salt and pepper shakers, and bring stanchions inside.

These updates ensure consistency and quality in our operations, providing guests with an exceptional experience at The Vault Speakeasy. Please adhere strictly to these guidelines.
