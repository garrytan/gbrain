## Assistant

The Dutch word for "thank you" is **"dank je"** or **"dank u"** (formal).

---

## User

Pronunciation?

---

## Assistant

"Dank je" is pronounced as:

**"dank"**: similar to the English word "dunk"  
**"je"**: sounds like "yuh" (short and soft)

So, "dank je" would sound like **"dunk yuh"**.

For "dank u" (formal):

**"u"**: pronounced like "oo" in "boot"

So, "dank u" would sound like **"dunk oo"**.
