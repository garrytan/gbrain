---

## Assistant

The P Line, also known as the phonogram copyright line, identifies who owns the sound recording. For your record label, "The Bubble Gum Factory," the P Line should include the year of the recording and the name of the owner. For example:

**P Line:** ℗ 2024 The Bubble Gum Factory

## Assistant

The P Line specifically denotes the copyright holder and year of the recording, so it's best to stick with the conventional format to avoid confusion. If "The Bubble Gum Factory" owns the recording, it would be better to use the standard P Line format such as:

**P Line:** ℗ 2024 The Bubble Gum Factory
