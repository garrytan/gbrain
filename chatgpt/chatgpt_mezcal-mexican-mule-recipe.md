## Assistant

Here is a recipe for a Mezcal Mexican Mule:

**Ingredients:**
- 2 oz Mezcal
- 0.5 oz fresh lime juice
- 4 oz ginger beer
- Ice
- Lime wedge for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Fill a mule mug or a highball glass with ice.
2. Pour in the mezcal and fresh lime juice.
3. Top with ginger beer.
4. Stir gently to mix.
5. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Enjoy your Mezcal Mexican Mule!

## Assistant

Here is a recipe for a Pineapple Mezcal Margarita with a Tajín garnish:

**Ingredients:**
- 2 oz Mezcal
- 1 oz pineapple juice
- 1 oz fresh lime juice
- 0.5 oz Cointreau or Triple Sec
- 0.5 oz simple syrup (optional, depending on desired sweetness)
- Tajín seasoning
- Lime wedge for rimming the glass
- Ice
- Pineapple slice or lime wedge for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Use a lime wedge to moisten the rim of your glass. Dip the rim in Tajín seasoning to coat.
2. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
3. Add mezcal, pineapple juice, lime juice, Cointreau (or Triple Sec), and simple syrup (if using) to the shaker.
4. Shake well until the mixture is chilled.
5. Fill the prepared glass with ice and strain the cocktail into the glass.
6. Garnish with a pineapple slice or a lime wedge.

Enjoy your Pineapple Mezcal Margarita with a Tajín garnish!
