When formatting lyrics according to Apple Music's guidelines, it's essential to pay attention to their requirements for clarity, structure, and readability. This includes capitalizing the first letter of each line, separating sections clearly, and ensuring punctuation is used consistently. Here's how your lyrics can be formatted to meet these guidelines:

Did you break free from it all instead?

Love, don't let me down.

**Verse 2 (V2)**

I don't need to know what you have found.

This format keeps your lyrics organized and accessible, making it easier for readers or musicians to follow along. Remember, the key elements in adhering to Apple Music's guidelines include proper section labels, initial capitalization, and clear distinction between different parts of the song.
