## User

Yo dawg, what's up? So I'm looking at the sales breakdown in Toast for December of last year, and it's only giving me sales by category. Where do I spit out a report, like, of items within a specific category?

---

## Assistant

In Toast you can drill down to item-level sales pretty easily:

1. **Log in to the Toast Web Back-Office** (not the POS app).  
2. Go to **Reports → Sales → Menu Item Sales** (sometimes labeled **Product Mix**).  
3. In the filters at the top:  
   * Set the **Date Range** to December of last year.  
   * Under **Categories**, select the specific category you care about.  
4. Click **Apply/Run Report.**  
5. Use the **Export** button (CSV or PDF) if you need a spreadsheet.

That report lists every individual menu item within the chosen category, with quantity sold, gross/net sales, modifiers, etc.
