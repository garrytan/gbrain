## Assistant

To calculate the weight you need to reach to have 8% body fat without losing any muscle mass, we can use the following formula:

1. Calculate your lean body mass (LBM), which is your total weight minus the weight of your body fat. This is calculated as follows:
   - \(LBM = Total\ Weight \times (1 - Current\ Body\ Fat\ Percentage)\)

2. Calculate the target weight, which is the weight you need to be at your target body fat percentage, using your LBM:
   - \(Target\ Weight = \frac{LBM}{1 - Target\ Body\ Fat\ Percentage}\)
