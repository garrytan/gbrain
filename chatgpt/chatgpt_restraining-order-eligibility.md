### 📜 **California Civil Harassment Restraining Order (CHRO) – Eligibility**
To qualify, the behavior must involve:
- **Harassment** (unlawful violence, a credible threat of violence, or a pattern of conduct that seriously alarms, annoys, or harasses you *and* serves no legitimate purpose),  
- And would cause a **reasonable person to suffer substantial emotional distress.**

You **don’t** need to prove physical violence or threats. **Stalking**, repeated unwanted contact, showing up at your workplace or home, and obsessive social media behavior can count — especially if you’ve made it clear you're not interested and they persist.
