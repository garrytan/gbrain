### Why the Costco “Genius Gourmet” thing tastes eerily similar
That part is way less mysterious than it feels.

- Genius Gourmet’s clear protein drinks list the same **acid stack** (malic/citric/phosphoric) and similar formulation approach. citeturn0search2turn1view1  
- Those acids (plus preservatives like sodium benzoate / potassium sorbate) are **standard tools** for:
  - making “clear protein” taste like juice/soda,
  - keeping proteins soluble,
  - and staying shelf-stable.  
- So “tastes the same” usually means **same flavor profile + same formulation constraints**, not necessarily the same company or “white label master product.” (Could be same co-packer or flavor house, but you can’t conclude that from ingredients alone.)

Also: Genius Gourmet and Pure Genius are **not the same brand** in any public way I can substantiate from what we pulled; what’s common is that they’re both playing in the “clear protein beverage” lane.
