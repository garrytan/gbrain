Mr. White — **this is absolutely a scam.** Hard red flags:

**1) Real production jobs NEVER ask the performer for personal payment details before scheduling.**  
Legit studios book you first, then pay you **after** the session.

**2) The “partial payment upfront” gimmick is the classic Craigslist voice-over scam.**  
They send a fake check → you deposit it → your bank credits it temporarily → later it bounces → **you owe the bank**, and the scammer pressures you to “refund” or “pay vendors.” Total loss.

**3) Refusing to share script until after payment is nonsense.**  
Industry norm: you see script, NDA if needed, signed deal memo, scheduling, studio call sheet.

No real studio does that. Ever.
