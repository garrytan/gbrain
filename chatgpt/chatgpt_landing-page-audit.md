## 🚨 Core Issues (what to fix)

#### 1) **Weak above-the-fold clarity**
Visitors should understand *immediately* what Jovie does and why it matters within ~5 seconds. Ideally:
- Strong headline that communicates the benefit (“AI link-in-bio for musicians that grows your fanbase”)  
- A supporting subheadline with specific outcome: what artists *get* (email/SMS subscribers, smart links, analytics).  
Right now the value prop is *functional* but not sharp or *benefit-driven*.citeturn0search3turn0search5

#### 2) **Missing quantifiable credibility**
You list logos (“Trusted by…”), which is good. But add real metrics or testimonials:  
✔ # of artists using Jovie  
✔ average increases in newsletter signups/streams  
✔ real quotes from musicians  
Social proof accelerates trust and lowers resistance.citeturn0search0

#### 3) **Call-to-Action needs hierarchy**
There’s an above-the-fold CTA (“Search Spotify…”), but it’s weak because it doesn’t signal urgency or benefit. Focus on a **single, dominant CTA above the fold**, with bold color contrast and concise action text (e.g., “Get Your Artist Page Live in 30s”).citeturn0search18

#### 4) **Navigation distracts conversion**
Landing pages convert best when distractions are minimized. Consider removing or de-emphasizing unnecessary nav links in the hero to keep focus on your single conversion goal.citeturn0search17

#### 5) **Visual hierarchy & cognitive ease**
Use strong visuals (mockups of artist pages, screenshots of analytics, etc.) that reinforce the copy. Break content into digestible chunks with bullets, contrast, and whitespace so it’s easy to scan.citeturn0search3
