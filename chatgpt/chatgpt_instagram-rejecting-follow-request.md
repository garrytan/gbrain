What's the best way to clean inside of a humidifier?

---

## Assistant

To clean the inside of a humidifier, follow these steps:

1. Unplug the humidifier and disassemble it.
2. Remove any mineral deposits or scale with a 3% hydrogen peroxide solution or white vinegar. Let it soak for 30 minutes, then scrub gently with a brush.
3. Rinse all the parts thoroughly with water to remove any cleaning solution.
4. Dry all components completely before reassembling.
5. Regular maintenance like this helps prevent mold and bacteria buildup.

Explain the hydrogen peroxide solution.

---

## Assistant

The hydrogen peroxide solution used for cleaning is typically a 3% concentration, which is commonly available at drugstores. It's a mild antiseptic and disinfectant, effective at killing bacteria, viruses, and fungi. When used in cleaning a humidifier, it helps remove mineral deposits and sanitize the parts. It's important to rinse the humidifier parts thoroughly after using hydrogen peroxide to ensure no residue is left that could be released into the air when the humidifier is in use.
