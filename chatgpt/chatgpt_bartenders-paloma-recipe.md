## Assistant

Certainly, Mr. White. Here's a concise recipe for a Paloma, as a bartender would typically prepare it:

**Ingredients:**
- 2 oz tequila (preferably blanco)
- 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
- Grapefruit soda (like Jarritos or Squirt) to top
- Salt for rimming the glass (optional)
- Grapefruit slice or lime wedge for garnish (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. If desired, rim a highball glass with salt: rub a lime wedge around the edge and dip in salt.
2. Fill the glass with ice.
3. Pour in the tequila and lime juice.
4. Top with grapefruit soda.
5. Gently stir to combine.
6. Garnish with a slice of grapefruit or a lime wedge, if using.

This recipe provides a balance of citrus and sweetness, typical of a classic Paloma.
