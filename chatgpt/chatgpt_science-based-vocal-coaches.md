**Estill Voice Training (EVT)**, created by the late **Josephine “Jo” Estill**, is a highly respected system that approaches singing and speaking with scientific precision. Her legacy, Estill Voice Training, is all about breaking vocal technique down to its anatomical components. The motto is **“Knowledge is power”**: by understanding and controlling the physical structures that produce your voice, you gain complete artistic freedom【33†L58-L66】. This method is essentially first-principles singing – you train the building blocks (like larynx position, false vocal folds, tongue, etc.) individually, which rapidly improves your vocal ability across styles.

This separation of **the science (mechanics) from the art (styling)** means Estill training gives you *tangible control*: you’re not relying on vague imagery or solely imitating a teacher’s sound【33†L31-L39】【33†L58-L66】.
