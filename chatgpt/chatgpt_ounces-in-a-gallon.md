## User

How many ounces in a gallon?

---

## Assistant

There are **128 ounces in a gallon** (U.S. liquid measure).  

Here’s the breakdown:
- 1 gallon = 4 quarts  
- 1 quart = 2 pints  
- 1 pint = 16 ounces  
→ **4 × 2 × 16 = 128 ounces per gallon**  

If you meant an **imperial (U.K.) gallon**, that equals **160 ounces**.
