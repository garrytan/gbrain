## The content mix

Your grid should stay photo-forward, but growth will come from Reels. Instagram says Reels ranking considers viewer activity such as likes, saves, reshares, comments, and engagement history; Instagram also has creator guidance around creation, engagement, reach, and algorithms. citeturn239868search1turn239868search2turn239868search4

Use this split:

- **40% editorial photos / carousels**  
  Keeps the grid premium.

- **40% Reels**  
  Drives reach and new followers.

- **10% founder essays / carousels**  
  Builds authority around Jovie and creator economy.

- **10% Stories / behind-the-scenes / live dispatches**  
  Builds intimacy without dirtying the grid.

## Visual universe

| Travel | black suitcase, white hotel sheets, gray airport, black car |
| Skin | real warmth preserved; environment stays neutral |
| Color | rare and intentional; Jovie/Bubblegum accent appears like a signal |

### Retouching rules

- Remove color casts from walls, floors, ceilings, furniture.
- Preserve natural skin tone.
- Keep blacks rich, not crushed.
- Keep whites clean, not blown out.
- No cheap teal/orange.
- No fake HDR.
- No “AI plastic skin.”
- Make the photo feel expensive before it feels futuristic.
