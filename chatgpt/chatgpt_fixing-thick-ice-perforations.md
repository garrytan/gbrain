## Assistant

To address the issue with your industrial ice maker where the sheets of ice cubes are not breaking easily due to thick perforations, you could try the following methods:

1. **Adjust the Ice Thickness Control:** If your ice maker has a control for adjusting the thickness of the ice, try setting it to make thinner sheets. This might make the perforations more effective.

2. **Manual Separation:** Use a clean and sanitized tool like an ice pick or a sturdy spatula to gently break the sheets into individual cubes. Ensure that the tool is appropriate for use with food and won't damage the ice maker.

3. **Regular Maintenance:** Sometimes, scale build-up or other issues can affect the performance of an ice maker. Regular cleaning and descaling might help in ensuring the perforations work as intended.

4. **Consult the Manufacturer:** Check the manufacturer's manual for troubleshooting tips or contact their customer service for advice. There might be specific recommendations for your model.

5. **Professional Service:** If the above steps don't work, it might be best to get a professional to service the machine. They can check for any mechanical issues or adjustments needed.

Always ensure that any method you use complies with food safety standards and doesn't damage your equipment.
