## Do **not** sell it as one giant kit first

Split the **body** and **lens**. Bundle the cheap accessories only as sweeteners.

A buyer looking for an **A7 III body** may already own lenses. A buyer looking for a **Sigma 85mm f/1.4 Art** may already own a Sony body. Selling the whole pile together shrinks the buyer pool and makes the listing feel like a $1,500+ commitment instead of a clean deal.
