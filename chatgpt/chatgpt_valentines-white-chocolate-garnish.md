## Assistant

For a Valentine's Day theme, consider garnishing with a strawberry cut into a heart shape, a small skewer of raspberries, or a rose petal on top for a romantic touch. A red or pink sugar rim on the glass could also add a festive and sweet detail.

## User

## Assistant

Certainly! For a Valentine's themed cocktail, you could also use:

1. Edible flowers, like violets or rose petals, for an elegant touch.
2. A chocolate-dipped strawberry on the rim of the glass.
3. A heart-shaped piece of white chocolate as a stirrer.
4. A sprinkle of edible gold dust for a luxurious finish.
5. A ribbon of red or pink curled zest (from an orange or grapefruit) for a pop of color.
