## User

I’m 13 % bf at 178.5 lbs. what do I need to get to weight wise to be at 8% bf assuming no muscle loss

---

## Assistant

To calculate your target weight at 8% body fat, we can use the following formula:

\[ \text{Target Weight} = \frac{\text{Lean Body Mass}}{1 - \text{Desired Body Fat Percentage}} \]

First, we need to determine your current Lean Body Mass (LBM):

\[ \text{LBM} = \text{Current Weight} \times (1 - \text{Current Body Fat Percentage}) \]

Given:
- Current Weight = 178.5 lbs
- Current Body Fat Percentage = 13% (0.13)

\[ \text{LBM} = 178.5 \times (1 - 0.13) = 178.5 \times 0.87 = 155.295 \text{ lbs} \]

Now, calculate the target weight at 8% body fat (0.08):

\[ \text{Target Weight} = \frac{155.295}{1 - 0.08} = \frac{155.295}{0.92} \approx 168.8 \text{ lbs} \]

So, you would need to weigh approximately 168.8 lbs to be at 8% body fat, assuming no muscle loss.
