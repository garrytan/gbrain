## User

What's the best way to clean air force 1 sneakers that are all white

---

## Assistant

To clean white Air Force 1 sneakers effectively:

1. **Remove Excess Dirt:** Gently tap the sneakers together and use a soft-bristled brush (like a toothbrush) to brush off any loose dirt.
  
2. **Prepare Cleaning Solution:** Mix a small amount of mild laundry detergent with warm water.

3. **Clean with a Soft Cloth or Brush:** Dip a soft cloth or a soft-bristled brush into the cleaning solution and gently scrub the sneakers. Pay extra attention to stains and heavily soiled areas.

4. **Wipe Off Soap:** Use a damp cloth to wipe off any soap residue.

5. **Air Dry:** Let the sneakers air dry at room temperature. Avoid direct sunlight or heat sources, as they can warp the sneakers or affect the color.

6. **Clean Laces Separately:** Remove the laces and wash them separately in the cleaning solution. Rinse thoroughly and let them air dry before re-lacing the sneakers.

For stubborn stains, a specialized sneaker cleaner or a gentle stain remover can be used, but it's important to test it on a small, inconspicuous area first to ensure it doesn't affect the color or material.
