Here’s how to install a **TN229XL toner cartridge** in your Brother **MFC-L3720CDW**:

---

### 1. Prepare
- Make sure the machine is **powered on**.
- Place it on a flat, stable surface.
- Have the new TN229XL cartridge ready (leave it sealed until right before installation).

---

### 2. Open the Printer
- Open the **front cover** of the MFC-L3720CDW.  
- Pull out the **drum unit and toner cartridge assembly** for the color you’re replacing.

---

### 3. Remove Old Cartridge
- Press the **green lock lever** on the side of the drum unit.  
- Lift the old toner cartridge straight out.

---

### 4. Prepare New Cartridge
- Remove the TN229XL from its packaging.  
- Gently **rock it side to side** a few times to distribute toner evenly inside.  
- Pull off the protective cover.

---

### 5. Install New Cartridge
- Insert the new toner into the drum unit until it **clicks into place**.  
- Make sure the green lock lever lifts automatically, securing the cartridge.

---

### 6. Reinstall the Assembly
- Slide the drum/toner assembly back into the printer.  
- Close the front cover firmly.

---

### 7. Reset (if needed)
The printer should automatically recognize the new cartridge. If it doesn’t:
- Go to the printer’s **Menu → Machine Info → Parts Life → Toner**.  
- Select the color you replaced and choose **Yes** to reset.
