## Assistant

Mr. White, here’s a concise, room-by-room 3-week plan to declutter and pack efficiently:

---

**1. DECLUTTER (Days 1–4)**  
- **3-box method** in every space:  
  - **Keep** (daily-use)  
  - **Donate/Sell** (good condition)  
  - **Trash/Recycle** (broken, expired)  
- Tackle least-used areas first (closet → bookshelves → kitchen cabinets → office).

---

**2. GATHER SUPPLIES (Days 2–5)**  
- **Boxes**: small (books, gear), medium (kitchenware), large (pillows, linens)  
- **Protect**: bubble wrap, packing paper  
- **Organize**: packing tape, labels, colored markers, Ziploc bags (for screws/cables)  
- **Furniture**: furniture blankets, rope/straps

---

**3. PACK ROOM BY ROOM (Days 5–14)**  
- **Office/Desk (see photo)**  
  - Empty drawers → sort contents into 3-box.  
  - Wrap monitor, speakers, mic in bubble; use original boxes if you have them.  
  - Coil and label every cable (e.g. “desk → monitor HDMI”).  
  - Disassemble mounts/legs; bag screws with a label (“desk leg screws”).  
- **Kitchen**: pack rarely used appliances first.  
- **Closet**: bag off-season clothes.  
- **Living areas**: start with décor and extras.

---

**4. LABEL & INVENTORY (Days 6–15)**  
- **Color-code** per room (blue=bedroom, green=kitchen…)  
- **Numbered boxes** + master spreadsheet: box # → contents → destination  
- **“First-night” box**: toiletries, chargers, a change of clothes

---

**5. DISPOSE & FINALIZE (Days 12–21)**  
- Schedule donation/drop-off for “Donate/Sell” pile  
- Bulk-trash or recycling pick-up for “Trash” pile  
- Final sweep: wipe down empty rooms, confirm nothing’s left behind

---

Stay on schedule, tick off each day’s tasks, and you’ll move out clean, lean, and stress-free.
