## Assistant

Mr. White, if you're pursuing a third-party claim against the other driver's GEICO policy, you can check the status a few ways:

### Option 1: Call GEICO Claims
**Phone:** 1-800-841-3000

Tell them:
- You are a third-party claimant.
- You have the claim number (if you were given one).
- Or provide the other driver's name, date of loss, and location of the accident.

### Option 2: GEICO Claims Portal
GEICO has an online claims system:

urlGEICO Claims Centerhttps://www.geico.com/claims/

Some third-party claimants can view claim status online after verifying identity and claim information.

### Option 3: If You Don't Have the Claim Number
Have ready:
- Police report number
- Date/time of accident
- Other driver's name
- Other driver's GEICO policy number (if available)
- Your vehicle information

GEICO can usually locate the claim from that information.

### Your Situation
Since:
- Your Mazda was parked.
- The other driver struck it.
- Police responded and prepared a report.

GEICO should have opened a liability investigation. Ask:
1. Has liability been accepted?
2. Has an adjuster been assigned?
3. What is the claim number?
4. Can they authorize repairs yet?
5. Is rental reimbursement available through the at-fault driver's coverage?

If you already reported the accident to your own GEICO, they may also be able to see the third-party claim and give you the claim number.
