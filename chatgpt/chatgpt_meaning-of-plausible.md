## Assistant

**Plausible** means something that *seems reasonable, believable, or likely true* based on the information available — though it isn’t guaranteed to be true. citeturn0search1turn0search0

Examples:
- A *plausible explanation* is one that could reasonably explain something. citeturn0search5  
- A *plausible excuse* is believable enough that someone might accept it. citeturn0search1

It can also describe a person who *appears honest or convincing* even if they aren’t. citeturn0search4
