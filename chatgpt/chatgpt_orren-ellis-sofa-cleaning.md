### Before you start (important)

- **Test first** on a hidden area. Chenille can darken or flatten if over-wet.

### Correct method (do this exactly)
1. **Vacuum dry first**  
   Remove all loose debris with the dry upholstery attachment.

2. **Light detergent only**  
   Use a **mild, water-based upholstery cleaner** (low suds). Less is more.

3. **Minimal spray**  
   Lightly mist the fabric — **do not soak**. Chenille hates saturation.

4. **Gentle agitation**  
   If needed, use a **soft brush**, short strokes, no pressure.

5. **Extract immediately**  
   Pull the water out right away. Make **multiple slow passes** until the fabric feels barely damp.

6. **Dry fast**
   - Open windows or use a fan
   - No heat, no hair dryers
   - Do not sit on it until fully dry (usually 6–12 hours)

### What NOT to do
- ❌ Steam mode  
- ❌ Hot water  
- ❌ Repeated passes in the same spot  
- ❌ Scrubbing hard  
- ❌ Leaving it damp (causes water marks and pile distortion)

### Pro tip (chenille-specific)
