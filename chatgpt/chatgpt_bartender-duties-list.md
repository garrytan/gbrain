## Assistant

For a comprehensive list of opening and closing duties for bartenders, you can consider the following tasks, categorized for clarity:

### Opening Duties

1. **Stock Glassware**: Ensure all types of glasses needed for the day are clean and adequately stocked at each station.
2. **Get Ice**: Fill ice bins at all bar stations to the required level, ensuring there's enough for service start.
3. **Uncap All Bottles in Wells**: Check all bottles in the wells are uncapped for easy access. Ensure bottles are full; marry and replace bottles if any are less than half full to maintain quality and consistency.
4. **Prepare Sanitation Stations**: Set up sani buckets and sprays at each bar station for cleaning surfaces and tools.
5. **Stock All Mixers**: Ensure all mixers, including sodas, juices, and syrups, are fully stocked and easily accessible.
6. **Equip Bar Stations**: Make sure every bar station has 2 clean shaker cups, a strainer, and other essential bartending tools like jiggers, muddlers, and bar spoons.

### Additional Opening Preparations

7. **Check Garnishes**: Prep and stock all necessary garnishes in sufficient quantities.
8. **Verify POS System**: Ensure the POS system is up and running smoothly.
9. **Review Inventory**: Quick inventory check to confirm availability of all spirits, beers, and wines.
10. **Set Up Bar Mats and Towels**: Place clean bar mats and towels at each station for a tidy workspace.
11. **Confirm Specials**: Be aware of any daily specials or promotions and understand the recipes and presentation.

### Closing Duties

1. **Clean Glassware**: Wash and polish all used glassware.
2. **Drain Ice Bins**: Empty out ice bins and clean to prevent contamination.
3. **Secure Bottles**: Cap all bottles in wells and secure any open bottles to avoid spills and ensure freshness.
4. **Restock Mixers and Garnishes**: Replenish any mixers and garnishes used during the shift.
5. **Sanitize Bar Stations**: Thoroughly clean and sanitize all surfaces, including bar tops, stations, and tools.
6. **Dispose of Trash**: Empty all trash bins and remove any debris from the bar area.
7. **Secure Alcohol and Valuables**: Lock up expensive liquors and secure the bar area.
8. **Final Inventory Check**: Note any stock depletion or requirements for the next shift.
9. **Turn Off Equipment**: Ensure all electronics, lights, and music are turned off.
10. **Lock Up**: Secure the bar, including doors and windows.

This comprehensive list should help maintain a smooth operation, ensuring your bar is always ready for business and closed securely at the end of the night.
