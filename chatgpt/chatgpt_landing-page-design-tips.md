## 1. Minimal SaaS Conversion (Stripe-level clarity)

image_group{"layout":"carousel","aspect_ratio":"16:9","query":["modern SaaS landing page hero section minimal","Stripe homepage design hero section","clean product landing page with gradient background","SaaS landing page social proof section logos"],"num_per_query":1}

**Best for:** B2B, SaaS, fintech, AI tools  

**Structure**
1. **Hero**
   - Clear outcome-driven headline  
   - 1-sentence subhead (who it’s for + core benefit)  
   - Primary CTA + secondary CTA  
   - Simple product visual (UI mock or animation)

2. **Social Proof**
   - Recognizable logos  
   - 1–2 short testimonials with metrics

3. **Problem → Solution**
   - Agitate core pain  
   - Show product in action

4. **Feature Blocks**
   - Benefit-led headers (not feature names)  
   - Visual + 3 bullet outcomes

5. **Results Section**
   - Hard metrics (%, $, time saved)

6. **Final CTA**
   - Friction reducer (no credit card, free trial, etc.)

**Design traits**
- Large typography  
- Strong whitespace  
- Subtle gradients  
- Clean sans-serif (Inter / SF Pro style)  
- Motion on scroll

**Best for:** Hardware, luxury, high-ticket offers  

**Structure**
- Full-screen hero with bold statement  
- Cinematic product shots  
- Scroll-triggered storytelling  
- Minimal copy  
- One dominant CTA  

**Design traits**
- Dark backgrounds  
- High contrast imagery  
- Slow, intentional animations  
- Large margins  
- Confident, short headlines

# What Makes a Landing Page “World Class”

**1. Message-Market Fit**
- Headline speaks directly to ICP  
- Clear transformation  

**2. Visual Hierarchy**
- One dominant CTA  
- 2–3 font sizes max  
- Scannable sections  

**3. Speed**
- Loads under 2 seconds  
- Optimized images  
- Minimal bloat  

**4. Trust Signals**
- Logos  
- Case studies  
- Metrics  
- Clear pricing  

**5. Ruthless Clarity**
- No jargon  
- No multiple offers  
- No vague benefits
