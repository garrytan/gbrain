### FOH Restaurant Opening and Operation Checklist

#### Pre-Shift (30 Minutes Before Lobby Open)

1. **Main Performance Space Setup**
   - Set up all tables with lamps, check batteries, and turn them on.
   - Place number cards in the center of each table.
   - Arrange chairs in the correct spots, ensuring all tables line up.
   - Check tables for any wobble and adjust feet if necessary.
   - Sweep and mop the main room (porter duty).

2. **NoMan's Land Setup**
   - Stock with 4 bottles of sani spray.
   - Ensure a supply of clean towels.
   - Ensure clean bus bins, dump buckets, and trash cans with trash bags are available.
   - Roll silverware (PAs, with server assistance if they have time). Roll-ups will be done in NoMan's Land and ready to go before the show.
   - Bring glassware from the kitchen to NoMan's Land, polish, and load into trays to bring to the bars for stocking.

3. **Lobby Bar Setup (On Staff Arrival)**
   - Ensure all 4 wells are stocked with:
     - Tequila, Vodka, Rum, Gin, Triple Sec, Jack Daniels
   - Ensure all 2 premium wells are stocked with:
     - Grey Goose, Hendricks, Woodford Reserve, Casamigos, Baileys, Kahlua
   - Ensure each bar has 1 backup bottle of each well liquor under it, restocked nightly.
   - Fully stock mixers at the bar.
   - Set up mis en place at the bar (cut fruit garnishes, prepare mixers, etc.).
   - Stock the bar with clean glassware (check for smudges or water spots).
   - Place 2 dump buckets and 1 clean bus bin under each bar.
   - Ensure all 7 batched cocktails are placed in the specialty well (the metal one in the center):
     - Epstein Island Breeze in Tito's bottles
     - Lewinsky Lemonade in carafes
     - Ruth Bader Ginsberg in Tanqueray bottles
     - Grand Old Fashioned in Jack Daniels bottles
     - Russian Collusion in speed pourers
     - Pineapple Margarita in carafes
     - Watergate in tequila bottles
   - Ensure there are at least 6 bottles of each batched cocktail in the bar fridges.
   - Bartender takes inventory at the start of the night, noting any drink with less than 6 bottles and flagging it for additional batching.
   - Ensure each bar has:
     - Clean mats
     - Fully stocked straws and bev naps
     - Fully stocked mixers
     - Fully stocked well
     - Fully stocked batch cocktails
     - 1 ice scoop
     - 1 strainer
     - 1 wine key
     - 1 champagne stopper
     - Fully stocked garnish tray
   - Check each Clover device at each bar for paper in the receipt printer and the service printer.

4. **Testing Equipment**
   - Each server fires off a test print to the bar and one to the kitchen pre-show.

#### Bartenders in Place (10 Minutes Before Lobby Doors Open)

1. **Final Preparations**
   - Bartenders get in place.
   - Ensure everything is ready for service.

#### Lobby Doors Open (1 Hour Before Show Start)

1. **During the Show**
   - **Bartender Duties**
     - Continually wipe up the bar top and customer-facing surfaces.
     - Replace any sticky or gross bar mats immediately with fresh ones.
     - Overcut fruit when possible and store extra in Cambros.
     - Place Sour Patch Kids in a sealed Cambro at end of night and do not refrigerate; other garnishes can go in the fridge.
     - Ensure a single ticket stabber is between the 2 large bars.
     - Restock glassware if anyone has downtime mid-show.
   - **Server Duties**
     - Ensure tables are bussed and wiped down as needed.
     - Assist PAs with rolling silverware if they have time.
     - Restock glassware if anyone has downtime mid-show.
   - **Porter Duties**
     - Empty bus bins and dump buckets throughout the night by taking them to the kitchen and bringing back fresh ones.
     - Empty trash as it gets full and do a final emptying at the end of each show (1 or 2 shows per night).

2. **When "Stones" Goes On**
   - Bartender closes lobby bar if it’s the last show of the night, bar team begins resetting and cleaning bar.

#### End of Night Procedures

1. **Main Performance Space Cleanup**
   - Bus the room, removing all dirty dishes and trash (server duty).
   - Wipe down all tables (server duty).
   - Collect menus and have PAs wipe them down.
   - Strike number cards and lamps on Sundays only. Set them up again if they aren't already set.

2. **Bar Cleanup**
   - Restock and clean the bar area (server duty).
   - Ensure all glassware is cleaned and stored properly (server duty).
   - Wipe down the Clover screens.
   - Place any sticky or gross bar mats in a bus bin and replace them with fresh bar mats.
   - Dump out all salt and Tajín rim trays and place them in bus bins.
   - Store Sour Patch Kids in a sealed Cambro and do not refrigerate; refrigerate other garnishes.
   - Place bar mats that got sticky or gross in a bus bin and immediately replace them with fresh bar mats.
   - Fully stock glassware pre-show and post-show.

3. **NoMan's Land Organization**
   - Restock and organize all supplies for the next day.
   - PAs wipe menus and keep NoMan's Land organized.
   - Ensure all silverware roll-ups are completed and ready for the next day.

4. **Server Duties**
   - Place Clover devices on chargers at the end of the night.
   - Wipe down the screens of the Clover devices.

This comprehensive checklist ensures that all areas, including the bar, NoMan's Land, and Main Performance Space, are set up correctly and efficiently, providing a seamless experience for customers and staff.
