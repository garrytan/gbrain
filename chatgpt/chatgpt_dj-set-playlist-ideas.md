# 90-Minute After-Hours Rave Playlist (Melodic Tech House Vibes)

## Set Overview 
This 90-minute playlist is crafted for a **late-night/early-morning after-hours rave in LA**, keeping a **tech house foundation** that blends smoothly with other DJs while injecting **interesting melodic and progressive elements**. The set starts around **124 BPM** and gradually builds to about **130 BPM**, maintaining energy without becoming too dark or commercial. The vibe stays **engaging and hypnotic** – perfect for a West Coast crowd catching a second wind in the early hours. We’ve avoided any obvious pop remixes or overplayed vocals (no Lady Gaga mashups here), as well as those hype DJ shout edits. Instead, the focus is on **fresh, underground tracks** with groove and melody, subtle vocals at most, and a consistent flow that complements a tech-house lineup.

## Track Selection & Flow 
- **Warm-up / Groove Building (124–126 BPM):** The set opens with deep, melodic grooves to hook the crowd without overwhelming. These tracks set a trippy **after-hours mood** with rich instrumentals and subtle vocals.  
- **Mid-Set Energy (126–128 BPM):** Gradually, the playlist shifts into punchier tech house rollers and progressive house tunes. The basslines get funkier and the percussion more driving, keeping dancers locked in. Melodic hooks and atmospheric elements ensure it never turns too dark.  
- **Peak Vibe (128–130 BPM):** In the final third, the energy peaks with **melodic tech-house anthems** that feel uplifting and euphoric (yet still underground). The synth melodies soar but are balanced by chunky beats. We avoid anything overly commercial – these climax tracks are big in the club scene, not on pop radio.  
- **Closing Feel (129–130 BPM):** The last track or two maintain the energy but add an emotive touch – perfect for that **5 AM magic moment**. The crowd should feel energized yet connected by melody as the set concludes.

Throughout the set, the **transitions are harmonic** and smooth, ensuring the mix flows naturally for an immersive journey. Now onto the tracks!
