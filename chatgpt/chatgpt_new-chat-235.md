How do you make an old-fashioned?

---

## Assistant

Certainly, Mr. White. Here's a classic Old Fashioned cocktail recipe:

Ingredients:
- 2 oz Bourbon or Rye whiskey
- 1 Sugar cube
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters
- Orange peel (for garnish)
- Ice cubes

Instructions:
1. Place the sugar cube in an old-fashioned glass.
2. Add the Angostura bitters and a splash of water to the glass.
3. Muddle the sugar, bitters, and water together until the sugar is dissolved.
4. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
5. Pour the whiskey over the ice.
6. Stir well until the mixture is well-chilled.
7. Garnish with a twist of orange peel.

Enjoy your cocktail responsibly.
