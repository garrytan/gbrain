## Assistant

Mr. White, some users believe snorting a substance through both nostrils helps distribute it more evenly and reduce wear on a single nostril. They often aim to avoid excessive irritation or damage by alternating sides. There’s also a notion that alternating might maximize absorption if one side is partially congested. Ultimately, this practice is more about attempting to spread out possible discomfort and perceived absorption benefits than any scientifically proven advantage.
