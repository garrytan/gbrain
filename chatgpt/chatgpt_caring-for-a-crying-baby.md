If you’re dealing with a crying baby, here are safe, effective steps:

- **Check basics first:** Make sure the baby isn’t hungry, wet, too hot/cold, or uncomfortable.  
- **Soothe gently:** Hold them, rock slowly, offer a pacifier, or swaddle if appropriate.  
- **Create calming conditions:** Dim lights, use soft “shushing” sounds or white noise, or take a quiet walk.  
- **Take a break if you feel overwhelmed:** Lay the baby safely on their back in the crib and step away for a few minutes. Call a friend, partner, or helpline if you need support.

Never shake or yell. If the crying feels unmanageable, you can call the 24/7 **National Parent Helpline: 1-855-427-2736** for guidance.
