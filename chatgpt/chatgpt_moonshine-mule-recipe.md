## Assistant

A classic moonshine drink perfect for a Prohibition-themed bar is the "Moonshine Mule." Here's a simple recipe:

### Moonshine Mule

**Ingredients:**
- 2 oz. moonshine
- 1/2 oz. fresh lime juice
- 4 oz. ginger beer
- Lime wedge for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Fill a mule mug or highball glass with ice.
2. Add moonshine and lime juice.
3. Top with ginger beer and stir gently.
4. Garnish with a lime wedge.

This drink combines the rustic appeal of moonshine with the refreshing bite of a traditional mule, making it a fitting choice for a Prohibition-era atmosphere.

## Assistant

Yes, a Moonshine Mule will taste different from a traditional Vodka Mule. Here's how:

1. **Base Spirit Flavor**:
   - **Moonshine**: Typically has a stronger, more robust flavor with a slight corn sweetness, reflecting its raw and unaged nature.
   - **Vodka**: Generally smooth and neutral, allowing other ingredients to shine.

2. **Overall Taste**:
   - The Moonshine Mule will have a more pronounced and slightly harsh character due to the moonshine's distinct flavor.
   - A Vodka Mule will be smoother and more subtle, letting the lime and ginger beer dominate.

The difference in base spirits gives each version its unique twist, making the Moonshine Mule a bolder, more rustic option fitting for a Prohibition theme.
