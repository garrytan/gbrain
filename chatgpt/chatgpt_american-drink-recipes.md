2. **Mint Julep** - A Southern favorite with bourbon, fresh mint, sugar, and crushed ice.
3. **Manhattan** - A timeless drink made with rye or bourbon, sweet vermouth, and bitters.
4. **Whiskey Sour** - A refreshing mix of bourbon, lemon juice, and simple syrup.
5. **Sazerac** - A New Orleans staple with rye whiskey, absinthe, sugar, and Peychaud's bitters.
6. **Margarita** - Popular in the Southwest, made with tequila, lime juice, and triple sec.
7. **Jack and Coke** - A simple yet classic mix of Jack Daniel's Tennessee Whiskey and Coca-Cola.
8. **Hurricane** - A fruity and potent New Orleans cocktail with rum, passion fruit, and citrus.
9. **Long Island Iced Tea** - A strong drink combining vodka, gin, rum, tequila, triple sec, and cola.
10. **California Dreamin'** - A refreshing cocktail with vodka, orange juice, and grenadine.
