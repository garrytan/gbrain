## User

If I worked 65, I mean $65.40 for 3 hours and 39 minutes of work, what's my hourly?

---

## Assistant

Let's calculate carefully, step by step.

**Step 1. Convert time to hours.**  
3 hours and 39 minutes = 3 + (39 ÷ 60) hours  
39 ÷ 60 = 0.65  
So total = 3.65 hours.

**Step 2. Divide pay by hours.**  
$65.40 ÷ 3.65 = 17.9178...

**Step 3. Round properly.**  
≈ **$17.92 per hour.**

✅ **Answer:** $17.92/hour
