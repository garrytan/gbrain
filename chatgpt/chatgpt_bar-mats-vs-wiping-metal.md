## Assistant

Use **thin black bar mats** for the bottle wells. Here's why:

### Pros:
- **Faster cleanup**: Just pull and rinse the mats.
- **Protects bottles**: Cushions glass from banging against metal.
- **Improved grip**: Prevents bottles from sliding around.
- **Easier maintenance**: Spills don’t pool—drain through mats.

### Wiping metal only:
- Looks cleaner *temporarily*, but sticky residue builds up fast.
- Takes longer to scrub clean each night.

Verdict: **Use bar mats.** Wipe underneath weekly.
