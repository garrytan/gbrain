## Assistant

Mr. White, try the following steps to clear the phantom Ethernet connection:

1. **Check the port:** Inspect the Ethernet port for any debris or bent pins. Sometimes physical damage or dust can cause false detection.

2. **Power-cycle completely:** Unplug the Fire TV Cube from both power and TV for at least 30 seconds. This is a deeper reset than just navigating “Reboot” from the menu.

3. **Software update:** Once powered back on, go to Settings → My Fire TV → About → Check for Updates. Ensure it’s fully updated.

4. **Reset network settings**: In Settings → Network, look for an option to forget or reset networks, then attempt Wi-Fi setup again.

5. **Factory reset (last resort):** If all else fails, try a factory reset in Settings → My Fire TV → Reset to Factory Defaults. This will wipe everything but can fix stubborn hardware detection issues.

If the problem persists even after these steps, the Ethernet jack or its detection circuitry might be defective, and you may need to contact Amazon support or request a replacement.
