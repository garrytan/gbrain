## User

Does a turmeric latte have an espresso shot in it?

---

## Assistant

Mr. White, **a traditional turmeric latte does *not* have an espresso shot.** It’s usually made from milk (or plant milk), turmeric, ginger, cinnamon, and sometimes honey—similar to “golden milk.”

**If it has espresso, it’s a variation** often called:
- **Dirty turmeric latte**
- **Turmeric latte with a shot**

So: **standard = no espresso**, but cafés sometimes add one on request.
