## Assistant

Mr. White — here’s how to maximize ab definition short-term (preshow) and long-term (conditioning):

---

### **Preshow / Temporary Enhancements**
1. **Lighting & Angle**
   - Overhead lighting emphasizes shadows and lines.  
   - Slight torso twist or crunch accentuates separation.

2. **Topical Application**
   - **Aquaphor** gives shine but can look greasy under bright light.  
     Better option: use **light oil + bronzer** (e.g., coconut oil or competition tan oil) for a dry, tight sheen that highlights definition.

3. **Pump Routine (5–10 min before)**
   - Pushups, crunches, vacuum holds, and short bouts of band work (curls, presses) to increase vascularity and fill muscles.
   - Avoid over-fatiguing abs—just enough to bring blood flow.

4. **Carb and Sodium Manipulation**
   - **Day before:** modest carb increase with normal sodium → glycogen fills muscles.  
   - **Morning of:** keep carbs moderate, sip water but don’t over-hydrate. Slight dehydration makes skin tighter.

---

### **Long-Term Conditioning**
1. **Low Body Fat (Menno / RP Strength)**
   - Ab visibility comes mainly from fat loss.  
   - Maintain ~8–10% body fat for strong definition without fatigue or hormone issues.

2. **Ab Hypertrophy**
   - Train abs 2–3×/week like any other muscle (weighted cable crunches, hanging leg raises, ab rollouts).  
   - Emphasize **resistance**, not just high-rep bodyweight work.

3. **Posing Practice**
   - Learn abdominal bracing and **vacuum technique** (Dr. Isratel’s “abdominal control”).  
   - You can make abs pop harder just by contracting correctly.
