## Assistant

To set up an effective Facebook ad campaign aimed at females aged 13-25 in the 90069 zip code to follow an Instagram profile, follow these steps:

1. **Campaign Objective**: Choose "Traffic" as your campaign objective, since you want to direct users to an Instagram profile.

2. **Ad Set Audience**:
   - **Location**: Enter the 90069 zip code to target.
   - **Age Range**: Set the age range to 13-25.
   - **Gender**: Select "Female" to target your specific audience.

3. **Placements**: Optimize your placements by selecting "Manual Placements" and choose Instagram-specific placements such as Instagram Feed, Instagram Stories, and Explore, which are popular among the target age group.

4. **Budget and Schedule**: Set a daily or lifetime budget based on your total ad spend availability. Schedule your ads to run continuously or within specific times when your target audience is likely to be most active on Instagram.

5. **Creative**:
   - **Images/Videos**: Use visually appealing images or videos that resonate with the interests of young females. The content should be engaging and relevant.
   - **Copy**: Write concise and attractive ad text that includes a clear call-to-action (CTA) like "Follow us on Instagram for more!" Ensure the message is relatable to the age group.
   - **CTA Button**: Use a clear call-to-action button like "Learn More" which can link directly to the Instagram profile.

6. **Tracking and Optimization**:
   - Set up the Facebook pixel to track interactions with your ads and follow visitors to the Instagram profile if possible.
   - Use A/B testing for different ad elements (images, copy, CTA) to see what resonates best with the audience and adjust your ads based on performance.

7. **Legal and Compliance**: Ensure your campaign complies with Facebook’s advertising policies, especially regarding targeting minors. Ensure that any content is appropriate for the 13+ age group.

8. **Review and Launch**: Review all settings and creatives to ensure they align with your campaign goals and then launch your campaign.

By closely monitoring the performance of your ads and making necessary adjustments, you can effectively drive the desired audience to the Instagram profile.
