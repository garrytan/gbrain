Mr. White, you're probably thinking of **Lipton Recipe Secrets Onion Soup & Dip Mix**. It's a go-to for adding flavor to chicken and rice — savory, salty, a little sweet from the onions. Just mix one packet into your cooked rice and chicken (or even while cooking), and it’ll punch up the flavor instantly.

Other solid options:

- **Knorr Chicken Bouillon** powder or cubes

Quick, cheap, and hits like comfort food.

I just wanna have some chicken and rice with something yummy in it like bbq sauce

---

## Assistant

Got it, Mr. White. Here are a few no-fuss, microwave-friendly flavor boosters for chicken and rice:

### Sweet & Bold:
- **BBQ sauce** (like Sweet Baby Ray’s or Trader Joe’s Carolina Gold)  
- **Teriyaki sauce** + sesame seeds  
- **Buffalo sauce** + a little ranch or blue cheese  

### Creamy & Savory:
- **Cream of chicken or mushroom soup** (mix in while microwaving)  
- **Kewpie mayo** + soy sauce + sriracha (Japanese-style umami bomb)  
- **Trader Joe’s jalapeño sauce** or **Cilantro dressing**  

### Tangy & Bright:
- **Pico de gallo** + lime juice  
- **Salsa** + shredded cheese  
- **Greek yogurt** + garlic powder + lemon + dill (tzatziki vibe)

Minimal effort, max flavor. Want macros to match one of these?
