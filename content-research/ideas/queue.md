# Content Idea Queue

## Overview

This is the master content idea queue. Ideas are ranked by **composite score** (higher = higher priority). The score is calculated from search volume, commercial intent, brand fit, and competition level.

See `../SCHEMA.md` for the scoring formula and field definitions.

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `queued` | Idea is approved and waiting for production |
| `researching` | Research agent is gathering supporting data |
| `drafting` | Content is being written |
| `review` | Draft is in editorial review |
| `scheduled` | Content is scheduled for publication |
| `published` | Content is live |
| `killed` | Idea was deprioritized or abandoned |
