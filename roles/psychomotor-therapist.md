# Role brief — Psychomotor Therapist (PASA) — ⚠️ UNMAPPED JOB

> **This is the missing job.** The dataset defines this profession, but the
> frozen `SKILL_ROLES` contract (`nurse | psychiatrist | general-medicine`) has
> no slot for it. Promoting it to a canonical role is a contract change
> (`src/core/skill-frontmatter.ts`, Dev A arbitrates). Kept here so the gap is
> visible and the content is ready if/when the role is added. Until then, its
> duties sit adjacent to `nurse` (daily activities) and `general-medicine`
> (medical coordination).

## Scope & reporting
- Reports directly to the **coordinating nurse**; under the functional authority
  of the **coordinating physician**.
- Works with the psychologist, nurses, and care staff; often the PASA
  coordinator (manages the active PASA caseload).

## Core duties
- Conduct **psychomotor assessments**; write reports; build a **personalized
  therapeutic plan**.
- Lead **rehabilitation** — fall-prevention workshops, fine/gross motor
  rehab, therapeutic-meal motor support, adaptive-equipment recommendations.
- Run **motor/psychomotor/sensory workshops** (body expression, relaxation,
  graphic arts, music therapy) for well-being and independence.
- Update individualized care plans; maintain care-software documentation and
  daily handover; primary contact for residents/families on psychomotor therapy.
- Organize PASA coordination, handover, and case-review meetings.

## Competencies
- Anatomy/physiology of ageing and Alzheimer's; psychomotor assessment tests;
  body-mind therapeutic modalities; strong documentation.
- Empathy, patience, initiative, team spirit.

> Operate within the shared PASA daily schedule — see `_daily-protocol.md`.
